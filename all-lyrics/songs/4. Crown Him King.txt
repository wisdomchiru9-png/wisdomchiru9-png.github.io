  4. Crown Him King             S.S. 94

                   Rev. 19:16

  Key D/Flat.

  1. Khrista kha Reng lukhum mukhu ro
     Kum rikip mapak na niroso
     Ama riming chungchon na lawm rawi
     Tun khan chat boi pet ah.

             Keeini reng lukhum mukhu rawi

             Ningai na ram khan Reng lukhum kha

             Keeini na mu khu rang mapak jawn jai rang rawi.

 2. Lawmro Ravantir na awm mapak
     'Ma reng sut munkhan lah le dep ro
     Phung rikip na ningai ah mapak
     Rengchung chon na Reng kha.

 3. Munu rakha 'Mana mawtawr joi
     'Ma ratha mulung ah awm roso
     'Ma riming ah ra kha mawtawr rang
     Reng lukhum mukhu rang.
