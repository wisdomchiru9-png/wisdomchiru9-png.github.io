172. Jesus Love Even Me            S.S. 38.

                  1. Jn. 4:8.
Key G/Flat.

1. Dawng ah kiningai chung ah napa awm
   "Ma numpok chawng reetheeng keeideeng neereei
"Ma chawng mirit Bible ah kumuk joi
Jisu numpok mirit nakha hinih.
Kiningai Jisuna numnopok
Keei num nopok (2)
Kiningai Jisuna num nopok
Kecihi num nopok det.

2. 'Amakha keemeeneei ah kachai awm
Kachai na munse khan num nopok joi
'Ma numpok nakha kawhawng mining le
'Ma numpok khut khana keeihi najap.

3. Rengpa maiso kha kumuk pha roi ah
Lah khat tak ah awm kawthaw ngam ala
Hektik neeiboi kala kha nirang nih
Sindawngroi ni Jisu numpok nahi.

4. 'Mana num nopok jarkhan num kopok
Numpok khana karatha nassan pe
Cross ah jongthi num inopok nahi
Reetheeng tak ah Jisu numpok kejet.

5. Jisu numpok nahi nawrawdawn le
Ningai ah keeina masang rang kani
Patheen Ratha Reetheeng na keei le awm
Net tak ah dingrang Jisu numpok hin

6. Chawng rakham nahin saw kumuchum joi
Jisu tak 'kawsawn ah phortha kaman
Seeitan kha keedeeng ah put khan tan joi
Jisu numpok nahi koro phong le.
