423. The Christmas Day

1. Christmas nikho hawngsok joi
Reng Khrista jongsok nikhan
Von mipui haina mapak
Nasaanpa Reng Khrista kha.
Lurtak ah mapak urawi
Christmas nikho alur hi
Von mipui hai na awm mapak
Von na nikho alur hi
Lutak ah mapak urawi
Nasaanpa kumsawt ah
Raavan pek rang jongjoi.

2. Chungram Ravan awm avar
Saklam miring haina jui
Ke-ngam roihai zan na thoi
Bethlehem teeng se ujoi.

3. Chung rasang ah Jubilee
Keeini rang jongsok nijoi
Seetna kholok kha mak ah
Rengthar chubai bawk urawi.
