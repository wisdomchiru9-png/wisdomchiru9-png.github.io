62. The King's Business          3rd Alex. 126.

                   2.Cor.5:20.
 Key E/Flat.

 1. Keei khomijin khat ah raam sik ah awm kanih
     Ki-inn tu a-la nih, Rangkachak var na mun
     Rengpa chawng chawi kanih, Tuipui vara teengkhan
     Reeipar ang ah chonthaw kanih.

            Hichawnghi keeina kavak chawi

            Ravantir na lah ah thaw uh

            Nangni ree-ngeei uro

            Ti-ah Pumina reei,

            Patheen le hawng ree-ngei uro.

2. Manrawp Ringkho napepa chawng reei jong kanih
    Rachak nepek rang nih, munu awm lai nawnih
    Hichawng hi keereei ret, Patheen na reeiro ti
    Rengpa rang ah chonthaw kanih.

3. Rengpa chawngpek khanih, reeleei mirikip na
    Munu lim ah put ah, Pumile awmsom rang
    Hichawng juihai khana,
    Kumsawt ringna mukrang
    Rengpa rang ah chonthaw kanih.

4. Numpok kinepek hi, jawtboi ah awm noro
   Nangmuk ngam rang ah tu, 'Ma mai ah numpok kha
   Ningai kha awm rang nih, keeideeng 'Mana neereei
   Mahi kerengpa chon khanih.

5. Ki-inn kha vardet, Sharon Phaijaw var nek ah
   Hektik neeiboi ringkho,ningaina kha awm joi
   Kupumina neereei, Ringjawn jai ah awm rang
   Mahi kerengpa chon kha nih.
