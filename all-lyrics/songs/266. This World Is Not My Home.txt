266. This World Is Not My Home              M.B.C. 252.

1. Manmeeseh hi pha bawngte kaw awm na inn nih
   Kaw-awm narang ah muntu ala tak ah awm
   Ravantirna keei Ravan mawkawt ah na-ngak
   Khan manmeeseh hin asawt awm jawt lai mi-ing.

         Pumi nangjet nang ang kawlawm awm mak

         Ravan ram khan ki-inn awm nawk ala Pumi

         Ravantir na keei Ravan mawkawt ah na-ngak

          Khan manmeeseh hin asawt awm jawt lai mi-ing.

2. Ravantirna na-ngak ah awm keeina kejet
   Nakhekpa nasan ro tun keei nangdeeng kahawng
   Adawk kinit ah awm 'Mana noloi rang nih
   Khan mammeeseh hin asawt awm jawt lai mi-ing.

3. Ravanram munhoi khan kunumpok kunu awm
   Rumuk pui ngam nawning tak ah kawsawn malim
   Ravan ram mawkawt ah Amana keei na-ngak
   Khan manmeeseh hin asawt awm jawt lai mi-ing.

4. Ravanram khan keeini kumsawt ah awm som rang
   Tak ah sawn hai lim na mawtawr na he mosok
   mapak na lah kha reeleei Ravan rehem joi
   Khan manmeeseh hin asawt awm jawt lai mi-ing.
