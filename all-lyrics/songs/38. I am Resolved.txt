38. I am Resolved (C.S.I. 147)
Key B/Flat

1. Manmeeseh hoina le ningai nahi
Keei ra-ngai lai nawning
Minek ah adik det le atha det
Thaw narang kading joi.

Jisu nangdeeng kahawng joi
Idawr hoi rang mo?
Chungchon na rasang Jisu
Keei Kahawng joi.

2. Hektik neeiboi ravan ram lut narang
Seetna thaw lai naw ning
Mina keei isawn boi ah nawthaw awm
I-awm sawn lai nawning.

3. Anakhekpa Jisu deeng se narang
Munu reetheeng sojoi
Adik Amani, athang boi Pumi
Aring jawn jai kha nih.

4. Nakhekpa Jisu anu jui rang rawng
Adik chang narang khan
Ama chawngpek kha dungjui ah awm ma,
Ama chon thaw rang rawng.
