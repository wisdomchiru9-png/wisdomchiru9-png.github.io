485. I Remember Calvary

                  LK. 23:33

Key E/Flat.

1.  'Ma lamkai khan keei aserang
    Ama te ringrep rang kanih
    Keeirangah rusaw kimining
    Ama Calvary ah ruthat uh.

            Jisuna sun zan na noroi

            Jisuna lam kaichak rang nih

             Ama adik kawlawm tak nih

             Calvary kha kimining le.

 2. 'Ma chawngpek khan keei kiningai
     'Ma numpok khutna nomoroi
     'Ma reetheeng na keei rang ah nih
     Calvary ah thikha rulong.

  3. Chit le mulung rakha boi ah
      Khrista kha ningai ah dep ro
      Nikhat 'Ma le rumu ngir rang
      Jisu Calvary ah kawlawm nih.
