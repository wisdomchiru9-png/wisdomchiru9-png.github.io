 79. I Know Whom I Have Believed                 S.S.857.

                                                 R.S. 148.

                II. Tim. 1:12.

Key E/Flat

1. Kijethoi Patheen numpok na
    Keeideng nemejet joi
    Rutuk boi keei 'Ma numpok na
    'Mata nama chang joi.

           Tak ah ki sawn pa kha kejet joi

            Kiru rehe chon thaboi kholok awm

           Ra-ngam pui Pumi Amana

           Nikhat rophong rang nijoi.

2. Kijet boi inakhek nakha
    Tak ah kawsawn rep joi
    Ama chawng ra-ngam pui haikha
    Kumulung ah awm joi.

3. Kijet boi ratha ranak na
    Miring munu mawtawr
    Jisu chawng khana malar joi
    'Ma tak ah kawsawn joi.

4. Kijet boi atha athaboi,
    Keeideeng rutung a-awm
    Sawna lam rangkachak nikho
    'Madeeng kumuk sojoi.

5. Kijetboi Pumi jong narang
    Zanna nisun nit'awm
    'Male lawnchak rang kinit'awm
    'Madeeng kumuk het joi.
