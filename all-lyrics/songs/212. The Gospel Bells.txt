  212. The Gospel Bells           3rd Alex. 162. S.S. 356.

                    Jan. 3:16.

  Key F.

  1. Atha chawng daar mini joi
      Mole tui chung rikip ah
      Ringkho mo rakan na chawng hoi
223
Nangle keeirang nepek joi
"patheen numpok jarra, Sapa khat te kha nepek,
Ama tak ah sawn hai khana, kumsawt ringna muk rang nih.
Chawnghoi daar miri joi
Mole tui chung rikip ah
Chawnghoi daar mirijoi
Atha chawng nang le keeirang.

2. Patheen chawng khana nokoi
Vanoi bumichin nakhan
Isawnboi ah thaw lai noro
Inokoi na manrawp nih
"Ringna bulawm kanih, ratha vawncham misip ro,
Nang munu khadawr rawp ah awm, Vur ang ah mechek rang
nih".

3. Daar khana nemejet joi
Ningthing kip miri sok joi
Phor tumrang khan na-ngak chak lai
Tik te nit'awm sum naw nih
"Rekhe ro ringkho rang, nangni rasai lai noro,
Nangnuk teeng vak en lai noro, Asawna nawmawtawr nih".

4. Ningai sipjoi daar miri
Kaw le kaw ah rehem joi
Rupum ping ra-ngaidam kha awm
Nasaanpa jongthi khana
"Ajeng chawng hoi ningai, mirikip rang kojong chawi,
Nasaanpa sok joi nang nirang, Pumi Reng Khrista kha nih".
S.S. 617.
