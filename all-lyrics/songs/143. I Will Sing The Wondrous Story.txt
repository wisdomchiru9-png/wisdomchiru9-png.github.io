143. I Will Sing The Wondrous Story                  S.S.875.

                  Ps. 139:1.

Key E/Flat

1. Relek risim lah ah thaw rang
   Khrista keei jarra jongthi
   'Ma inn mirit jong mathan ah
   Calvary Cross ah jongthi.

           Relek risim liha ah thaw rang

           Khrista keei jarra jongthi

           'Ma lurna lah kha thaw tai ah

           Tuipuikha ruchun som rang.

2. Ke-ngam ang ah kuruthang joi
   Jisuna nojong tawk ret
   Ratha rat na 'Ma khut kha le
   Ringna lamkhan nawchawi joi.

3. Mithaboi khat ah kuru awm
   Jisuna nojong tawk ret
   Chitna rang ah mun rikip ah
   'Ma ratna kha nepek joi.

4. Azingnakhana numukhu joi
   Seetna karra kuru awm
   Pumina num nopok retah
   Seetna i-neeilai mi-ing.
