52. 'Tis The Saviour Let Him In
Key A/Flat.
Rev. 3:20
S.S. 446
.

1. Nangmulung ah lut narang Pumina atawk
Jonglut pha roi ah nangna rumikir rang maw?
Neereetheeng masak rang nih
Nangkha nasaan chak rang nih.

Nakhekpa kha nang mulung mawkawt ah ding
Mawlawng petana mulut ro
Kir mala hin mulut rang ro.

2. 'Ma niboi ah munu mawkawt mawlawng ngam mak.
 Kumsawt ah Rengchang puirang tun awm natawk lai
 Numulung azingkha namavar pe rang nijoi.

3. Nang mulung jawt nawk ah awm tunse na-ngak lai
 Kum khatuk nuruthang awm tun nojong tawk lai
 Nangle keei niringnarang Jisuna nojong thit peh.
