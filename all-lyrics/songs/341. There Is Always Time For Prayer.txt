341. There is Always Time For Prayer
R.S. 836.
Key C/Flat.

1. Nikho thar khale pok rik jang rang nih
Nangsawna doi ngam boi khab
Tawk ro numpok nakha pha awm lai hin
Niik narang ah pha kha awm.
Zingkar hoi khan awm nik narang pha awm
Nisun var khan awm pha awm
Pha rikip ah rukhuk sun na niro
Niik narang ah pha kha awm.

2. Nikho thar kha le mulung mathar ro
Ra-ngat na pha awm lai hin
Chon thaw ah saw hai kha le roroi ah
Patheen deeng khan nichak ro.

3. Ringkho lam ah ke nang ruthui pha khan
Makha nangdoi ngam nawk awm
Nisun var se khan saw na maw nang neei
Patheen deeng khan nichak ro.

4. Nilut le avaar jongcheem pha roi khan
Ruri ring jer nawrawng maw?
Mulung thar le nikho kha mathan ro
Niik naranga pha kha awm.
