273. Over Jordon            R.S. 392.
Key E/Flat.

1. Numpok le mirit tak ah
   Pumina nawchawi rang nih
   Mole phaijaw ahoi mun Jordon vara
   Nawsaw pha saw muchum rang
   Tui reetheeng longna munkhan
    Keeini nuru jawng rang nih Jordon vara-ah.

           Jordon kawmchang vara teeng khan

           Sawna muchum rang nanih

            Tui reetheeng long na munkhan

            Jordon vara kawmchang teeng khan

            Ningai ah rumusom rang Jordon vara-ah.

2. Rampa rubuk raw karra, Nakhekpa nawchawi uro
     Kharamse sok rang nani, Jordon vara-ah
     Jan na relek meeichaw le, Nisun na reemeei chaw le
     Lam nakai perang nih Jordon vara-ah.

 3. Arat angar 'Makhut le, keeini nomo roi rang hin
     Atha ahoi ram munkhan, Jordon vara-ah
     Theeira phung kip bir ah mawng
     Tuiring put, aring tui long
     Chamle ra awm lai nawni, Jordon vara khan.

 4. Chawng rakham na ramhoi se, Pumina nawchawi rang nih
     Canaan ram numuk malim Jordon vara-ah
     Dawng nikhan 'Male awm rang, Nawchawiro nomoroi roh
      Resen na awm lai naw ni, Jordon vara khan.
