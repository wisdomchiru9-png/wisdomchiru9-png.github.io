377. For Thy Mercy            S.S.1050.

                  Ps. 23.6.

Key F.

1. Nangnumpok mirit jarkhan
   Kum kawhawng metheng ujoi
   Lah le kanamapak uh
   Nakhekpa nejet pero.

2. Nawsawpha nawdawk phakhan
   Naw-awm na lungpum khani
    Lam awmboi ramthim munkhan
   Aring lam nachang pero.

3. Tumo thit nalam rawrawng
    ahawng rang ah kumthar hin
    Pumi nang sun sawraw le
    Keeinihi nama ngar ro.

4. Tak' sawn rep ah natet ro
    Nangta namachang het ro
    Sawna munkhan nasaan ro
    Reng lukhum tum narang khan.
