331. Hold The Forth              S.S. 669.

                  Rev. 2:25.

Key D.

1. Kawlawm haiseng Ravan na
   Zetna hi en uro
   Nara changpui numuk joi
   Mawtawr narang nai joi.

           Mukui rep ro kajong joi ti-ah

           Jisuna reei

           Masangro Ravan teeng

           Keeinina mawtawr ngir rang.

2. Seeitan i-neei ranaak kholok
    Kha sawngjoi muro
   Ni-ra-ngampui ranak neei
    Miring kholok pur joi.

3. Ningaina ponlap melek joi
    Sumlawi ra-ngairo
    'Ma riming ah mawtawr rang.
    Maraha pui haikha.

4. Chiroi raa rawthaw kha sawt 'awm
    nasaanpa naaijoi
    Raa lamkaipa kha jongjoi
    Kawlawm hai nigai rawi.
