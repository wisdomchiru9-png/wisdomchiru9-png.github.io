50. The Prodigal Son.
Lk. 15:11.
Key G/Flat.

1. Thoitang ah khan kapa deeng serang kani joi
Kamaton reemeei rujing awm
Manar rang teei neeilai mi-ing reeleei sung hin
Kapa niboi ara rok keeng.

Munu ah kamathang awm
Kathat na mawk naw nih
Kumulung kimikir le
Oh, num nopok rang nih.

2. Kapa Inn na sum le neei kip birra awm kha
Mi mulung saw kha menem ah
Keeihi vawncham tuira doi ah kithit rang maw?
Sara ang ah karavak hin.

3. Oh, kapa deeng kaw-awm laikhan ohoi hai le
Reeleei hoina kholok katawk
Kimining ret le kapa inn kara ngai joi
Chawngdai hawngkho rang kanijoi.

4. Keei mithaboi chawng rangai boi Patheen eh-se,
Keeirang munkha awm ni mawta!
Oh, reeleei ah seetna reeipar apar haihi
Par sole tu achoi matat.
