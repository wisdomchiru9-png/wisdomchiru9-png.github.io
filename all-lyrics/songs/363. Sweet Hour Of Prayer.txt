363. Sweet Hour Of Prayer
Ps. 4:17.
Key D/Hat

1. Patheen deeng niik na pha hoi hin
Reeleei mulung mining awm mak
Kapa Rengsut na mun hoi khan
Raavat rikip kha nojong pek
Sawna renro reeveei napha
"Ma riminga ningai kumuk
Lemna aput kerekhe jawn jai
Kha phakhan niik na pha hoi nih.

2. Patheen deeng niik na pha hoi hin
Kiniik nakha nawjawt peh
'Dik le tak ah sawn neei hai deeng
Ratha raavan pek rang na-ngak
Pumi riming ah kawhawng pek
Ama chawng numpok tak ah sawn
Sawna kholok 'Madeeng kepek
Niik na pha hoi keeneei pha khan.

3. Patheen deeng niik na pha hoi hin
Ning nitna chawng hoi kepek joi
Lawn rasang khan tin kata ah
Inn hoi sok narang kimining
Reeleei tak sa kholok mak ah
Hek tik neeiboi law kha muk rang
Ravanram lamkhan ra-ai rang
Reeleei keresen pui pha khan.
