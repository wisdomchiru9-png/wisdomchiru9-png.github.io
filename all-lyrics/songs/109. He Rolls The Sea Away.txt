109. He Rolles The Sea Away             S.S. 880.

               Exodus 24:21.

Key F

1. Tolai khan Israel naihai na
   Sok ah ru-awm a-awm
   Patheen ratna kha mumuk ah
   Tuipui kha sen pejoi.

           Tuipui kha sen pejoi (2)

           Jisudeeng tu naraha pui chirang awm mak

           Tuipui kha sen pejoi.

2. Munu na karatha anawk
    Lemna na nimichit
    Pumina ra-ngaidam nepek
    Tuipui kha sen pejoi.

3. Chitna khana lam khar ah awm
    Jisudeeng ra-nga rawng
    'Ma numpok na deeng khan keeihi
    Natet chak rang nijoi.

4. Jisu ruseso pha sung khan
    Sawna kawrawtawng pui
    Keedeeng kung ah nang awm khana
    Kalam nang nemezet.
