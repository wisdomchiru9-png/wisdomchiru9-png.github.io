329. Jesus Leads His Army              S.S.689.

                Joshua 5:15.

Key C

1. Ra-ngairo nokoilai tumo ase rang?
   Kochon thawrang atawk jawtboi tumo awm?
   Tumo ponlap raak ah raa maton sawn rang?
   Naraha pui awmpha, Patheen rang dingro.

          Jisuna Ponlap thoi ah raa lam kai joi

           Adik khana naraha pui mawtawr rang.

2. Patheen raanak beeiro mawtawr mala seng
   Seeitan raa mawtawr narang raapho sawngro
   Reetheeng le adik Pumi patheen jarkhan
   patheen raa nawng rangkhan pumkhat ah awm rawi.

3. Ra-ngairo sumlawi miri raakha nawngro
   Raa lamkai Rengpa rimingkha lur roso
   Ramla ram nai kholok reetheeng chon rang hin
    Raa he ra-ngai tana raa maton sawngro.
