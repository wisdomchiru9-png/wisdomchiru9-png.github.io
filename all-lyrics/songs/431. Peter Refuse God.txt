431. Peter Refuse God

1. Nupumi Jisu thit deeng
'Ma khaichawi haina, anu juichak u
Pumi thit pha hawngsok joi
Peter awm mulung lek joi.
Ra-ngaidam nepe rang ro
Mulungsaw adik boi munu khana
Atha boi ijawt boi chon
Ra-ngaidam nepe rang ro.

2. Jisuna Peter deeng reei
Rapha lai noro
Nangpha hawngsok joi
Voithum nang musun phakhan
Aarkha hawngkhong rang nijoi.

3. Nupumi thit narang khan
Theempuleen haina
Chawng mocho sojoi
Peter mulung hawngdawng joi
Pumi kha en-na chap joi.
