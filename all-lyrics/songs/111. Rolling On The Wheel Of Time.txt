111. Rolling On The Wheel of Time               MBC. 61.

                  CSL. 254.

Key G.

1. Pha dungjui ah se-ah awm rang
    Nikho dawdawn na sejoi
    Manmeeseh chonhi mathan na
    Ahoi ki-inn khan hawng rang.

           Phadungjui........Phase dungjui ah awm rang (2)

           Ahawngrang... ahawngrang ki-inn hoikhan.

           Pha dungjui...Phase dungjui ah awm rang.

2. Tuikhang leen tuikha kara kan
    Mole phaikha karakan
   Mulung ah sawna ningai na
   Munu le chap na karkhan.

3. Ningthipkip Pumi mapak rawi
   Adik Khan nawchawi narang
   Lam dik naboi khan se mini
   Azingkhap mumu lam khan.

4. Ringkho kethawtna khatuk kha
   Khat khat ah rakan veek ah
   Ringkho tan reeseet mawlawr ah
   Karatha inn na hawngrang.
