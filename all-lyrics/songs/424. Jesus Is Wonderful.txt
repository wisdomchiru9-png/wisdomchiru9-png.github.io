424. Jesus Is Wonderful
Chungchondet riming Jisu nih
Athaw ngam veek Patheen nih
Riming Jisu nih, Rachaak Reng Sapa nih
Reng ah Rengpa Nupumi kha nih.

1. Ke-ngam roi haina lawnchung ah
Nisok nateeng mitheei
Haina rachaak nai kha vak en
'Manina ivak muk ukha Jisu nih
... Chungchondet Jisu nih.

2. Mun rikip ah 'Ma riming ah
Ana, athi miring
'Madeeng 'hawnglim ningai muk joi
Jisuna pok rikip relek masaak joi
.... chungchon det Jisu nih.

3. 'Ma riming vonse rotho mak
Ringna le dam nakha
'Ma riming ah numuk ngam veek
Adik tak ringna le lam kha Amani
Chungchon det Jisu nih.
