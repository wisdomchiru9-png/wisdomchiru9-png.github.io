388. God Will Take Care Of You S.S. 517.
Ps. 71:6.
Key D/Flat.

1. Patheen na nurung rang nih chi noro
Zing le var ah nangkha noroi rang nih
Renem tak ah 'Mana nurung rang nih
Nangse na munkhan namathan nawnih.
Patheen na nurung rang ni dawng sekhan
Nang nasaanpa le nang lawm dit kha nih
Nangkoipha Mana namasang rang nih
Tak ah sawn rep ro, noroi chak rang nih.

2. Patheen na nisun sawt noroi rang nih
Nang kachawina lam nemejet rang nih
Pumi narung, naroi, lamkai khanih
Tuihoi nateeng khan nawchawi rang nijoi.

3. Nang ring lai lim' Mana noroi rang nih
Tu-na awm pek ngam boi phor nepek joi
Pha hawnghek sekhan awm nasaan rang nih
Rengmun na nawchawirang nih dawng nikhan.
