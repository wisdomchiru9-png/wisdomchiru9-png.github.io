94. He Will Safely Hide Me               S.S. 535.

                   Ps. 17:5.

Key E/Flat.

1. Ama numpok sung khan keeihi nethe rang nih
    Manmeeseh sawna rana put khan nasan
    Ama numpok rilip khan keei saw kumuchum
    Nakhek pana keei nasan rang nih.

             'Mana keeihi nethe rang nih

             Sawna le lemna na nikhit ngam naw ni

             'Mana keeihi nethe rang nih

             'Ma numpok sung khan keeihi nethe rang nih.

 2. Ama numpok sung khan keeihi nethe rang nih
     'Ma lurna avar kha numu muk rang nih
     Ama avar kha le keeihi najo rang nih
     Mulung ningai na kumuk rang nih.

  3. Ama numpok sung khan keeihi nethe rang nih
      Ama numpok mirit kha hek lai naw nih
      Nakhekpa kha le rawtawp chak rang kanijoi
      Chungteeng ah ningai na mun hoi khan.
