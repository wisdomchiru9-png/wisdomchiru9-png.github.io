1. How Great Thou Art    C.S.L. 13
Key E/Flat.

1. Pumi Patheen idawr sindawng roi moni !
   Nangkhut kon kholok kemeje pha khan
    Arshi kumuk, relek kho miri kejet
    Nang ranaak le nangjeng nang lurna nih.

            Karatha na nangkha lah le na-awi

            Chungchon na leen Pumi nang nih
            } 2

2. Mangpui, thingpui, rubuk hi sindawng awm naw!
    Zu le va hai lah hoi thaw kha kejet
     Lawn rasang ah put khan kojong en pha khan
     Hawr ah tuilong ruhu khana nesem.

 3. Patheen na, Sapa kha manar boi pet ah
     Kuruhut ah thirang nang jong tir joi
     Kopok rik Cross chung ah ningai ah ruphung
     Nithit pe ah kumunu chawi sojoi.

 4. Khongle lawi le Khrista na jongchum pha khan
     Ningai ah Inn teeng keei nawchawi rang nih
     Keeina chiboi ah 'Madeeng chubai thaw rang
     Chungchon na leen Pumi nangni tirang.
