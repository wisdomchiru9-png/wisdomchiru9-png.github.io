385. My Mother's Prayer              3rd Alex.116.

                 Prov.6:20

Key D/Flat

1. Nikho kha meeneeingam naw ning
   Kunu numpok chawngreei kejet
   Tun' Mana neresen puijoi
   Patheen deeng iniik kimining.

          Kunu kimining pha rikip

          Tunse keedeeng kung ah awm ang

          Kunu hekha kejet jawnjai

          Patheen deeng iniik kimining.

2. Kunu chawng meeneei ngam nawning
   Kumulung ning ma-ngai jawn jai
   Kachai na mun patheen na jet
   Patheen deeng iniik kimining.

3. Kum khatuk kha ruse joi awm
   'Ma numpok chawng kimining chak
   'Ma rusut nakha kumuk lai
   Kunumpok kunu kimining.

4. Apha kha meeneei ngam nawning
   Anakhekpa ranaak jarra
   Kumunu kholok napai pe
   Kunu iniik na mun kha nih.

          Nasaanpa pumi mapak ro

          Ravan na keeini rumu rang

          Ravan Innkhan a-awm som rang

          Kunu iniik kha masang joi.
