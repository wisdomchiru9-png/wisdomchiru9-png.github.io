 404. The Joyful Theme               C.C. 34.

 1. Anasaanpa Nupumi kha shok joi
   Ningaina daarkha miri joi
    Rodit hai n' awm ningai na lah thaw u
    Nasaapa khrista jongsok joi.

           Ningai ah mapak na lah thaw ro

           Amana keeini nasaanrang jongsok

           Mulung reeirit tak ah lawm ro

           Mapak na lah kha thaw ro

          Ningai na daarkha sut rangro

          Nerengpa kha keeinirang jongsok joi

          Mapak urawi ningai na lah khale

           Rasaang nigai chungchon nih.

2. Ra-ngairo ravaantir na ijongreei
   Bethlehem raam ah chawng risim
   Sikawr saak-saak na reetheeng det munkhan
   Nasaanpa khrista kha sok joi.

3. Ra-ngairo ravaantirna lah thaw uh
   Manmeeseh rachaak narang khan
   Relenro sikawr saak-saakna munkhan
   Nasaanpa khrista kha sokjoi.

4. Erelek chawng risiim kha reeirang ro
   Munrikip ah, orong-rong ah
   Anasaanpa Nupumi kha sok joi
   Ningaina daarkha mirii joi.
