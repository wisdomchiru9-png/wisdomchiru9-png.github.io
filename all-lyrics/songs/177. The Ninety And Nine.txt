177. The Ninety And Nine              S.S.97.

                                                            Sindawng khomelek he karkhan

Key Flat           Lk. 15:6.                              5 Lawn rasang chungkhan rawkal tungkha khe-ah

                                                             Ravaan makwawt kungkhan he nang mawsok joi

1. Makhan ke-ngam sawmkoleeiko haiseng kha                  Ningal ro Ke-ngam kha kumuk joi
   Inn sung ah hotak ah awm uh                              Ravantir hai he Rengmun le rehem joi
   Kha karkhan khat tu ram latak ah chaijoi                  Ra-ngairo Pumina vakchawi ret joi.. (2)
   Rangkachak kawipui hoi ah put khan
   Sarang khadawr karra ram rubuk nakhan
   Ke-ngam roipa ah put khan rolajoi (2)

2. Pumi, Sawmkoleeiko hailim tu hin awm
   Hiseng hi nang rang hun mak maw?
    Makhan Ke-ngam roi pakhana rumasang
    Keeideeng a put achal, abi kha
    Lamkha  idawr cho ah rumatat ah awm
    Athang Ke-ngam tawk se-ngir rang kanih. (2)

3. Rekhe masak narang tutena jet mak
    Rakan rang tul idawr thuk ah
    Zanpha idawr zing 'awm Puri na se-ah
    Athang Ke-ngam khat kha vak muk joi
    Ram rubuk na-ah a-saw Ke-ngam hekha zet
    Amana athi rangkhan rasamjol.. (2)

4. Pumi Jisu thisen sok sawna khana
    Lung karra lam norokon peh
    Athang hairang thisen kha rusok jarkhan
    Athang ah awm Ke-ngam kha muk joi
    Pumi nangkhut le nang ke apawp haikha
    Rampa riling haina inusun nih (2)
