180. The Child Of A King
James 2:5.
Key F.

1. Kapa tu inn le loi ah rawchawng nih
Reeleei neei kholok hi ' Ma khutchunga awm
Rangkachak, Dangka, Lungmaan rawp kholok
Reei ngamboi neei kholok' Ma Inn na sip.
Rengpa naikhaa kanih
Reng naikha kanih
Nakhekpa Jisu le
Rengpa naikha kanih.

2. Apa Sapa pumtak na nojong khek
Sara det kha ang ah reeleei ah jongleng
Tuntu kumsawt ah rasang Reng nijoi
Ravan ram ah Inn khat nepek rang nih.

3. Te theeiboi khomijin na kuru awm
Munu neei miring khat ah kojong sok awm
Tuntu noloi-ah kiriming jawjoi.
Reng Sapa changna lukhum kumuk joi.

4. Ki-inn hoiboi jarra lungdawng naw ning
Keeirang ah rasam sa Reng khopui khat awm
Inn naput nawnawt awm lah hoi thaw rang
Reng naikha kani patheen lur roso.
