100. Numberless As The Sand Of The Sea

                                            S.S. 448.

                Hoseah. 1:10.

Key A/Flar.

1. Jordon kawmchang ah nuruchun pha khan
    Kheksa haikha ningai ah muk rang
    Te-ngamboi tui khangleen nge kha ang ah
    Idawr hoi ah en-awm rang moni?

           Te-ngamboi tuikhang leen nge kha ang ah (2)

           Oh, ituk hoi rang mo kheksa hai numuk pha khan

           Te-ngamboi a tui khangleen nge kha ang ah.

2. Rong rong ah kheksa hai meh numuk pha
    Saw renro reetheeina hek pha khan
    Rureeisa raam ahoi kha nutum le
    Idawr ah hoi en awm rang moni?jo

3. Tui vara hoi ah nuru muk phakhan
    Ringna thingkhung rilip ahoi khan
    Rureeisa raam ahoi kha nutum le
    Idawr ah hoi en-awm rang moni?

4. Nakhekpa khale nuruchun phakhan
   Malur na le leen na kha muk rang
   Niring narang ah chungkhopui chawng hi
   Idawr hoi ah en-awm rang moni?
