  225. One is Our Master             S.S.500

                    Matt. 23:8.

 Key A/Flat.

 1. Numnapo Nakhek pa Nupumi khat nih
     Keeini 'Madeeng kunga nuruchun ngam
     Oh, 'Ma numpok nakha vaar jawn jai roso
     Meeichong kha dawk naboi pet rang semro.

            Jisu deeng rutuk sokpui kha nirawi

             Lam nakai pepa deeng atha thawrawi

             Mulung repe ah reeijui kha nirawi

             'ma lurna Hosana niti malim.

 2. Ningaile chon nithaw perang ah Pumi
     Khat teni'Ma idit kha thaw urawi
     Raaman boihai rang rutuk thep ranaak neei
    .. Nemna neeitak ah rotok narang nih.

3. Chungchon det Mulung leendet Nupumi kha
    Khat te, 'Ma nun renem tu rotho mak
    "Ma ponlap kha richit rajak pe urawi
    Hek tikneei boi 'Ma riming niroso.

4. Pumi khatte sok haikha raavantha ni
    'Ma jong naret rang rurasam rang ro
    Ama lur naput khan meh relek rang nih
    'Ma inn Rengmun na sawlaw awm rang nih.
