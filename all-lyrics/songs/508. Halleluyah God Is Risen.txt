 508. Halleluyah God Is Risen.

 1. Halleluyah athikha mawtawr joi
     Athina pumi kha mawtawr ngam mak
     Jisuna athikha mawlawr het ah
     'Von pumi Jisuna hawng ringjoi.
Halleluyah...Halleluyah
Jisu thaankhur aput hawngthoi joi
Halleluyah...Halleluyah
Mawtawr zetna patheen na hawngring joi.

2. Easter zingkarra Marry thaankhur teeng
Lawm hai le Jisu a-en ruse uh
Makhan Jisu thaan na ru awm lai mak
Jisuna hawngring ah ruse joi.

3. Ravaantir haina' Manideeng ru rei
Nupumi kha hin tawk ulail noro
Amakha mahi munhin awm lai mak
Athikha mawtawr ah hawng ring joi.

4. Halleluyah athikha mawtawr joi
Iru reeikha dungjui ah hawng ring joi
Hawngring joi, Nupumi hawngring het joi
Halleluyah, athi mawtawr joi.
