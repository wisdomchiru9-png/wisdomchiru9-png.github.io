96. Oh. Where Are The Reapers                R.S. 639.
Key G.

1. Par rawkhawm haikha khose teeng mo awm?
    Munu kar ah awm atha sephe kha
    Nang kawite kha le vak aat so rang ro
    Mawtawr malalim saw muchum thamak.

            Par rawkhawm rang khose teeng mo awm?

            Ahoi ah aat hin tumo hawngkhawp jawt awm?

            Munu karra sephe tha hai kha

            Rawkhawm rang tumo asan rang awm?

2. Lam rikip khan se tana vak tawk ro
    Rampa buk khan ara cho awm rang ni
    Ram rikip khan tawk ro mohot boi ah
    Rawkhawm ro Ravan ram lut na pui rang.

3. Moloi phailoi kholok kha min so joi
Reeleei ah par rawkhawm rang na-ngak joi
Rawkhawm rang mi tawm hai loila leendet
Nasum hai tampui ruthang rang nijoi.

4. Mikholok kawite rak tana va ro
Rangkachak sangvui kha rawkhawm rang khan
Thawro Pumina jong rawkhawm malim
Ningai tak ah vak rawkhawm som urawi.
