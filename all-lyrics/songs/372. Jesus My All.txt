372. Jesus My All          S.S.478.
Ps. 9:1.

1. Pumi mirit sut na khan kabu lai
   Pumi kene-ngen nara ngai pero
   Nangchon kha put rang rawng neereetheeng masak ro
   Keeseet na rikip nurusuk peroh.

2. Kisir na mik rithi le kawhawng bu
    Tak ah sawn na neeiboi keei nasaan ro.
    Hidawr renro keereeveei awm, kara ngam nah
    Jisu na keeirang hin nithit pe joi.

3. Ra-ngai ro numpok na chawng nepek joi
    Pumi nangta kani, kata nangni
    Rakhana thang sojoi, mulung michim koi joi
    Pumi nangta kani.

 4. Pumi mirit sut na khan kabulai
    Pumi nangchawng rakham na kene ngen
    Tak ah sawn matha hile nangdeeng hawng vung rawng
    Jisuna keeirang hin nithit pe joi.
