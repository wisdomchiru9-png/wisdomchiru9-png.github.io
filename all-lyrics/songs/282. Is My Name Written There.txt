282. Is My Name Written There
D.H. 184. M.B.C. 15.
Lk. 10:20.
Key A/Flat.

1. Rangkachak Dangka neeinak keeina manar nawning
Ravan na riming buk khan kiriming awm narang
Kumunu hi nang thisen le nuru suk pero
Jisu keeideeng neereei ro kiriming maw awm joi.
Reetheeng riming buk khan
Kiriming meezeek maw?
Ravan na riming buk khan
Kiriming maw mezeek

2. Tuipui nge le rajat ah kumunu ah rawp hi
Nangthisen along khana kumunu rusuk joi
Khajarkhan riming buk khan kiriming mezeek joi
Keeseet na kholok awm nuru suk pe rang nijoi.

3. Avar jawn jai nang awmna khopui ahoi sung khan
Erelek pon reeseei ah Ravan tir hai awm uh
Khamun khan atha boi chonthaw lim mulut naw nih
Kha-ang Ravan ram khan kiriming maw meezeek joi.
