401. Hark The Herald Angel's Sing                  S.S.30.

                   Lk.2:14.

Key G.

1. Ra-ngairo ravaantir lah,
    Jongsok Rengkha lur roso
    Reeleei ah rachaak renem
    Munuphung miring hai rang
    Phung rikip ningai rangro
    Ningai kha jaw cheen rangro
    Ravaantir na rophong joi
    Khrista sok Bethlehem ah
    Jongsok Rengkha lur roso
    Ravantir na lah thaw uh.

     Ama keeini le awm joi
     Lawmrawi jongjir khrista kha
     Ravantir na lah thaw uh
     Jongsok Rengkha lur roso
     Nupumi Immanual.

 3. Ravan rachak Reng Sapa

      Mda luma renem khale
     Jogsok thiret lai nawnih
