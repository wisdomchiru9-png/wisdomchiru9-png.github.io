(441. Enthroned Is Jesus Now
Rev. 7:9.
Key E/Flat.

1. Tun Jisu Reng chang joi
'Ma sut na Ravan na
Renglukhum lu-ah rukhu joi
Mireetheeng hai awm joi.
Makhan ning-ai lurna
Nakhekpa deengkung khan
Makhan keeinina ningheen vengrang
Dawdawn na...dawdawn na
Keeinina ningheen na kha mukrang.

2. Avaar peng khan ding uh
Miring tampui karkhan
Mawtawr' ti zetna vanoi na
Lah kha thaw som ujoi.

3. Ke-ngamte mapak uh
Manirang jongthi ah
Ke-ngam thisen le akhek joi
Lukhum rukhu veek joi.

4. Oh, reetheeng nang numpok
Misaan na nang nepek
Keeini awm Ravan na ruchun
Mawtawr nakhan awm rang.

                           R.S. 211.
