93. Murmuring Stream                 S.S. 701.

                  Prov. 20:11.

Key G.

1. Lawn rasang ah put tui hawr khat jong long
    Chat boi pet ah jonglong jawn jai
    Nivar peng khan miri hoi kha mosok
    Chat boi ah long jawn jai.

            A long tui hawr miri khana

            Tui khang leen se sok joi

            A long tuihawr miri sok kha

            Reetheeng lah thaw kha-nih.

2. Nisaat pha sawbo rachak masak
    Ningai tak ah munui lam ah
    Ju le va reeipar hai deeng nek rang pek
    Ningai tak ah nek uh.

3. Tui hawr long kha jong leen dedet joi
    Tui miri kha sok dedet joi
    Ningai na nikip ah phor kha chawiro
    Tui phon miri sok joi.

4. Naite ang ah avar tui hawr khana
    Ningai na lah kha thaw jawn jai
    Mulung reetheeng numpok rasan nakha
    Lah ah thaw som urawi.
