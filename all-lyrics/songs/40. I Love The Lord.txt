40. I Love The Lord (C.E. 72)
Key F

1. Keeina Pumi numpok chak rang
'Ma riming phung rikip na mapak rawi
Thi reetheeng na keeihi nakhek
Nama tharra kining nama ngai peh

Keeina Pumi.............Kumulung ah
Num kopok ningthingkip 'Makha katawk'
Mana keeihi nare rip joi
Keeina Ama lamdik khan lawn chak rang

2. Reeirang kani mikholok deeng
Idawr mo keeina Pumi num kopok
Karatha nirili masaak
Pumi rang ah kha, tak ah kawsawn joi

3. Miring haina namak a-awm
Pumikha num kipok na chawng khana
Karatha hi athar nijoi
'Mana keeihi naidit nama chang joi.

4. Keeina 'Madeeng mulung kepek
'Male rawtawp ah lawn chak rang kani
Karatha hi ningai khana
Nubuk chip ah keeihi namavar joi.
