488. Break Thou The Bread Of Life 3rd Alex. 145.
Lk. 24:35.
Key E/Flat.

1. Ringna bulawm khoiro keeirang pumi
Tuipui kawmchang ah nang ikhoi angkhan
Reetheeng munkhan pumi nang kanatawk
"Aring chawng" nangdeeng karatha awm joi.

2. Ringna bulawm kha nangni kupumi
Nangchawng dik khana keeihi nang na khek
Chungteeng ah ringna saak kha nepe ro
Numpok adik nangni nuruchu ro.

3. Tun pumi nang ratha nojong pero
Kimik hina avar numumuk ro
Nangchawng retheng adik keeina murawng
Nangchawng rophongkhan pumi kunumuk.

4. Keeirang adik pumi reetheeng nangni
Galilee ah bulawm phorpek angkhan
Sawna reeveei kholok bangrang nijoi
Rachak kha keeina mukrang kanijoi.
