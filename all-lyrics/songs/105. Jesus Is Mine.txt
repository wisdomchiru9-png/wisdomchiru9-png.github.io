 105. Jesus Is Mine          S.S. 1045.

                Solomon. 2:16.

Key E/Flat.

1. Reeleei ningai nahi thang so a-awm
    Ra-ngampui awm nawk awm Jisu keeneei
    Mun rikip zing sojoi, sawmuchum na awm mak
    Keeirang raavan nape Jisu keeneei.

2. Lemnakhan cho nawning Jisu keeneei
    Phasawt awm lai nawning Jisu keeneei
   Athang theei neeinak lim ralan khat rang ah ni
    Mulung ah dit nawning Jisu keeneei.

3. Thangrang ro zan ramang, Jisu keeneei
   Khovar kha thang 'awm, Jisu kata nih
   Mulung sawna rikip kapai ngam pha roi khan
   Jisu ningai nih, Jisu katani.

4. Athangtheei rikip kha seso rang ro
   Athangboi rang limkha kunu rujawng
   Numpok le phortha kha, nasaanpa deeng kung ah
   Pumi kurujawng, Jisu kata nih.
