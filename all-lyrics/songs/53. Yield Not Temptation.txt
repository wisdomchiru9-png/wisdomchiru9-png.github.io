53. Yield Not Temptation
S.S.698.
Rev. 2:7.
Key B/Flat.

1. Lem nakhan jawt noro, ajawt munu keeng
Nasaan ngir rang nijoi, 'sen hai saan narang '
Pakhat chang malar ro, azing mavar ro
Jisu te enchak ro, nang mawtawr rang nih.

Nakhekpa deengkhan reeiro
Mulung ngamna nepek rang nih
Mawtawrna ngir rang khan
Narachang pui rang nih.

2. Thaboikha pairang ro, ranai pui noro
Patheen riming lur kha chit rajak pero
Adik le mulung net mulung ah poro
Jisu kha en chak ro nang mawtawr rang nih.

3. Amadeeng ah hawngkha lukhum pek rang nih
Natak ah sawn khana nawmawtawr rang nih
Nasaanpa na ranak namathar pejoi
Jisu te enchak ro, nang mawtawr rang nih.
