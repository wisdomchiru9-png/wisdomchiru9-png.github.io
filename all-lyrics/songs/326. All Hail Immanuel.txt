326. All Hail Immanuel             3rd. Alex. 217.

                 Rev. 5:12.

Key B/Flat.

1. Mapak urawi Immanuel
   Nangdeengkhan kawhawng bu-uh
   Nang iditkha jui veek urawng
  He rikip na lawm roso
   Nakhekpa Reng mapak nakha
   Ravan khopui miri roso
  Vanoina mapak urawi, Immanuel.

          Oh, Immanuel, Immanuel

          Oh, lawm urawi, Immanuel

          Singna raatna nangta niroso

          Tun awm kumsawt awm

          Oh, Immanuel, Immanuel (2)

          Reng ah Reng rikip Rengpa

          Immanuel Rengpa.

2. Mapak urawi, Immanuel
   Kheksa haina naheei uh
   Reeleei rengna raadon aa-seet
   Reng lukhum mukhu rang
   Kheksa hai awm ruse so-ah
   Rengsut mun deengkhan ruchun uh
   Kumsawt ningai lah kha thaw uh
   Vanoina mapak urawi, Immanuel.

3. Mapak urawi, Immanuel
   Naraha puihai taan sojoi
   Thantik neeiboi athaw ngam det
   Athi, munu meeikhur thang joi
   Seeitan raatna awm koi sojoi
   Kumsawt lurna nang riming nih
   Vanoina mapak urawi, Immanuel.
