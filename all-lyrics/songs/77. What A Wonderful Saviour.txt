 77. Faith Is Victory          S.S. 682.

                   1.Jn. 5:4.
 Key F.

 1. Avar awm na lawn na awmhai, Khristen rami thoiro
     Ra thaw tana zansawt nawngro, azing rokoi rang ni
     Naraha pui hai kha doiro, ranak mosok tana
     Tak ah sawn hi ra mawtawr ni, Khan reeleei mawtawr rang.

             Tak ah sawn na mawtawr (2)

             Oh, ningai ah chungsok na

            Reeleei mawtawr rang nih.

 2. Ma ponlap hi numpok khani, chungchon 'Ma chemjam ni
     Mireetheeng hai lawn na nalawn, ningaina hekha le
     Tak ah sawn na pusip thoi ang, Ram rikip ajap veek
     Tak ah sawn na thitna mawtawr, makha avar pho nih.

3. Naraha puihai nodoisom, chiroikha ru-ngui joi
    Reeleei pon inn kha marang ro, Maton na inn hoi awm
    Ringna lukhum rukhu-veek rang, adik kawngve thaw rang
    Makhan reeleei rawnawk rang nih, heso rehem rang nih.

4. Raa kha Ama tena mawtawr, ponchek nepek rang nih
    Ravantir mala 'Mana jet, 'Ma riming malaar joi
   Khan avar lawn rasang ah put, mulung numpok chongjoi
   Munu Pumi maimawk mukjoi, Jisu riming jarkhan.
