160. How Sweet The Name of Jesus S.S. 112
Phil. 2:2.
Key E/Flat.

1. Tak ah sawn hairang Jisu riming idawr hoi moni?
Sawna sebe kha namadam, Chitna kha awm nawt ngam.

2. Apawp ratha kha madam ngam, sawna renem masak
Ratha vawncham nimisip pe, sawmuchum na nang nih.

3. Inn mothoina lungpum khani, rethena pho nang nih
Asip jawnjai neeikawk kha nih, numpok rutung nanih.

4. Jisu Ke-ngamroi Nakhekpa, naracha Theempu Reng
Kupumi kiringkho kineei, kawhawngchawi ruloi ro.

5. Nang numpok rophong rang kani, Phuk kasan rikip pha
Nang riming rawchawi bak hina, ratha miring roso.
