44. Faith of Our Fathers (M.H. 172. CSL. 74.)
Key G.

1. Napa uleenchang tak ah sawn
Mirawp, mipui chemjam chimak
Patheen tak ah sawn na neei uh
Nit' awm pha bawngte ruhoi uh.
Napa uleenchang tak ah sawn
Nithit malim tak ah sawn rawi.

2. Napa uleenchang tak ah sawn
Ratha ningai kha rumuk joi
Keeini awm amani ang ah
Tak ah sawn ah thi-ngam rawng maw?

3. Napa uleenchang tak ah sawn
Rahana, ree-ngeei naboi khan
Numpok hi nuru mezet joi
Nupumi riming mapak rawi.
