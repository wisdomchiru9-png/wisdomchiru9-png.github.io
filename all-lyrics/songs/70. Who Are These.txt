70. Who Are These           S.S. 1003.
Key E/Flat.

1. Chungteeng ah Rangkachak daar le
   Lah thaw khi tuhai moni?
   Num napo Jehovah ti-ah,
   Lah thaw khi ra-ngai uro.

           Munu pon rusuk hai keeng

           Mechek so joi, ke-ngam thisen kha le
           } 2

2. Kumsawt ringna ahoi mun ah
   Awm haihi tuhai moni?
   Reeleei sawna apai so ah
   Ravan na saw muchum joi.

3. Amani pon ledeer haikha
   Enro vur ang ah chek joi
   Ke-ngam thina rawchawk so ah
   Ringna lukhum rukhu joi.

4. Patheen ke-ngam na lamkai pe
   Sun jan na chonkha thaw pe
   Ravan saak khale vaai jawn jai
   Mik rithi cho awm lai mak.

5. Patheen Khrista Amadeeng khan
   Ahek boi ringna man joi
   Sawna rana awmboi pet ah
   Rengmun na lah kha thaw uh.
