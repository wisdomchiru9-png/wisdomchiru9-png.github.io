426. Oh, Come All Ye Faithful               S.S. 31.

                 Lk. 2:15.

Key A.

1. Ha ngro tak ah sawn hai
   Ningai tak ah tun hin
   Bethlehem teeng serawi chubai bawkrang khan
   Enroh, raanring rokong ah za Rengnai kha.

           Hawngro chubai bawk u rawi (2)

           Khrista Reng Jisukha Chubai bauk rawi.

 2. Ravantir hai na awm
    Ningai na lah thaw uh
    Keeini awm Nupumi kha mapak u rawi
    Chungchon na rasang, Nupumi niroso.

 3. Raavan kenepek   u
     Nakhekrang nang jongsok
     Nang riming kha chubai nabawk chak u-rawng
     Patheen chawng tak kha taksa-ah jongjir joi.
