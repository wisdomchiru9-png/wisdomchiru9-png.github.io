507. Christ Is Risen             S.S. 156.

                 Matt. 28:6.

Key C.

1. Khrista ringjoi...Halleluyah
    Ringkho avaar phor thakha
    Enro, thaan khan ru awm lai mak
    Athi kha Mana mawtawr.

            Jisu ringjoi.. Halleluyah

            Ningai manmeesch sip joi

            Thaankhan Jisu ru awm lai mak

            Lungmukhu kha rulet joi.

2. Jisu ringjoi...Halleluyah
     Jisu lawmhai ningai ro
     Azing maaihup pon naput khan
     Patheen sapa malang joi.

 3. Khrista ring joi....Halleluyah
     Ireei dungjui khan ring joi
     Tun 'Ma alur Rengkha nijoi
     Ningai pumi kha nijoi.
