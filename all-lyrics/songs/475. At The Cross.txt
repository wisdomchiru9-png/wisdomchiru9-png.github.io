475. At The Cross              S.S. 883.

                 II. Tim. 1:12.

Key E/Flat.

1. Jisu hi kata machang rang
    Keei zak na neei mi-ing
    'Ma chawng richit rajak pek rang
    Ningaina 'Ma Cross kha.

            Cross kung kha (2)

            Avaar kha kumuk joi

             Kumulung ah pokrik kha seso joi

             Tak 'kawsawn jarra, avaar kumuk joi

             Tuntu ningthingkip kining ngai joi.

 2. Jisu Patheen riming kezet
     'Ma riming kabeei rep
     Karatha zak na neeiboi ah
     Ra-ngam na thang naw nih.

 3. Chawng rakhamna, Rengsut na mun
     Net tak ah awm sojoi
     Ama khut ah kerepe joi
     Chawng rakham sok malim.

 4. Tun keei 'Mata kanijoi maw?
     Patheen maikung sekhan
     Athar Jerusalem sungkhan
     Karatha mun awm joi.
