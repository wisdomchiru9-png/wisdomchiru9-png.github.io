45. Does Jesus Care (M.H. 278.)
Key D/Flat.

1. Kasaw hai ah kapur ving sekhan
Jisu nejet mak che maw?
Kumunu arik na nede rep ah
Kapur hi numu mak che maw?

Sindawng awm naw! lawmdit Jisu
'Mana ijet boi awm mak
Azingkhap janchim rip khan awm
Zingkar nisun awm, nomoroichak.

2. Karatha hi mikchaw rep ang ah
Mukui chak ah kalawn na
'Rachangpui lawmdit awmboi pha sekhan
Jisu keei numu mak che maw?

3. Nalem khana naheeichip pha khan
Tan narang khan zetboi ah
Mulung ah ree-eek ah kachap pha khan
Jisu num nopo makche maw?
