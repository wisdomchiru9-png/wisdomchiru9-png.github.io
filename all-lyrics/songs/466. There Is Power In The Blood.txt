466. There Is Power In The Blood               S.S. 145.

                 1. Jn. 1:7.
Key B/Flat

1. Munu pok arik kha nang pai joi maw?
   Munu lik ngam thisen ranaak khan
   Munu ranaak kha nang mawtawr joi maw?
   Relek thisen ranaak jar khan.

          Ranak kha awm.. relek ranaak kha

          Ke-ngam thisen long khan

          Ranak kha awm.. relek ranaak kha

          Amaan rawp ke-ngam thisen nih.

2. Nang leen rasaat nakha nang pai joi maw?
   Relek ranaak neei thisen jarkhan
   Nang reetheeng narang Cross teeng khan hawngro
   Relek thisen ranaak jarkhan.

3. Vur nek ah chek det ah nang awm joi maw?
   Adik thisen mirit sok jar khan
   Munu akhe rikip sok sujoi maw?
   Relek thisen ranaak jarkhan.

4. Nang rengpa Jisu chonkha nangthaw maw?
   Relek ranaak neei thisen jarkhan
   Mapak na lah kha nangthaw jawn jai maw?
   Relek thisen ranaak jarkhan.
