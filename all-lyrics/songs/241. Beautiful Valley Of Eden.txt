241. Beautiful Valley Of Eden               S.S. 908.

                   Heb. 4:9.

Key E/Flat.

1. Ahoi phaijaw Eden ram kha
    Nisa renem rang nih
    Mulung dawng ah saw hai kha awm
    Saw muchum na muk rang.

            Ahoi phaijaw ram Eden kha

            Inn reetheeng mirit khan

            Tui kha reher jawn jai a-awm

           Saw muchumna kha kumuk

2. Mulung nat na doi hai chung ah
    Rangkachak ni jongvar
    Ravantir hai lah thaw jong chawi
    Rehem joi ram tin nah.

3. Makhan Nasaanpa inn khat awm
    Kheksa naihai awm na
    Chung rasang ram alur tak khan
    Lah thar jong rehem joi.
