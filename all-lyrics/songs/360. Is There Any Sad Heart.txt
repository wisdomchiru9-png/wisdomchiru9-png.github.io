360. Is There Any Sad Heart             R.S.244.
Key G/Flat.

1. Mulung sawna kha pok rik ah phung tumo awm?
   Tumo nang awm? (2)
   Ratha ah sarachang nakhekpa num apo,
   Hawngro lam neereei urang kanih.

          Pumina nangkha nasaan rang nih

          Relen na hawngro

          Jisu anu jui rang ratha dawk tumo awm

          Hawngro lam neereei urang kanih.

2. Ringna ranga tuikha a-nejawt tumo awm?
   Tumo nang awm?(2)
   Aput ringna tuikha a-ra-ngai tumo awm?
   Hawngro lam neereei urang kani.

3. Jisukha atum jawt a-ngak tumo nang awm?
    Tumo nang awm?(2)
    Tuntak hin tak ah kawsawn ati tumo awm?
    Hawngro lam neereei urang kanih.
