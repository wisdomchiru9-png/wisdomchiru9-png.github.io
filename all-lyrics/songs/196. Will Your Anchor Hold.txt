196. Will Your Anchor Hold              S.S. 879. M.

                 Heb.6:19.

Key F

1. Ringkho rithi khopui nah na- nor awm
   Nangratha thirchuk kha runet ni maw?

                                                   burk

   Tuipui rawnawng khana navak nawng le
   Nang thirchuk khana nemenet ni maw?

          Ratha thirchuk kha keeini neeneei

          Rithi khopui rat na-ngam narang

           Lungpum anet tak khan thungrep ro

           Nakhekpa minumpok na reeirit kha.

2. Nang thirchuk khana nasaan ngam ni maw?
   Nakhoi ranga khana navak ep joi
   Rithi khopui tuina nawnawng pha ah
   Tuipuina rokong melet ni maw?

3. Thitna tuipui longkha hawngrang phakhan
    Dawngkhit ah nangphuk san kha hawngdawk le
    Rithi khopui reher kha hawng rat awm
    Ringna thirchuk na nemenet nimaw ?

4. Tungmun Rangkachak khopui avar kha
    Zingkho hawngvar lekhan en ngam rawng maw?
    Ringkho rithipui reher bang so le
    Kawmchang ah Ravan ram kha sok rawng maw?
