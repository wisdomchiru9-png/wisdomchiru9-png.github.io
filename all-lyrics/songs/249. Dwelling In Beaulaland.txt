  249. Dwelling In Beaulaland               3rd Alex. 385.

                    Isaiah. 62:4

 Key B/Flat.

 1. Ram latak ah roso rakhana hekha kuru jet
     Makhan reeleei miring munu sip joi itikejet.
     Keeirang nem naboi chit le rakhana khana nokoi
     Beulah ram ah put hin rephet nawning.

             Keei ka-awm joi lawn chung khan

             Reemeei awm naboi thoikhan

             Keei kenek tui aput kha

             Makha char lai nawni

             Ahek iti awm boi pet "manna"

             Khale keeihi navai joi

            Beaulah ram munkhan keei ka-awm joi.

2. Rakhana ruhu thahboi khana manmeeseh achawk
    Patheen miringrawthaw pui rang rahapui hai dingsom.
    Patheen chawng khana nasaan ah innpui sungkhan
    Tui na nawhawng si ngam lai nawni ka-awm.

3. Rithi khopui rang khana numupur ngam lai naw nih
    Patheen khutna nuhup chak ah keei kerethe joi.
    Nivar na na-eh chak ah ningheen naboi awm lai mak
    Beaulah ram khan hoitak ah ka-awm joi.

4. Patheen chonkha ke-en rep le kiring narang rok ni
   Ama he hoi kezet le lam nokonpe kumuk
   Ratha ah ring ah awm hina ring khomo karakan
   Ningai tak ah awm rang Beaulah ram ah.
