242. Coming Home            3rd Alex. 396.

                 Lk. 15:18.

Key B/Flat.

1. Munu phung jak naroi kinit 'awm
   Taw ah sawn rep ah nangdeeng kahawng
   Neereetheeng masak ngam nang deeng awm
   Innteeng kahawng joi.

           Kahawng joi... kahawngjoi

           Seet na lam teeng khan seret lai naw ning

           Keei kahawng joi... kahawng joi

           Pumi Jisu deeng kahawng joi.

2. Apana naithang itawk ang ah
    Pumi na keei hi nangna tawk chak
    Nasan rang awboi munu phung keei
    Inn teeng kahawng joi.

 3. Here nem le nang nokoi kejet
    I jarra mo jawt boi ah awm ning?
    Chit na dik naboi nang napai peh
    Inn teeng kahawng joi.

 4. Man rawp Jisu thisen long khana
    Nurusuk vur ang ah nemechek
     Kara ngam na Pumi nangte nih
     Inn teeng kahawng joi.

 5. Kunu deeng kithaw chon kha reei roh
     Patheen na nudeeng khan reeipe joi
     Reng lukhum rukhu malim serang
     Inn teeng kahawng joi.
