297. The Better Land         S.S.981.
Heb. 11:16.

1. Kimik na ramhoi khat kumuk
   Sawmuchum na ramhoi munkhan
   Kha mun na awm lim var sojoi
   Sawna rikip awm lai nawnih.

          Numpok na ram ningai avar

          Azing reeleei kha mavar joi

          Athim kawmchang muk rang nanih

          Niringkho hi hawng hek sole.

2. Chungteeng ah ningai kawmchang khin
   Ningnit na saw muchum na awm
   Rumu lim resen lai nawni
   Ni-awi hai lim kha muk veek rang.

3. Reeleei hoina le ra-ang mak
   Khoijing khovar kha awm lai mak
   Kha munkhan ningai ra-ngai mak
   Nem na boi ah pha hek awm mak.

4. Lungsaw chap na awm lai nawni
   Rachak ahoi ravan ram khan
   Ringna lam tawk ah chai hai awm
   Sawna ngak na Inn muk rang nih.
