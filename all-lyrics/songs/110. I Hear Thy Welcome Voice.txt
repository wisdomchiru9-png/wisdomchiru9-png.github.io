 110. I Hear Thy Welcome Voice              S.S. 475.

                 Matt. 11:28.

 Key E/Flat.

 1. Nang nokoi he kejet
    Nangdeeng kung ah rang khan
    Calvary ah thisen long khan
    Neereetheeng masak ro.

            Pumi keci kahawng

            Tun nangdeeng kahawng

            Thisen le nurusuk ro

            Calvary ah longkhan.

2. Kawdawk nale kahawng
    Karanak kha nangnih
    Keereetheeng malalim sekhan
    Nang neereetheeng  masak.

3. Jisuna keei nokoi
    Tak ah sawn nadet rang
    Numpok le rachak neei narang
    Chungle noi rikip ah.

4. Jisuna menet joi
    'Ma chonthaw na sungkhan
    Numpok mirit kha nepek joi
    Seet rengchang na munhin.

5. Chawngkungchangkha nepek
   Athaw ngam hai deengkhan
   'Ma chawng rakhamna sok sojoi
   Tak ah sawn na jarra.

6. Lawmro, damna thi kha
    Lawmro, nasaan numpok
    Khrista Pumi khutchawi khanih
    Ranak le adik nih.
