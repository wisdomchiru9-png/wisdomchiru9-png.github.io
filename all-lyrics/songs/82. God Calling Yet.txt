82. God Calling Yet           C.S.L. 205.
Key Flat.

1. Nupumina nangkha nokoi
    Munu chon rok hi dit rang maw?
    Pha le kum kha-awm se sojoi
    Nang ratha la aja rang maw?

           Pumina nokoi

           Nangkha nokoi

            Tun awm nokoi lai

            Munu phung hai nang jet maw?

2. Nangla anu jui nawrawng maw?
    Ama he ra-ngai jawt mak che maw?
    Mulung renem tak ah nokoi
    Pumina tun awm nokoi lai.

3. Mulung renem ah nokoi lai
    Munu neei mulung mawsaw rang maw?
    Numpok hena nokoi reret
    Nang ratha nang mawsaw reret.

 4. Ajawt boi iti awm noro
     Nang mulung kha mathar jawt rang ro
     Manmeeseh chon hi mathan na
    'Ma chawng mulung ah lut roso.
