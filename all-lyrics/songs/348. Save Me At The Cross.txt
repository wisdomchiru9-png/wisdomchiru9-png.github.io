348. Save Me At The Cross R.S. 287.
Key A/Flat.

1. Numpok ah keei nakhek pa
    Kiniik noloi pero
    Nangdeeng kung ah kawhawng bu
   Cross khan keei na khek ro
   Munu kuru neei jarra
   Nangna nang nithit pe
   Numpok sung khan nethero
   Cross khan keei nakhek roh.

           Pumi Jisu keeihi

           Numpok ah noloi roh

           Nawmawsaw lai nawning

           Cross khan keei na khek roh.

2. Munu khan kuruthang awm
   Nangdeeng niik reret rang
    Ringna ranga lam nang nih
   Cross khan keei nakhek ro
    Nang nepek ningai numpok
   Manboi ah nang nepek
    Pumi keeite nang na re
    Cross khan keei nakhek roh.

3. Nang thisen le nurusuk
Nang neereetheeng masak
Thi khan keei naw maw awm ro
Cross khan keei nakhek ro
Tak ah kisawn te hina
Ra-ngai dam kumuk joi
Tak ah sawn na kana beei
Cross khan keei na khek ro.
