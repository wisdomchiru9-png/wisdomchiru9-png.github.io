120. Abide With Me           S.S. 297

                  Lk.24:29.

Key E/Flat.

1. Zaan khojing pha hin naw awm puichak ro
    Kupumi Jisu namathaan norois
    Tute na keeihi nasaan rang awm mak
    Nasaan ro kupumi naw-awm pui ro.

2. Manmeeseh hoina ningai kholok hi
    Ralan khat te-ah athang rang rok ni
    Kupumi tu athang iti awm mak
    Rotho boi Pumi keei naw-awm pui ro.

3. Rengpa kuchung ah lunghang awm boi ah
    Numpok damna nang matha rilip khan
    Chapna sawna kholok kha pai so-ah
    Munuphung keeihi naw awm pui chak ro.

 4. Pha rikip ah Pumi kana ra-ngai
    Nang numpok ranaak mawtawr na-ngam kha
    Nang makai nakhan net tak ah awm rawng
    Nivaar reemeei jing awm naw awm pui ro.

5. Kupumi raavantha kaman lekhan
    Anaraha pui tumo kichi rang
    Thit na rang le mawtawr na mun se khan
    Kupumina nang nawrawtawp pui chak.

6. Kiringkho dawngse khan nurung chak ro
   Nangvaar khana keeihi nojong vaar ro
    Azing thang ah avaar hawngsok phakhan
   Kithit kiring ah awm naw-awm pui ro.
