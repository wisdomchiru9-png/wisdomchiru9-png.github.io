168. Only Trust Him             S.S. 392.

                 Matt. 11:29.

Key G.

1. Hawngro munu pokrik phunghai
    Pumi numpok nakhan
   Sawmuchum na nepek rang nih
    'Ma chawng tak ah sawn lim.

           Tak ah sawn ro (2)

           Tak ah sawn rep ro

           Nakhek rang nih (2)

           Tak ah sawn hai lim.

2. Nangrang Jisu thisen longjoi
    Phortha kha nang muk joi
    Thisen longkhan rawnawm rang ro
   Vur ang ah chek narang.

3. Jisu hi adik lampui nih
   Saw muchumna nepek
    Pumi kha tak ah sawn rep ro
   Raavan nang muk rang nih.

4. Hawngro mireetheeng hai kar hin
   Ningai tak ah hawngro
    Kumsawt ringna manrang nanih
    Ningai bang lai naw nih.
