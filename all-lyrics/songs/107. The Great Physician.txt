107. The Great Physician             S.S. 89.

                 Matt. 28:20.

Key E/Flat.

1. Theempuleen na nara naipui
   Num napo chungchon Pumi
   Mulungsaw nem narang nokoi
   Jisu hekha ra-ngai ro.

Ahoidet Raavantir lah
Chungchondet ahoi riming
Lah hoi khale mapak rawi
Ahoi Jisu riming.

2. Nangmunu kha napai pejoi
Jisu hekha rangai ro
Ravan lam hoikhan hawng tana
Reng lukhum hawng rukhu ro

3. Ningaina rikip Amata
Jisu tak ah kawsawn joi
Ningaina Jisu Nakhekpa
Numpok awm Jisu riming.

4. Chitna munu napai pejoi
Jisu riming hoi khana
Karatha idawr hoirang mo?
Jisu riming kejet le.

5. Hawngro Jisu riming ahoi
Lah le namapak pui ro
Sokpui hai Jisu rimingkhan
He rasang ah mapak rawi.

6. Vanoi asin aleen kholok
Jisu riming numpo lim
Jisu inokoi ra-ngai ro
Jisu kha le awm som rang.

7. Chungteeng ah avar ram hoikhan
Jisu meh kha numuk le
Numpok ah lah hoikha thawrang
Jisu riming ahoi kha.
