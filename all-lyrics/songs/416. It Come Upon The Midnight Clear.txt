416. It Come Upon The Midnight Clear
H.F. 33.

1. Arapa lai ah rureei sa
Chawngkha vonjan sok joi
Ravantir haina awm lah thaw
Salangdar aat u-ah
Manmeeseh hin rachak reei ah
Miring ningai roso
Manmeeseh hin rachak reei ah
Lah hoikha ra-ngai uh.

2. Ravantir haikha na mohot, iti awmboi pet ah
Asaw iti awmboi pet ah
Ningaina lah thaw uh
Manmeeseh hai asaw doikha
Saw muchum narang khan
Amanina lah kha thaw uh
Lah kha ra-ngai uro.

3. Ravanse-ah mulung saw ah
Asaw reeveei doihai
Ahu sok ah achap haikha
Mulung mawdawng noro
Enro, ningaina lah thaw uh
Ravantir hai na-awm
Ningai ah saw muchum tana
Lah kha ra-ngai rang ro.
