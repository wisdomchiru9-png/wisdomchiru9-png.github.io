174. Come Thou Almighty Kings.s. 6.
Jn. 5:7.
Key

1. Jongro athaw ngam Reng, keeini nasaan uro
Nang riming kha lah le mapak urawng
Ra mawlawr na neei rawng, kereng u jongchum ro
Dawng awm boi ah.

2. Chawng ah 'jir nang jongro
Nangrat nakha neeirawng
Kininiik uh, phortha nepe uro
Nangchawng chungsok roso
Reetheeng ratha keeini, deeng jongchum ro

3. Reetheeng mulung chum na, keeinideeng jong awm ro
Ningai Phahin rat na jong awm roso
Athaw ngam kha nang nih
Mulung sung rikip ah, ratha neei rawng.

4. Thum ruchun Pumikhat, rasang det ah lawm rang
Hektik neeiboi, nangleen nang lur nakha
Ningai nakha muk rang
Chat boi pet ah num napo kha nang nih.
