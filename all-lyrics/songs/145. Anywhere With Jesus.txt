145. Any Where With Jesus               S.S. 627.

                   Ps. 37:5.

Key G/Flat.

1. Jisu le kawrawtawp lai lim rekhe rang
    Reeleei mun rikip ah' Mana lam kaichak
    'Ma-awm naboi atu ningai na awm mak
    Jisu le kaw-awm lai seng keei chi mi-ing.

            Mun rikip ah chitna kha jet lai nawning

            Jisu le kaw-awm lai seng keei rekhe rang.

2. Khose mun khan awm kibing ah ninaw ning
    Role chim haina keeihi namathan awm
    Numpok khut le lamhoi khan mana kai pe
    Jisu le kaw-awm laiseng ningai na awm.

 3. Jisu le kaw-awm laiseng sawmuchum rang
    Azingna nubuk ah nawmawsaw ah awm
     Kirehe nakha zet ah chailai naw ning
     Jisu le ka-awm hi ki-inn hoi khanih.
