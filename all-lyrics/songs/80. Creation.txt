 80. Creation        C.S.L. 9.
 Key A.

1. Nimik na en-na phak ngam boi
    Ala tak Ravan ram kha awm
    A-awm kha zan na saw muchum boipet ah
    Chon thaw chakrangnih.
    Ratha seet neeiboi pet ah,
    Nii var na reeleei kholok hi
    Ningthing kip mavar jawn jai ah
    patheen ranak kha numumuk.

2. Nilut lekhan manmeeseh hi
    Azing khana mukhu so joi
    Manmeeseh hi hawngvar, ahoi,
    Ningai na risim kholok kha
    Neereei pha khan dawdawn na se
   Miring na hihai hi jet ah
   Patheen kha mapak jawn jai joi. (2)

3. Avar kha hawngsok pha roi-ah
   Pha hi dawdawn na se pha khan
   Sawrpa Arshi var khana awmchan, haihi nemejetreret
   Mapak na khut kha melek ah
   Ningai na lah kha thaw jawn jai
   Hihai hi Patheen ikon nih
   'Ma riming hoikha mapak rawi.
