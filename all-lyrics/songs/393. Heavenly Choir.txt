393. Heavenly Choir.

1. Azing jaankhan reeleei hin
    Patheen avaar na jong vaar
    Ravaan lah hoi kha thaw uh
    Manmeeseh rachaak narang.

           Rasaang Rengsapa khana

           Jongsok joi ningai urawi

           Ravaantirna lah thaw uqiators on

           Manmeeseh rachaak narang.

2. 'Vaantirna ponchek reeseei
    Jisu sokna Bethlehem teeng
    Mapak na lah thaw lam ah
    Nikho hoihi alawm uh.

3. Ravaan na lah thaw lai lim
    Ravaan na khrista lurna
    Bethlehem ningai nakha
    Ke-ngam roihoi na rophong.

4. Ningai lah kha ra-ngai ro
    Ke-ngam roihai kha ang ah
    Von Bethlehem lah hoi jongthaw
    Manmeeseh rachaak   narang.
