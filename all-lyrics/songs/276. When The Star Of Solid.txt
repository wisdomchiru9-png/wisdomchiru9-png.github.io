276. When The Star Of Solid            C.E. 132.

1. Chon kholok mawtawr so le
   Mawtawr na lukhum man rang
   Rangkachak ram hoi mun khan
   Sawna awm awmlai nawni
   Resen awm awm lai nawni
   Rangkachak ram nosok le.

          Rangkachak ram hoi mun khan

          Ratha saw muchum na ram

          Sawna awm awm lai nawni

          Resen awm awm lai naw nih

          Rangkachak ram nosok le.

2. Chit na awm-awm lai naw ni
   Mulung saw awm lai nawni
   Rangkachak ram hoi munkhan
   Ningai na sip rang nijoi
   Ni-ningai bang lai naw nih
   Rangkachak ram nosok leh.

3. Ni-inn awm meeneei so rang
   Athar ravan ram mun khan
   Rangkachak ram hoi munkhan
   Hawngruchun som rang nanih
   Ningai le mulung par ah
   Rangkachak ram nosok le.
