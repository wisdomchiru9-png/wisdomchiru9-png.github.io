461. When I Survey The Wondrous Cross
SS.115.
Gal. 6:14.
Key E/Flat.

1. Ningaina Reng Sapa thitna
Sindawngroi Cross kithir phakhan
Kawrawchawng chang na kete le
Leen rasaat pui rang awm lak mak.

2. Namakha ro ka-ngar narang
Khrista thit khana nasaan ro
Taksa hoina leen na kholok
'Mathisen khan kerepe joi.

3. 'Malu, 'Ma-ke, 'Ma khut enro
Numpok sawna renro doi ah
Numpok le sawna riling lu-
Khum irukhu hi ni-ngai maw?

4. Irikip kata nit se awm
'Ma deeng ah pek rang sin hai lai
Relek numpok reetheeng deengkhan
Ratha ringkho neeilim perawng.
