209. My saviour's Love           3rd Alex 4.
Key A/Flat.

1. Nazareth Jisu maikung ah
    Mulung dawng lam ah kading
    Seetneei num inopok hi
     Kimining le sindawng roi.

             Idawr relek numpok moni?

             Lah hi thawchak rang kanih

             Idawr relek numpok moni?

              Relek numpok nepek joi.

   2. Keeirang tu rohon sungkhan
       Idit daan khan niniik peh
       Keeirang mikrithi cho ah
       Ahu thi namasar peh.

   3. Ravantirna rujawng rang nih
       Reeleei avar rang jongjoi
       Asaw haikha maw-awi rangkhan

        Karatha rang saw doi joi.

    4. Munu sawna nawchawi pejoi

        Amata het machang joi

        Calvary ah sawna doi ah

        Theente rang ang ah thijoi.

    5. Kheksa haina ningai nikho khan

         Ama maiso kha muk rang

         Kumsawt ningaina nirang nih

         Numpok na lah ah thawrang.
