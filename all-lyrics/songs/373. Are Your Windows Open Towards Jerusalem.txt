373. Are Your Windows Open Towards
       Jerusalem?          S.S. 167.

                  Dan. 6:10.

1. Raring sur Hebrew na sun le jan nah
    Rukhuk sun na iniik nang muk maw?
   Inn hoi sung khan raam latak Zion kha
   Amana mining jawn jai.

           Nangbang pawp hai kha Jerusalem teengkhan

           Isur sok pa kha-ang ah

           Nang mawlawng maw?

           Lurtak ah ajong rang ah Reng sapa kha

           Ningthing kip nang ru ngak maw?

2. Meeipui inn kang rang khan chi noro
   Keeipui roko khan awm chi noro
   Daniel Patheen khana nasaan rang nih
   Ravantir jongtir rang nih.

3. Aring Patheen naihai raanak loiro,
   Nasaanpa khana lah hoi thaw
   Zion lawn teeng khan nangmai meeheei tana
   Rengpa kha rulawm rangro.
