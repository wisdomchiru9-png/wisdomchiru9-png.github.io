402. A Little Lord Jesus              A.H. 314.

                  Lk. 2:16.

Key D.

1. Seei inn khan awm aa-ja narang ru awm mak
   Naitepa Jisu hup narang pon se awm mak
   Jisu sok na munkha Arshi na jongvaar joi
   Jisu seei saak-saak na rokong ah maja.

2. Seh-kawrna awm naitepa kha vaak manam
   Nit' awm Jisu unu-na theeiheei ah pawm
   Lungboiroi Jisu nojong en pe daak ro
   Keeihi nawpawm tana nangdeeng natet ro.

3. Pumi Jisu keeihi nawrawtawp pui ro
   Keeihi numnopo ro ti-ah kininiik
   Naipang hai deenghin nawrawtawp pui uro
   Pumi nangle rawtawp ah karing jawt uh.
