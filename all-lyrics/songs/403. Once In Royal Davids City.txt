403. Once In Royal Davids City    S.S.32.
LK.2:11.
Key G.

1. David Rengpa khopui sung ah
Seh Inn araam khat ru-awm
Seh saak saak na rokong khat ah
Naitepa kha ama ja
'Rachak 'nu kha Mary nih
Naite pakha Jisu nih.

2. Ravaan naput reeleei ah jong
Vanoi Pumi Patheen nih
Sara inn neeiboi, minoi seh
'Ma zana kha seh inn nih
Nakhekpa na reeleei hin
Keeini le awmchak rang nih.

3. Naite aput sindawngroi nih
'Mana reeijui khat runih
Minoi seh'nu kha numrupok
'Nu khut renem khanru bawk
Khristen naipang kholok na
'Ma ang ah reeijui niro

4. Nanaite chang kha ang ah 'Ma
Nikho-nikho ah hawngleen
Asin, adawk, achaap munui
Keeini le ra-ang re nih
Keeini ang ah sawna zet
Ningai awm nara-ang puij.

5. Nimiik na dawngkhit atu' Ma
'Nakhek na numuk rang nih
Naitepa kha numpok dit awm
Ravaan Nupumi kha nih
Ma naihaikha lam kai peh
'Ma awm narang munse khan.

  6.Sara 'noiseh nilai naw nih
    Seh inn ru awm lai nawnih
    Ravaan ram te-ah Amakha
    Rengmunna numuk rangnii
    'Ma naihaikha Renglukhum
    Mukhu rang ru-ngak rang nih.
