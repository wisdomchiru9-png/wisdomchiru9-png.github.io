231. My Faith Looks Up To Thee                    S.S.2

                    Ps. 25:1.

Key E/Flat.

1. Nakhekpa Calvary
    Ke-ngamte tak ah sawn ah ke-ne-en
    Kinik noloipe ro, munu napai pero
    Von naput nangta namachang het ro.

2. Mirit numpok khana
    Karatha apur nomothoi pero
    Keei jarra nang ruthi
    Tak ah nawsawn rep rawng
    Rotho boi aring jawn jai kha nang nih.

 3. Kiring kho ah ajing
     'Lungsaw na naheei awm, nomoroi ro
     Azing hi mavaar ro, sawna napai pero
     Nangkhale keei resen lak lai nawning.

 4. Kiringkho hek pha khan
     Thitna tuipui khana nawchawi pha khan
     Numpok phor nepe ro, chitna napai pero
     Kheksa ratha haile naw maw awm ro.
