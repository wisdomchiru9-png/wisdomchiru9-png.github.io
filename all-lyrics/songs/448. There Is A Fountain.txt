   448. There Is A Fountain           S.S. 129.

                   Zech. 13:1.

  Key C/Flat.

  1. Thisen nalong tuipui khat awm
     Jisu ratha rui ah
     Thisen khan munuphung rawnawm
     Seetna reetheeng sojoi.

            Seetna reetheeng sojoi (2)

            Thisen khan munu phung rawnawm

            Seetna reetheeng sojoi.

2. Murusapa kha ningai joi
    Thisen longkha muk le
    Keei awm munuphung kha kani
    Nurusuk peveek ro.

3. Tak ah sawn na thilong kumuk
   Nang sebe ah jonglong
   Nakhek na numpok chawngkung hi
   Kithit se awm roso.

4. Mulung leen na lah hoi det kha
   Nasaan nakha thawrang
   Kawchawng ra-aak hehoi boi le
   Thaan na kaja sekhan.
