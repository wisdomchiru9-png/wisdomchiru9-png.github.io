204. For Christ And The Church                S.S. 1096.

                 Ps. 71:16.

Key D.

1. Khrista le Beekrup rang he mosok rawi
   Nerengpa riming richit rajak na perawi
   Narat lai mulung lut tak ah chon thaw rawi
   Chit awm tak ah Rengmun adik rang ding rawi.

                  Toro wa yu

           Khrista nakhekpa kha nih

           Nakhek rang khan jongthi

           'Ma thi'na nawrawchawk ah

           neereetheeng masak joi.

                       mwc-u ensin      ben

2. Khrista Beekrup rangkhan niniik niroso
   Ningthingkip Cross phung tai ah Ama juichak rawi
   Ratha ranak khale neeiman chang urawi
   Pumi chongkha mulung lut tak ah thawrawi.

3. Khrista Beekrup rang ape rat urawi
   Phale neei rangkachak Pumi rang niroso
   Nineei athadet kha Amadeeng pe rawi
   Numpok khutchawi kha lekse thang lai nawnih.

4. Krista Beekrup rang athaboi pai rawi
   Chitna leen rasat na 'Ma numpok na mawtawr
   Ahoi ringkho nidin kha manrang nanih
   Nakhekpa khutchang teeng nokoi ra-ngai ro.
