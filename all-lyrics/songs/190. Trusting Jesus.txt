 190. Trusting Jesus          S.S.836.

                 Job.13:15.

 Key 2/Flat

 1. Ningthing kip tak ah kawsawn
    Rithi khopui kar khan awm
    Katak' sawn na dawk a-awm
    Jisu te tak ah sawn rang.

           Pha rikip tak ah kawsawn

           Ningthigkip tak ah kawsawn

           I rikip sok sekhan awm

           Jisu te tak ah sawn rang.

2. 'Ma ratha avar khana
   Kumulung sung ah jongvar
   Lam nakaipe pur nawning
   Jisu te tak ah sawn rawng.

3. Kalam lawn na releng leh
   Lah hoi thaw a nik chak rang
   Thit natheei khan' Ma koirang
   Jisu te tak ah sawn rang.

4. Kiringkho hawnghek malim
   Reeleei hawngthang malalim
   Ravanram kosok malim
   Jisute tak ah sawn rang.
