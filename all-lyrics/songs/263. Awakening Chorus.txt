263. Awakening Chorus               3rd Alex. 405.

                    Ps. 97:1

Key C.

1. Thoiro, thoiro, mapak na lah kha thawro
    Thoiro, thoiro lahtha heso rasang khale
    Thoiro, thoiro, reeleei ningai sip roso
    Chungteeng ah put avar khana jong eh
    Mole lawn thing le long na'awm, mapakna he mosok
    Patheen khut kon lim khana mapak uh.

            Jehovah Reng nijoi, munu awm mawtawr joi

            Ningai uro mapak uro Jehovah Reng

            Reeleeihin Ama jeng na rophong joi

             Ma jengna ponlap rasang masak

             Rengkha changjoi, Mapak uro, Lawmro,

            Jehovah Reng.

 2. Suro, suro ningaina daarkha suro
     Reeire ret ro ningai na chawng le lah hoikha
     Vanoi reeleei sawna hi thang malalim
     Ravantir hai le ningai lah thaw rang.
      Manmeeseh thitna munu aput Anakhek joi
      Kumsawt ningaina lah kha le lawm ro.
