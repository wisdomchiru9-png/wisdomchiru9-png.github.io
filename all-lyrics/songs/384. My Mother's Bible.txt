384. My Mother's Bible             R.S. 716.
Key B/Flat.

1. Atha maanrawp Buk khat awm
   Makha nikho haikha nimining masak
   Kunu atawr chung ah
   'Ma khut le nuru maw-awi
   Kunu he renem le raw-awi kha kejet.

           Reetheeng Buk, maanrawp chawng

           Mikrithi cho mabang ngamkha kiti jawt

           Nikho rikip kete jawt

           Lawn na lam nejet nawk awm

           'Mana Inn hoi sekhan lam nakai pejoi.

2. Jisu numpok kha neereei
    'Na naihai num nurupok
    Idaws saw ah thing chunga nuruthit pe
    'Mana pokrik nuphung pe, kimik rithi nonope.
    Kununa nutum ah chawnghoi neereei joi.

3. Aseso nikho haikha
    Tunse kimining jawn jai
    Chawng reetheeng Buk khana lam nakai pe lai
    'Ma idit thaw rang kani, kunu inuru reeipe
    Kumulung ah machawngkha kopok rep joi.
