 353. Dedication Hymn               T.N.B.L. 80.
 Key E/Flat.

 1. Renro reetheeina manmeese hin
    Ama chon thaw rang nojong tir
    Kineei ak nangdeeng kenepek uh
    Numpok ah nuruloi uro

           Nangdeeng kenepek uh

           Reetheeng athar namachang uro

           Nangdeeng kawnawhawng pek uh

           Inn thar tia no loi pe uroh.

2. Solomon na inepek inn kha
   Atharra nang loipe, kha ang ah
   Jak la lam ah kawnawhawng pek uh
   Inn thar ti ah noloi pe uroh.

3. Chon kholok mawtawr narang khan
   Keeini rang nang, 'mining khaw
   Kumulung sung ah jong awm roso,
   Lungngam tak ah kenepek ujoi.
