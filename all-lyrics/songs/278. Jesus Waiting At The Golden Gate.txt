278. Jesus Waiting At The Golden Gate
C.S.L. 429.

1. Ravan ram iti avarpeng khan
Jisu na mawkawt kung khan na-ngak
Makha ram kha manmeeseh hile rala tak ah awm
Kha munkhan hawng rumu veek rang.
Athar ram hoi chungchon
Mawkawt khan Jisuna keeini hi nuru ngak ah awm
289
Rangkachak ram hoi khan
Keeini hawng rumu ngir rang.

2. Raavantha chawng hi keeini deeng nemejet
Keeirang ah chawng hi ahoi rok kha nih
Pumina neereei "Kanaihai keeideeng hawng uro" ti
Nomoroi chak urang kani.

3. Makha munkhan athang awmlai mak
Ratha mulung saw ah chap awm lai mak
Athi resen awm laimak Pumi tak ah sawn hai kha
Athar Jerusalem munkhan.
