192. Tenderly Pleading            S.S. 449.             201

                  Jn. 10:9.

Key G.

1. Hawngkir rang ro saw reeveei ah thang hai;
   Enro, Khrista na vonhin nokoijoi
   Nasan rang le neereetheeng narangkhan
   Hawng relen ro Jisu deeng sum tinih.

           Numpok hekha na nokoi lai nangkha

           Ra-ngai ro Jisu numpok hekha

           Numpok he khana nokoilai nangkha

           Mirit ah nokoi hawngro ti-ah.

2. Ana-ngak chak lai nangthang naboi rang
   Ram latak sok ah nang ruthang a-awm
   Ama thisen mirit le nakhek joi
   Relek numpok nahi reei ngam boi nih.

3. 'Ma chawngtha le numpok na mining ro
   Munu neeiboi pet ah Cross ruphung joi
   Nangkha nakhek ah ringna nepek joi
   Athang boi rang ah ratha nepek joi.

4. Lam adik teeng khan nangni hawng rang ro
   Adik mawkawt Jisu deeng lut rang ro
   Jisuna he renem ah nokoi lai
   Hawngro nang dawk nakha en lai noro.
