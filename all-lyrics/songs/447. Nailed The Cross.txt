447. Nailed The Cross 3d Alex.54.
Co.2:13.
Kay F.

1. Kuruhut ah thirang ajawt mikhat ru-awm
Karatha aringrang rutuk mak
Cross khana keeirang ringna lam nokon pe ah
Kumunu arik napai pejoi.
Manina Cross ling khan
Jotpi le hem ujoi
Idawr ra sawna i-rudoi mo?
Saw reeveei tak af Jisuna Cross teeng ruse
Kumunu nuru chawi peso joi.

2. Keeirang khan nun renem, numpok le nodoi pe
Keeseetna mulung numuk pe
Keereetheeng 'tunsojoi seetna neeilai naw ning
Cross khan kumunu achi sojoi.

3. Kumunu haihi Cross kungkhan kamak sole
Munu pokrik rede lai nawni
Pumi Jisu Cross ah' khai ah athi khana
Munuphung keei hi nawrawchawk joi.

4. Jisukha dep rep ah, mathan lak lai naw ning
Ningkip ningai ah' Ma chon thawrang
Kener na mulung ngam na ningai lah thaw ah
Keeseet na napai perang nijoi.
