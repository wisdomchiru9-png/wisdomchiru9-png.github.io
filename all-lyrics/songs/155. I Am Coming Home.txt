155. I Am Coming Home
Matt. 11:28.
Key F.

1. Asaw renro reeveei keeina
Pumi nangchawng hi jui rawng
Khrista kha tak ah kawsawn ah
Vonhin nangdeeng kahawng joi.
Keei kahawng joi...keei kahawng joi
Raavantha tum narang khan
Keei kahawng joi... keei kahawng joi
Saw muchum narang kahawng.

2. Reetheeng det le mulung renem
Jisu keei kahawng ret joi
Na-ngak chak ro mohot boi ah
Kaw-awm na mun rikip ah.

3. Nangdeeng kung ah kaw-awm lai hin
Karatha hoina muk rang
Zing nisok thar kawhawng muk le
Ningai raavantha muk rang.

4. Ningai ah nuru ngak tana Munuah put nakhek ro
Kamaiso namavar pero
Kumunu reetheeng roso.
