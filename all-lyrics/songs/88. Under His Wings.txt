88. Under His Wings S.S. 541.

                   Ps. 17.8.

Key D/Flat

 1. Ama matha thoi khan hoi tak ah ka-awm
    Azing rithi khopui kha rang a-awm
    Ama beei rep ah 'mana nuhup rang nih
    Nakhek ah keei Ama nai kanijoi.

           Ama matha....matha thoi khan

            Tunamo nesen ngam nih?

            Matha thoi khan karatha awm rang

            Hoining ah awm jawn jai rang.

 2. Ama matha thoi khan rekhe na mun awm
    Mulung ngam chet ah sawmuchum na awm
     Reeleei chung hin dam na jawn jai rang awm mak
     Kha munkhan ningaina hoina kaman.

 3. Ama matha thoi khan manrawp hoina awm
     Makhan kiring lim rethe rang kanih
     Nurung chak ah atha boina nisi mak
     Jisu deeng kumsawt saw kumuchum joi.
