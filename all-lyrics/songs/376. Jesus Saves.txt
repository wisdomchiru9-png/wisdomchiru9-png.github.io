 376. Jesus Saves         S.S. 1079.

                 Acts. 16:31.

 Key G.

1. Ningai he kha nejet joi, Jisuna nasaan joi
    Chawngkha reeiro munrikip, Jisuna nakhek joi
   Chawngkha chawiro mun rikip, Tui le mo rakaan tana
   Sero Patheen chawngpek ni, Jisuna nasaan joi.

2. Sawna rana kha doiro, Jisuna nasaan joi
   Munuphung hai deeng reeiro, Jisuna nakhek joi
   Tuile mo-ah lah thawro, tuipui le rehem roso
   Reeleei ah 'Ma Jubilee, Jisuna nasaan joi.

3. Lunghang pha khan lah thaw ro, Jisuna nasaan joi
   'Mana nithit pe jarra, kumsawt ringna nepek
   Nangsaw phakhan lah thawro, Numpok kha nangdit phakhan
   Mawtawr na lah kha thawro, Jisuna nakhek joi.

4. He rasang tak ah reeiro, Jisuna nasaan joi
   Phung rikip ningai roso, Jisuna nakhek joi
   Ringkho mo nara kan na, tuipui kawmchang rikip ah
   Mahi mawtawrna lah ni, Jisuna nasaan joi.
