381. The Old And The New

1. Ase kumlui sung ah sawna le hoina
Kholok haikha tuntu kumthaar nikho hina
Mulungthaar khale sawna kha mawtawr joi
Reemeei rinim so le avaar kha ang ah (2)
Chubai kumlui
Resen pha hawngsok joi
Patheen na kum le tha, nikho, rotho
Nangle resen naret rang ah, phakha
Hawngsok joi, kumthaar nikho le
Ningai damna, ringna awmchak roso
Ahoi kumthaar niroso.

2. Sawleng reeipar athale kholum hoipha
Raamthim ah reeipar khana kumulung mapar
Muchi rawkhawm pha theeira amin ang ah
Kumlui le choiso ah dawnthar loi ret joi (2)

3. Chubai kumlui resen na pha hawngsok joi
Kumthar le ningai tak ah rumuk narang khan
Nang miroina meeneei ngam u mi-ing
Nangjarra kumthar lukhum kamaan ujoi (2).
