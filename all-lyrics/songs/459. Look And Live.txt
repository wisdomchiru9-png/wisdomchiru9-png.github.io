459. Look And Live            S.S. 411.

                Isaiah 14:22.

Key G.

1. Pumi chawngkha keeneeijoi, Halleluyah
    Chawngkha nangni deeng nepek joi
    Machawng meezeek ah awm joi, Halleluyah
    Mahi rang ah hi entana ringro.

           Sokpui enro ringna rang

           Jisu entana ringro

            Machawng meezeek ah awmjoi,... Halleluyah

           'Hirang ah hi entana ringro.

2. Numpok na chawngkha keeneei, Halleluyah
    Role chim ningai rang ah ni
    Ravanram chawngkha inih... Halleluiyah
    Jisu chawng adik hi keeina kezet.

3. Nangdeeng ringkho kepek joi.. Halleluyah
    Kumsawt ringna nangmuk rang nih
    'Ma tekha nang en atu... Halleluyah
   Nakhek ngam Jisu tekha enro.
