274. In The Upper Garden             R.S.866.
Key G/Flat.

1. Jordon tuipui vara teengkhan
   Tuipui let na rakan le
   Kumsawt ringna rang ram khat awm
   Khamun khan ringna tui long
   Tuipui avar longna munkhan
   En awm ringna thingkung mawng
   Lawmdit haina nuru ngak chak
   Chungteeng ah rohon sung khan.

          Zingkar hoikhan rumuk pui rang

          Tuidung khan saw muchum rang

          nahawng rang khan nuru ngak chak

          Chungteeng ah rohon sungkhan

2. En awm reeipar haikha ru mawng
   Chungteeng ah rohon sung khan
   Makha ram kha nosok le khan
   Ravan rohon khan awm rang
   Achoiboi reeipar kholok par
   Khamunkhan thitna awm mak
   Avar ringkho kha awm jawn jai
   Chungteeng ah rohon sung khan.

3. Reeleei ringkho hi rotho le
   Nahawng rang nuru ngak chak.
   Chungteeng ah rohon nosok le
   Ahoi rapa kung tak khan
   Sawna mulung hi koi a-awm
   Pokrik kholok kha pai ah
   Zingkar hoikhan hawng rumu rang
   Chungteeng ah rohon sung khan.
