349. Take My Hand Precious Lord
TNBC. 19. MBC. 34:
Key A/Flat.

1. Kupumi Keeihi nomoroi chak rang roh
Kasaw joi ranaak neei lai mi-ing
Rithi khopui hawng rang phakhan nomo roi ro
Kupumi keeihi nomoroi chak ro

2. Kawlawn na lam hi hawng khawk sisit pha khan
Num napo hawng tawm de det ah awm
Kina hai na he hi nuru jet peroh
Kupumi keeihi nomoroi chak roh.

3. Mateeng lai keeina kumunu rujet mi-ing
Pumina keeihi numukui ah
Munu pok rik kiphung Pumina numuchum
Idawr ra num inopok mo nijoi?

4. Manmeese sawna meeisa doi ah kachap
Kawmawng jarra rusaw kanijoi
Jisu kabeei relen na num nopok ret ah
Zingkar var kha ang ah namavar joi.
