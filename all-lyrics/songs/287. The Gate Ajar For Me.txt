287. The Gate Ajar For Me             S.S. 372.

                Rev.21:25.

Key C/Flat.

1. Kawtpui rawlawng ah keeirang awm
   Ahoi mawkawt khan mun awm
   Ala tak alur Cross deengkhan
   Nakhekpa numpok malar.

           Oh, 'Ma numpok athuk khana

           Kawt kha keeirang rawlawng ah awm

           Keeirang kawt kha rawlawng.

2. Vanoirang kawt rawlawng a-awm
    Ringna atawk hai rang khan
    Rawchawng sara asin aleen
    Vanoi phung rikip rang khan.

3. Maton sawn tana nawng rang ro
    Numpok mawkawt mawlawng lai
    Cross jarra Reng lukhum tum ro
    Kumsawt numpok khutchawi nih.

 4. Tuipui vara hoi teeng khan keei
    ni ranga Cross kha ru-awm
    Ring na Cross kha rukhu ah
     Pumi kha numpok det rang.
