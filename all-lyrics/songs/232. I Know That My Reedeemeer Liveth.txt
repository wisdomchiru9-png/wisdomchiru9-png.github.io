232. I Know That My Reedeemeer Liveth
R.S.919.
Key D/Flat.

1. Aring Nakhekpa kha kejet
Reeleeihin jongleng ret rang nih
Kumsawt ringna nepek kejet
Numpok le Ngam 'ma khut ah awm
Keeina kejet Aring Jisu kha
Reeleei hin jongleng ret rang nih
Keeina kejet ringna nepek joi
Numpok le Ngam 'Ma khut ah awm.

2. 'Ma chawng rakham na koinaw nih
'Ma ireei chawng thang lai nawnih
Athina taksa hi mathang awm
keeina Ama mukrang kanih.

3. Awm narang munkon kha kejet joi
Khamun khan keei 'Male awm rang
sindawngroi keeirang ah miningkha
Dawng nikhan jongsok rang nijoi.
