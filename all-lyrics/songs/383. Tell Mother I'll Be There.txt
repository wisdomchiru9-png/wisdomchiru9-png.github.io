383. Tell Mother I'll Be There               R.S. 742.

1. Kisinlai kiringkho awmchan kawhawng mining le
    Reei ka-ra-ngai boi jarra kunu khadawr khan rusaw
    Tunla kunu kha ruthi joi, kachap zet lai nawni
    Pumi kunudeeng khan hawngrang kani.

            Oh, patheen reeipero kunudeeng recipero

            Pathen deeng ah nihi meeneei mi-ing

            Kunudeeng reeipero keei awm hawngrang kani

            Kunudit awm nakhan hawngrang kani.

 2. Chawng kujui nawk awm kunu na khadawr num nopok
    Kunukha kurupor na awm numpok ah nuru beek
    Kawsaw kara nakhan awm, amana nurudoi pui
    Pumi kunu deengkhan hawng rang kani.

 3. Kunu chawng juiboi khat kuru chang ah kuru chai pha
     Keei nimining na mulung saw ah thitna runeei deng
     Patheen tenret 'muk narang sun-zan na nuru niik pe
     Pumi kunu deeng ah hawng rang kani.

 4. Nikhat kunu na chawng inavak mawchawi kha
     Kunu meh kumuk malakhan kunu rang ruchat joi
     Kunu thit mala khan keeina chawng rakham khat keeneei
     Kunu awm na sekhan hawngrang kani.
