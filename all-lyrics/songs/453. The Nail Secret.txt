  1. Nang mulung mining kha sok boi jar khan
       Mulung saw ah awm maw nang ni ?
       Mulung rachak narang teei tang awm lai mak
       Nun renem Jisu tehi nih.

         Khut ah ichi kha en rangro  vel 1 som

         Dep ah ivit kha en rang ro

         Ama tena nangkha num nopok rep joi

         Ama niboi sik awm lai mak.

                        twa warltwa

2. Miring na inapai kha nangsir pui maw?
  Nang mikrithi jongcho lamkhan
  Jisu inokoi nakhan hawng lut rang ro
  Pokrik nang muchum rang nijoi.

3. Thitna lamkhan nangthen na lawn maw nang ni ?
  Thimna munkhan awm maw nang ni ?
  Jisuna nokoi 'Madeeng hawngro ti-ah
   Madeeng lawmdit kholok awm joi.
