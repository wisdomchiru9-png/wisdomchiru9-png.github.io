 284. A Home On High    S.S. 957.

                    Jn. 14:3.

 Key N/Flat

 1. Nitak na ram mun kha khe ah
     Reemeei awm na khe ah
    Zah Arshi var na mun khe ah
    Ahoi inn khat keeneei.

            Khut ah konboi Ravan inn kha

            Keeirang rasam so joi

            Ravan tir hai lah awina mun

            Inn kha keei rang ah nih.

2. Saw reeveei tenret na khe ah din
    Kumsawt ringna awm na
    'Pha rotho iti awm naboi mun
    Madan sa ki-inn awm.

3. Thitna azing ram kha khe ah
    Sun jan awm naboi mun
    Mulung ah ningai sip pha khan
   Ki-inn varkha muk rang.

4. Munu sawna chitna kholok
   Kamathan so veek le  prot
   Kumsawt ah hoi na ram munkhan
   Khrista le awmsom rang.
