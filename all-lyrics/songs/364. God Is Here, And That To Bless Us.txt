364. God Is Here, And That To Bless Us
S.S.307.
Ps. 29:11.
Key A/Flat

1. Patheen na raavan nepek joi
Ratha ranak neei nakha
Enroh, reemeei hawng releng joi
Ngak ro phortha jong chum kha
Kupumi nangdeeng kininiik
Raavantha jongmuchumro
Keeini na kangak chaku,
Mulung namathar pero.

2. Patheen na keei le awm som
Amadeeng ringna mun awm
Ratha namathar pe uro
Nang numpok ranak kha.

3. Patheen kha tak ah kawsawn joi
Nidit 'Madeeng hawng chawi ro
'Ma numpok khana nasaan joi
Mulung nelek malalim

4. Kininiik u noloi pe ro
Tak ah sawn na kabu uh
Nang numpok pawp mawlawng tana
Raavan nojong pe uro.
