                 1.Cor. 2:9.
Key G/Flat.

1. Nisun nek ah 'vardet ram khat awm
   Tak ah sawn jarra muk rang kani
   Makhan napa na nuru ngak chak
   naw-awm narang mun nuru kon peh.

          Hoitak ah..... daw dawn nah

          Ahoi kawmchang khan rumu som rang (2)

2. Ahoi kawmchang khan lah kha thaw rang
   Ravantha lah hoi chungchon khale
   Ratha sawna ret awm lai naw nih
   Numpok saw muchum nakha muk rang.

3. Chung ah ravat boi napa awm na
   Mirit tak ah mapak rang nanih
   'Ma numpok khutchawi alur kha le
   Phor khana ringkho reetheeng masak.
