258. The Honme Over There               S.S. 942.     PRE

                John. 14:2.

Key A.

1. Oh, mining ro ahoi inn kha
   Avar tuihawr vara deeng tak khan
   Khamun na awm mireetheeng hai kha
   Pon ramasa achek reeseei.

           Khamun kha... Khamun kha

          Oh, miningro inn hoi daan kha

          Kha mun kha.. khamun kha.

          Oh, miningro inn hoi daankha.

2. Kharam ah awm kha mining ro
   Namateeng ah ruhawng so hai kha
   Ningai na lah kha thaw jawn jai
   Pumi khopui inn hoi sung khan.

3. Khamunkhan Nakhekpa ru-awm
   Lawmdit sokpui hai awm na munkhan
   Sawna renro reetheei awm nawnih
   Makha munhoi sekhan vungrang.

4. Relen tak ah sok rang kani
   Reeleei ringkho hi mawtawr sole
   Khamun khan numpok mirit haina
   Na-ngak ah nuru enchak rang nih.
