 478. The Suffering Of The Lord.
 Ney B/Hat

 1. Nakhekpa thisen kha jonglong
    Munu reetheeng narang
    'Ma lu reetheeng kha mukun joi
    Karatha ringna rang.

           Ke-ngam te nun-renempa kha Calvary ah jongthi

           Ke-ngam te athi hawngring ret pha roikhan

      Turn Anakhekpa kha nih.

2. Kiru rehena awm jarra
   Thing Cross chung ah jong chap
    Numpok miritna keei naa-re
   Nepek kumsawt ringna.

3. Ini-awm ranaak tak ah vaar
   Nit'awm 'vaar reetheeng mak
   Nakhekpa na seetna rikip
   Nawchawipe Cross chung ah.

4. Kawraw ngawi awm maton sawn rang
   Pumi Cross kungteeng khan
   Idoi sawna kha mining rawng.
   Nithit pe joi Cross ah. 01
