465. "Hark Ten Thousand And Voices" S.S. 149.
Heb. 1:6.
Key G.

1. Ra-ngai ro, chungteeng ah hekha
Bawpsawm tingtang mirijoi
Rengchang joi Ravan ningai joi
Numpok Patheen Jisu kha
Ama Rengmun khan sujoi
Jisu reeleei Reng changjoi.
Halleluyah (2)
Jisu reeleei Rengchang joi.

2. Kumsawt alur Rengkha nijoi
Kumsawt Reng lukhum neeijoi
Nangmumpok khan resen nawnih
Nang miring chang hailimkha
Nangnumpok ningai nakha
Nangmaipar khana malar.
Halleluyah (2)
Nangmai par khana malar.

3. Nakhekpa meh malang narang
Ningai nikho kha naijoi
Koina he kha kejet phakhan
Ravan reeleei thang rang nih
Makhan ningai lah kha thaw rang
Lur roso Nereng pakha.
Halleluyah (2)
Lur roso Nakhekpa kha.
