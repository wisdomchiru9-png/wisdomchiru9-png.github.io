                  Isaiah, 14:22.

 Key A/Flat.

 1. Enro, Calvary ah nasaanpa
     Nangle keeirang sawna nodoi pe
     Ra-ngairo numpok ah nang nokoi
     Nangring rang nijoi.

          Enro, enrangro

          Nangring rang nijoi   197

          Enro Cross ah nangrang athi kha

          Nangring rang nijoi.

2. Munu pokrik phung nakhek rangkhan
   Kumsawt ringna kha nang neei joi maw?
   Jisu tena ringna nepek ngam
   Enro, ring narang.

3. Thaan na hawngthoi Nakhekpa enro
   Mun awmlai hin hawng relen rangro
  Ama maivaar na na-eh rang nih
   Enro ring narang.

4. Jisuna chungteeng numpok chawng reei
  Munu phung hai sawna kha zet veek
  Nang kachawi nakhan noroi rang nih
  Enro, ring narang.
