254. We Are Marching To Zion               S.S.823.

                  Ps. 49:2.

Doh is G.

1. Pumi kha num apo hawngro ningai muk rang
   Lah hoi thaw khan hawng jaw rang ro.. (2)
   Makhan Rengmun kha heeichip ah ding urang ro

           Keeini se urang rawi ahoi ram Zion munse

           Keeini Zion teeng nase ujoi

           Patheen khopui ahoi sekhan.

2. NeePetheen azetboi khan a lah thaw nawk awm
   Ravan Rengpa naihai natu.. (2)
   'Mani ningai nakha ramtin na reei roso.

3. Zion lawn khan muk ngamboi a-tui tampui numuk
   Ravan loimun nosok mala. . (2)
   Rangkachak lamhoi khan nalawm malalim seh.

4. Lah kha thaw rawi mik rithi kha ho rang nijoi
   Emmanuel teeng se urawi.. (2)
   Ahoi Ravan ram khan keeini nasok rang nih.
