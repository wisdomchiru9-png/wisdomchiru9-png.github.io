422. Rejoice In The Lord            S.S. 207.

                 Ps. 68:3.

Key 'B/Flat.

1. Mulung dik miring rikip na
   Pumi deeng ningai ah hawngro
   Amakha nangta machang ro
   Nang sawna he sok rang nijoi.

          Ningai...Ningai                 2

           Pumi deeng ningai ah hoirawi

2. Ravan le manmeeseh chunghin
   Leek ah rasang det Pumi nih
   Arat ajeng inakhek na
   Chawng khana keeini nopok joi.

3. Adik rang rathaw na munkhan
   Narahapui hai sip a-awm
   patheen rami awm muruk ah
    naraha pui mawtawr narang.

4. Avar nang ineei rikip kha
    Azing khana numu khu-ah
    Mulung suk let ah tet noro
    Patheen deeng net tak ah awm ro.

5. Nupumi rangkhan ningai ro
    Amakha lah le mapak ro
    Khong le lawi he mosok tana
    Halleluiya ti-ah lawm ro.
