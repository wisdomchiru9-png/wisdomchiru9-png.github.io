343. Thy Will In Me.
Ps. 14:8.
Key B/Flat.

1. Pumi mu ngam mi-ing
Lam nang neereei makhan
Riling ru awm ah awm,
Reeipar ru awm ah awm,
Imo sok ni iti, Pumi nangna nangjet
Nangna nang i-nawmaw thawjawt kha neereei ro,
Nang idit kha keei thaw rawng
Pumi nang deeng kenepek
Kithit kiring awm nasaan ro
Pumi nang idit thaw rawng.

2. Sawna rana tui hawr kha keeina rakan rang
Chit narang awm naw ni, saw narang awm naw nih,
Lam nang neereei atu, mutur ping a lawn rang
Chinoro nang ini tikhan, keei ra-ngai rang.

3. Ningai lam ah lawn rang, lamkha kejet nawk awm
Nang inemejet lam khan, keeina alawn rang
Nang inawmawthaw jawt, kha keeina thaw ngir rang
Nang numpok na nang misaan na khan nawchawiro.
