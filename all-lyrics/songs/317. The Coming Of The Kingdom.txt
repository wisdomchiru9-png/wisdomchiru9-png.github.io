 317. The Coming Of The Kingdom                   S.S.168.

                  Lk. 21:28.

 KeyB/Flat

 1. Ravan lai chung ah Rengmun khan ningai khana nuru ngak
   Kumsawt ah mireetheeng hai ruchun na mun
   Nikho dawdawn na ase hina Ravan ram jong ma nai.
   Patheen ram jong narang ah phakha naijoi.

           Oh, Patheen ram jong narang ah phakha nai joi (2)

           karatha rasam rang ro

           Sumlawi miri kha jong rehem rang nijoi

           Ma lurna jong lar rang nih.

2. Nimining ah phak ngamboi ningai Rengmun rasam a awm
   Ne-enle Nakhekpa kha numuk rang nih
   Reeleei ningai lim na inikhit aput nasok rang nih
   Patheen ram jong narang ah phakha naijoi.

3. Patheen ram jong le ningaina Reng Pumi numuk rang nih
   Reng numuk mala Rengmun malang rang nih
   Halleluiya 'Ma riming mapak ro thisen le nakhek
   Patheen ram jong narang ah phakha nai joi.
   Sawt tak ah nuru ngak jarra reeleei sawna hawng rawp joi
   Chit jarra miring mulung noi rede joi
   Patheen ram chawng reei tai ah lah le mulung mapar perawi
   Patheen ram jong narang ah phakha naijoi.

                                                                   Sp. Pani maikha, parts: Rumuk phakhan
