306. He Is Coming            S.S. 172.

                  John. 14:3.

Key A/Flat.

1. Reeleei sawna kar hin 'Ma jong joi
    Tun rasang ningai awm
     Hossanah ti-ah Pumi jongjoi
     Ravan reemeei karra.

             Halleluiya... Halleluiya

              Ama jong ret rang nih

              Keeini na ningai ah ruchun rang

              Ama Rengchang nakhan

   2. Numpok ah Nakhekpa kha jongjoi
       Ke-ngam ang ah that uh

        Napa Patheen leen na lur na khan

        Reeleei ah awm rang nih.

    3. Nupumi lamkaipa kha jongjoi

        Keeini Nakhek Rengpa

         'Ma meh avar kha numuk rang nih

         Mapak na lah thaw rang.

     4. Ama miring lim rawkhawm rang nih

         'Ma riming ah koirang

         Amana ikhek phung rikip kha

         Ama miring nijoi.
