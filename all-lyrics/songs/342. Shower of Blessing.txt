342. Shower of Blessing           R.S. 10.
Key Flat

1. Nangriming ah kuruchun uh,
   Nomothoi uro Pumi
   Raavantha phor pek rang ti
   Nangchawng hina rophong joi.

          Pumi noloi pero

          Kinik kine ngen nahi

          Pumi nang numpok na phor kha

          Von hin nojong pe uro.

2. Tun hin nang numpok na phor kha
   Mulung hin jong awm roso
   Numpok awm na nang deeng kung khan
   Keeini na kininiik uh.

3. Raavantha phor kha jongrang ni
   Chawng rakham na koi naw ni,
   Kininiik uhi nang jet ah
   Tak ah sawn nakha awm joi.

4. Ningai phor tha kha ke ngen uh
   Nangdeeng a awm phor hai kha
   Keeini nang nepek ujar khan
   Nang jeng na leen rang nijoi.
