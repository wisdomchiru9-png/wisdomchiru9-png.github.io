351. Deeper Deeper C.S.L. 28.
Key A/Flat.

1. Pumi Jisu nang numpok nakhan net tak ah awmchak rawng
Lungboiroi hai san narang nang rat nakha nojong pero.
Numpok awm na det rang
Nomothoi ro Pumi
Theem nakha nepero
Thuk det ah nuru chu ro.

2. Ratha ring na Patheen Raavantha kha nojong pe uro,
Khrista deeng kiring khohi net tak ah dep nadet rang khan.

3. Tak ah sawn na ara neei narang khan nemejet chak ro
Arachak nakhekpa risim kha keeideeng naracha ro.

4. Khristana jongkir pharoi khan leek ah ningai na muk rang
Mirikip ratha ringna le saw muchum na kha muk rang.
