89. Let Other See Jesus M.H. 224.
Key A.

1. Munu lamkhan nawlawn reetheei
Nawlawm isik na ne-en
Mulung reeetheeng masak heei ah
Nang deeng Jisu malar ro.

Nangna Jisu meh malar ro
Ama chawng le adik chon khan
Se na hawng na rikip khan reei pe tana
Misik deeng Jisu mumuk ro.

2. Nang ithaw chon kholok en le
Misik haina jet rang nih
Adik rok kha mumuk tana
Mideeng Jisu malar ro.

3. Ituk ningai rang mo nanih
Atha chon nawthaw le khan
Nang ivak chawi miring kha le
Ravan ram ah rumu rang.

4. Azing awmboi ram hoi mun khan
Khrista le ring jawn jai rang
Khamun khan awm nasom rang khan
Athang ratha vak chawi ro.
