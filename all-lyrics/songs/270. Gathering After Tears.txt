270. Gathering After Tears              S.S. 992.

                 Rev. 7:17.

Key B/Flat.

1. Innteeng sena rang lam midik rawi
   Mining sok narang khan jap ro
   Nuru ngak lawmdit hai mining ro
   Rokong muchum le makhan awm rang.

           Mikrithi ho le ruchun rang nanih

           Chon thaw ah saw hi bang so le ruchun rang

           Ina khekna ningai lah hoi kha ra-ngai ro

           Ningai na mun hoi sung khan ruchun rang.

2. Inn teeng se narang lam midik rawi
   Chit na neeiboi pet ah serawi
   Rokong chum na mun nedep lekhan
   Saw ra-ngak na avar kha muk rang.

3. Ni inn ru awm na avar lawn khan
   Apar jawn jai reeipar na buk
   Khamunkhan Nakhekpa ru awm joi
   Ningai mapak na lah kha thaw rang.

4. Inn teeng khan rachak rithi hawng rang
   Ahoi zingkar awm hawng var joi.
   Nawlawm dit hai khale rumurang
   Sawna natna thit na awm lai mak.
