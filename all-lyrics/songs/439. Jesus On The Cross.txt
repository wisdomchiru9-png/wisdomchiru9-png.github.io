439, Jesus On The Cross.

1. Jisuna ravan naput nang jong
Thing Cross chung ah nang ruthi
Sawna kholok keeinirang nang doi
Sawna nangdoi hai naw!
Sawna kha nangdoi hai naw!
Nasaan rang khat se awm mak
Ravan ram nangsok phakhan
Raavantha jongtir ro.

2. Ravan ram ahoi nang mathan nah
Sawna doi ah nang ruthi
Seetna aput nang nojong khek uh
Numpok leenhai dak naw!

3. Seetna neeiboi sawna nangdoi naw!
Munuphung hai keeini rang
Nangthisen le nang nojong khek uh
Ningai reei ngam boi' nih.
