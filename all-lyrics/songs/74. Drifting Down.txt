 74. Drifting Down           R.S. 233.
 Key A/Flat.

1. Rokong jap nangdawk jarra
    Kawmchang ah put nawchawi joi
    Nangkha nawchawi, nangkha nawchawi dedet joi
    Tuipui letna nawnawng joi
    Athuk tuili lai teengkhan
    Jamti-ah nangkha nawchawi joi. nawchawi joi.

            Nangkha nawchawi joi...nawchawi joi

            Ningnit naboi sawna teeng

            Nangkha nawchawi joi

           Napa numpok ten ret na, jam ti-ah nawchawi joi

           Nangkha nawchawi joi.

2. Naram teeng avar khana
    Rekhe narang nemejet
    Nangkha nawchawi, nawchawi nangkha nawchawi joi
    Rokong chumna vara teeng,
   Jap nawrawng maw rokong kha?
   Jamti-ah nangkha nawchawi joi, nawchawi joi.

3. Naram teeng ah hekhana rekhe narang nahai joi
   Nangkha nawchawi, nawchawi nangkha nawchawi joi
   Oh, sokpui ngak lai noro, ra-ngai rep ro sum tini
   Maton sawn na nang nawchawijoi, nawchawi joi.
