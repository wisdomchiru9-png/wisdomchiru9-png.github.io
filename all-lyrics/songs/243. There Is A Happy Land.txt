243. There Is A Happy Land                R.S.680.
Key F.

1. Makhan ramhoi khat awm
   Ram latak ah
   Mireetheeng hai awm na, nivar ang ah
   Oh, lah hoikha thaw u
   Nakhekpa reng chungchon nih
   He rasang ah lawm ro
   Mohot boi ah.

2. Ram hoi khan hawng uro,
   Hawng relen ro
    Rakhana neeiboi ah
   Sum lai noro,
   Oh, ningal rang nanih
   Munu sawna hek so le
   Pumi le awm som rang
   Chat boi pet ah.

3. Nimik hawngvar rang ni
   Ramhoi munkhan
   Numpok bang lai naw nih
   'Pa mai kung ah
   Ni-ningai na khale
   Reng lukhum mukhu rang nih
   Ni nek awm var det ah
   Reng chang rang nih.
