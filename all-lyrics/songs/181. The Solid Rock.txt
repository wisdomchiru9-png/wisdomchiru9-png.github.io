181. The Solid Rock           S.S. 902.

                  Ps. 74:22.

Key G.

1. Jisu thisen kha niboi ah
    Nem naboi kiru mining kha
    Chawng hoikha tak ah sawn naw ning
    Jisu riming khan awm chak rang.

            Khrista lungpum khan keei kading

           Mun teeitang lim tu nge rok nih (2)

2. Azing na 'Ma kha ri-ngi awm
    'Ma numpok khan saw muchum rang
    Rithi le khopui rang khan awm
    Thirchuk khana nemenet joi.

3. 'Ma chawngpek le thisen khana
    Sawna karkhan awm keei nasaan
    Ratha rang lam sik awm ah awm
    Ama deengkung tekhan awm rang.

 4. Sumlawi miri le jongpha khan
    'Mana keeihi numu ni maw?
     Munu kuru neei nawk atu
     Rengmun khan su rang pumi leh.
