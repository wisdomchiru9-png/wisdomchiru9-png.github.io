23. The Lord Is King                       S.S. 1081.

                   Ps. 10:16.

Key G/Flat.

1. Ravanlai ah rehem kumsawt lah ra-ngai ro
    Patheendeeng rasang lur nakha niroso
    Raavan pek na avar khan reetheeng haina lah kha thaw uh
    Nupumi kha Reng ni ti-ah ra-aai uh.

2. Mawtawrna lah ra-ngai ro, reelei rehem joi
    Tuipui leen ar a at ang ningai he kha nih
    Halleluiya mapak rawi rakhat ah mawtawr na lah thaw
    Pumikha Rengni ti-ah he mosok ro.

3. Thum pumikhat mapak ro ravantir haina
Apa Sapa Ratha reetheeng mapak ro
Ruseso phakha ang ah kum rikip rang khan mirijoi
Pumi kha Reng ni ti-ah rophong jown jai.
