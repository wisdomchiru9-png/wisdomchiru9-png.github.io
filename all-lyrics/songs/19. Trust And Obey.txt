19. Trust And Obey            S.S. 642.

                 Prov. 16:20.

Key F

1. Pumi le nawlawn pha, 'Ma chawngpek var sung khan
   Nalam raw kha idawr hoi rang mo?
   Atha chon nawthaw le 'Mana nurung rang nih
   'Mate kha tak ah sawn na juiro

           Tak ah sawn ro, chawngjui miring niro

           Hiniboi lam sik awm mak

           Ningai na muk narang.

2. Rilip awm lai naw nih, reemei jing lai naw nih
   'Ma mai var khana na-eh pha khan
   Chit na le rakhana, mik rithi long naw nih
   Tak ah sawn na-a jui hai lim kha

3. Munu pok rik phung ah asaw awm lai naw nih
   Nawsaw law 'Mana nepek rang nih
   Lung nat na awm naw nih, lunghang na awm naw nih
   Phor muk na rang khan tak ah sawn ro.

4. Ama numpok nakha, rekhen narang awm mak
    Pek munkhan nawhawng pek mala lim
   'Ma numpok athuk le, ningai inepek kha
   Tak ah sawn chawng jui hai rang ah nih.

5. Ahoi ah rawlawp ah, 'Ma kekung ah surang
    'Male rawtawp ah nawlawn pha khan
    "Ma chawngpek kha jui ah, nitir nakhan serang
    Chinoro tak ah sawn na jui ro.
