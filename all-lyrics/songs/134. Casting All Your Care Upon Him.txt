134. Casting All Your Care Upon Him S.S.87

1.Pet. 5:7
Key A/Flat

1. I dawrra ngam na awm moni
Nakhek pa ranaak hi;
Lemna rikip ah 'Ma khut le
Numukui kha kejet.
Pai veek ro...mulung kham nakha
Paiveek ro.. Ama deengkung khan
Paiveek ro...mulung kham nakha
'Mana nangkha ten naare rang nih.

2. Kipai ngam hi nang idit nih
Kidoi sawna rikip
Renro reeveei kholok nangdeeng
Neemejet rang kanih.

3. Nang numpok mirit tak' sawn rawng
Nangte kha ne-en rawng
Nangdeeng te saw muchum na awm
Kininik le pumi.

4. Ithaw mulung mawsaw rang mo?
Nuk ah sawna rang khan
Karatha nasaan pe nimaw?
ti-ah imo chirang.
