357. This The Blessed Hour Of Prayer
S.S.303.
Key E/Flat.

1. Numulung renem pha khan, Niik na pha hoi kha nih
Nakhekpa nawlawm Jisu deengkhan ruchun rang
Tak ah nawsawn rep ah tu 'Mana nasaan rang nih
Sawdoi hairang kha phakhan idawr ah hoi rang mo?
Niik na pha hoi kha nih.. (2)
Sawdoi hairang kha phakhan idawr ah hoi rang mo?

2. Pumina nedep phakhan, Niik na pha hoi kha nih.
'Ma miring haina zet narang numpok kha le
Neereei pha khan sawna kha 'ma kekung ah pai rang
Sawdoi hairang kha phakhan idawr ah hoi rang mo?

3. Lemna dawina phakhan awm, Niik na pha hoi kha nih
Nakhekpa na num ipok haikha saan rang nih
'Ma numpok miring haideeng saw reeveei apai peh,
Sawdoi hairang kha phakhan idawr ah hoi rang mo?

4. Tak ah nawsawn rep atu, Niik na pha hoi tak hin
Phor niniik hai kha keeinina nutum rang nih,
Ningai chunchon kar khan nawsaw nakha thang rang nih
Sawdoi hairang kha phakhan idawr ah hoi rang mo?
