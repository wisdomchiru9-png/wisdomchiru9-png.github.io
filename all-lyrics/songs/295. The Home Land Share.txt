Key D/Flat

1. Rithi khopui imijing na mun vara teeng khan
   Kumsawt ah ningai avar kha awm
   Nisun var ang ah var.

           Makhan sawna resen na jum na awm lai naw ni

           Tui vara kawmchang ah inn hoi khan

           Keeini rumu ngir rang.

   Ningai lah hoi thaw uh.

3. Azing phaijaw vara khat teeng khan lawm dit ruse
   Inn hoi khan rumusom rang nanih
   Ruchun som rang nanih.
