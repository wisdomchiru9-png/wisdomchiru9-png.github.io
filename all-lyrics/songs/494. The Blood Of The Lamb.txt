    494. The Blood Of The Lamb                 S.S. 113.

                      Ps. 32:7.

   Key D.

   1. Thisok longkha raavantha nih
      Manmeeseh munu jongro phong
      Patheen Sapa numpok khana
      'Mana sawna doi ah ruthi
      'Ma aput kuruchai joi awm
      Lungsaw na-ah put navak chawi
      Ke-ngam thisen le nuru suk
     Vur nek ah chek rang kanijoi.

             Vur nek ah chek rang (2)

             Ke-ngam thisen le nurusuk ro

             Vur nek ah chek rang kanijoi.

 2. 'Mana riling lukhum rukhu
    Cross kha' Mana mawtawr ah
    Sawna renro kholok phung ah
    Nem narang tak saw reeveei doi
    Keei awm thisen lamkai nakhan
    Munu reetheeng masak rang ro
   'Ma thisen le nuru suk ro
   Vur nek ah chek rang kani joi.

3. Patheen deeng ah put kuru chai
   Kumulung raraw nawk jawnjai
   Kumunu thisen niboi ah
   Tuile nurusuk ngam nawnih
  Jisu aput jonglong thinih
  Nangchawng rakham nakhan lengrang
  Nangthi khale nurusuk ro
  Vur nek ah chek rang kanih joi.
