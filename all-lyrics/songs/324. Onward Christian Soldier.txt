324. Onward Christian Soldier
Deut. 31:6.
Key F

1. Khristen raami sero, raa rawthaw pui rang
Maton na ruse Jisu kha en tanah
Raa pumi khrista na raadon na maakai
Raami asekha enro, maton sawnro.
Khristen raami sero, raa rawthaw puirang
Maton na ruse Jisukha en tana.

2. Jisu riming khantu raaikho awm tanveek
Makhan khristen haina raa mawtawr ngir rang
Nilawm na he khana meeikom kha cheem rang
Sok puihai lawm uro he rasang tak ah.

3. Araat raami ang ah Patheen Beekrup hai
Reetheeng hai sena khan keeini awm nalawn.
Keeini resen makme khat te-ah na-awm
Mulung awm richi awm khat te nanijoi.

4. Rengchangna le lukhum kha athang theei ni
Jisu Beekrup tu net tak ah awm jawn jai
Beekrup ranak kha meeikom na mawtawr mak
Khrista chawng rakhamna kha thang lai naw nih.

5. Maton sawn na lutro ningaina munkhan
Raa mawtawr na lah thawkhan he ruchun joi
Reng khrista deeng richit rajak na perawi
Ravantir le lah thaw bang lai naw mini
