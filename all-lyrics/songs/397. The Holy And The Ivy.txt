397. The Holy And The Ivy
Key F.

1. Thinbo ring le ruijam
    Hawngleen hawngchang pha khan
    Mo le lawn na awm thing kholok na
    Reng lukhum ang ah paar.

2. Zingkar nii-engseei pha
    Saaki hai ningai uh
    Organ radai miri hoi khe le
     Choir na lah kha thaw uh.

 3. Reeipar na thingkung ah
     Tui ab Lily na paar
     Mary na-awm Jisu kha ru neei
     Keeini nasaan rang khan.

  4. Maanrawp thing mawng narang
      Munkha tawm pe, siin pe
      Jisu awm sok narang mun awm mak
      Seh inn na niboi ah.

  5. Thingkung ah reeipar rui
      Rajam, rumum, apaar
      Jisu na-awm' nu Mary atawr
      Chung ah bawk ah hawngleen.

   6. Thingra haikha hawngmin
       Araa kha ringna nih
       Jisu awm jongsok, a-athi mawtawr
       Ringna kha nepek joi.

   7. Riling neel thingkung ah
       Asen peng reeipar-paar
       Jisuna awm thiir riling rukhu

        Thisen sok keeini rang.
