135 Victory Through Grace             S.S. 673.

                 Rev. 6:2.
Key 'B/Flat.

1. Raa mawtawr joi, mawlawr ret rang
   Rengpa Ma jengna kha le
   Tak ah sawn haikha makai pejoi
   Raa rawthaw na kar tak khan
   Raat na le i-nawngkha enro
   Singveng ah rasam sojoi
   Lam kaipa riming ah ra-aai.
   Mawsawk na chawng ra-ngairo.

           Arat haina mawtawr nawnih

           Atan ratna awm khe naw nih.

           Tak ah sawm le adik numpok

           Khana mawtawr rang nijoi.

2. Raa mawtawr joi, mawtawr ret rang
   Relek Rengkha tumo ni?
   Raa mawtawr se ma rukaipa kha
   Lah hoi kha le a lawm uh
   Amakha nasaanpa Pumi
    Nakhekpa reetheeng Rengnih
    Kumsawt Arshi kha ni-uh
    'Ma awm nakhan vaar rang nih.

3. Raa mawtawr joi, mawtawr ret rang
    Rampo kha Jisu nang nih
    'Mani rengmun, raatna, thang rang nih
    Renglukhum awm cho rang nih
    'Ni-ah

         Inang lamkai khantu

    Adik kha dawng nirang nih
    Awm narang inn hoi tum rang nih
    Raa thaw kha mawtawr so le.
