444. He Did Not Die In Vain             R.S. 709.
Key E/Flat.

1. Pumikha Cross ah ruthat u
   Nikho kha azing sawna nih
   Nakhekpa hawngthoi malalim
   Mining ah ruzet ngam boi nih.

           Ahoi ningai chawngkha reei ro

           Chungchon sawna doi joi

           Ranaak neei pumi hawngthoi joi

           Maan neei tak ah ruthi.

2. Amana ringna jongchawi joi
   Ama numpok jarkhan naring
   Sawna 'put rachak nepek joi
    Mana maan neei tak ah ruthi.

3. Numpok le ringna jongchawi joi
   Jisuna chungteeng Rengchang joi
   Ningai na lam nokon pe-ah
   Mana maan neei tak ah ruthi.
