16. Victory In Jesus.

1. Nakhekpa lurtak ah
Jongchum narang risim lui kejet
Khadawr ah seet neei keei
Nakhek rang Calvary ah jongthi
'Ma thisen jonglong lai ah
Sawdoi na heso kejet le
Kumulung kawhawng mikir ah
Mawtawr na kumuk joi.
Mawtawr na Jisu deeng awm
Kumsawt ah nakhekpa
Natawk ah nawrawchawk
'Ma thisen mirit le
'Manumpok nakha kejet
Reetheeng nah thisen jarkhan
mawtawr na kumuk het joi
Mawtawr na kumuk joi.

2. Seet reetheet na le mimadam na
Ranak ineei kejet
'Mana mikchaw hai var mumuk
Kekhawr haikha madam
Pumi karatha nojong madam pero ti-ah kokoi
Jisuna jong ah mawtawr nakha
Keeideeng nepek joi.

3. Keeirang inn pui alur tak
Nirasam pe chawngkha kejet
Lamkha Rangkachak rok le kon
Khopui ahoi sung khan
Nakhek na risim lui
Ravantir haina lah thaw uh
    Chung khopui khin nikhat
    Mawtawrna lah thaw rang.
