394. We Three Kings Of Orient Are

1. Nisok teeng mitheeithum haina
Maanrawp khutchawi kha vak chawi ah
Mo le lawn kha vak rakaan na
Arshi kha vaak jui uh.

Oh, sindawngroi Mitheeithum kha
Arshi na lam vaak kai pe
Bethlehem vak mosok joi
Arshi vaar kha awm vak ding joi.

2. Mitheeithum haina pumideeng
Rengchungchon na Reng 'ti zet na
Jisu kha chubai vak bawk u,
Khutchawi vak pek ujoi.

3. Aring jawnjai Reng' ti zet na
Manmeesseh reeleei hawngchun joi
Ningai Reng mapak na khutchawi
Kip birra vaak pek uh.

4. Khutchawi beraw hi zet natu
Ringna phuk nang rawkhawm rang nih
Saw tak ah thisen sok rang nih
Thaan na rukhum rang nih.

5. Enro,' Mana hawngring ret ah
Pum repe ngam Reng Patheen nih
Halleluya... Halleluyah
Ravan reeleei rehem.
