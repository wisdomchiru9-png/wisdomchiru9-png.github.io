157. We Have A Story To Tell To The Nation
R.S. 636.
Key F

1. Phung rikip deeng reeirang risim neeneel
Khana mulung midik rang nih
Adik le ahol risim nih, ningheen na risim khanih
Rachak le avar risim.
Azingkha amavar rang nijoi
Makha nisun var nirang nih
Khrista raam reeleei ah jong rang nih
Numpok le avar Rengmun.

2. Phung rikip deeng thaw rang lah kha neeneei
Mulung mining midik rang nih
Athaboikha mawtawr rang nih, Chemjam ranak a-dek ngam
Chem jam ranak adek ngam.

3. Phung rikip deeng sem rang chawngtha neeneel
Chungteeng ah Pumi Rengchang nah
Khana Sapa nepek joi
'Ma numpokna numukoi (2)

4. Vanoideeng malar rang Nakhekpa awm
Lam khawk na ah alawn hairang
Manmeeseh ah miring rikip, Nupumideeng va rang nih
Pumi deeng vak kir rang nih.
