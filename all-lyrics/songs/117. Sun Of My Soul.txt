117. Sun Of My Soul            S.S. 302.

                  Lk.24:29.

Key F.

1. Kumulung nivaar nasaanpa
   Nang nedep le zaan awm naw ni
   Reemeei thoi le reemeei vaar joi
   Nangsok kimik ne the pero.

2. Hoitak ah zaan na kaja pha
   Hoitak ah kimik kawmawchawt
   Ahoi mulung mining khale
   Bangboi ah hoitak ah zarang.

3. Zingkarra put ah nitak sekhan
   Nang niboi ah ring ngam nawning
   Zaanpha khan awm nedep chak ro
   Nang awmboi ah zaa ngam nawning.

4. Kohoi pha ah nuru dep ro
    Manmeeseh lam raw naboi rang
    Nangnumpok na kumuk malim
    Ravan raam kama thang rang nih.
