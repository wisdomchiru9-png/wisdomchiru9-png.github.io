319. Coming             S.S. 186.

                James. 1:8

Key A/Flat.

1. Lawn rasaang ah Narungpana
    Jong narang rophong joi
    Enro, rangkachak meeichon 'na,
    Rakhat ah akhin joi.

            Ravan na put 'Mana

            vaarpeng ah jong rang nih.

            Jong narang 'Ma chawng rakham na

            phakha naijoi.

             kheksa limna Ma jong,

            narang ah lah kha thaw.

            Ching haikha vaar pengjoi, lawmrawi

             Ajong rang ah Rengkha.

 2. Riringboi Beekrup kha mawsawk
     Thoita na, Niirang ro.
     Moipa jong narangkhan naaijoi
     Tun hin Ama jongjoi.

 3. Zion Rengpa kha mapak urawi
     Rureei masa nijoi
     'Vaantir haile lah ah lawm ro
     Ningai mosok rang ro.
