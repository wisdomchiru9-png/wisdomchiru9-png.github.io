27. A Call For Reapers                    S.S.1086.

                 Matt. 9:37.

Key A/Flat.

1. Ramla ram nai ah loimun sip joi
   Sang haikha minso joi
   Phaijaw Mo-ah sangvui min haikha
    Nivar le rakap joi.

           A-aat rang jongtir ro Pumi

            Kininiik noloi pero

           Jongtir ro sangvui min rawkhawm rang

            A-aat pha re khe nih.

2. Jongtir rang ro zingkar hoi khan awm
    Nisun var hoi khan awm
    Nitak vaar eng na a-eh pha khan
    Rawkhawm rang jongtir ro.

3. Relen rang ro Sangvui choi so joi
    Tunhin rawkhawm rang ro
    Nitani inn teeng chawi rang ro
    Ningai kha nang tum rang nih.
