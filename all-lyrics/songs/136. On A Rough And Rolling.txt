136. On A Rough And Rolling                S.C.M. 15.
Key F.

1. Lah kha thawrang tuipui chung ah
    Rithi hawng reher pha khan
    Kha ang ringkho renro reetheei
    Kar khan awm lah hi thaw rawng.

2. Kho hawng melek kho hawng miri
    Tuipui hawnglet pha sekhan
    Lah kha thaw rawng kha-ang sawna
    Rana hawngsok pha sekhan.

 3. Chungteeng rithi hawng reher ra
    Chiroi tuipui hawng let pha
     Kha ang renro reetheei kar khan
     Keeina lah hi thaw rang rawng.

 4. Maton sawn rang inn rojaw rang
     Tuipui hawnglet chung se khan
     Lah hi thaw rawng kha-ang sawna
     Rana hawng sok pha sekhan.
