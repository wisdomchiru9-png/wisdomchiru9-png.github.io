 146. Let The Blessed Saviour In                   R.S. 188.
Key E/Flat.

1.. Ra-ngairo Nakhekpa mawkawt ah ding
    Mulut ro, mulut ro
    Mawkawt ataw ah na-ngak pha sawt joi
    Mulutro, mulut ro.

            Nakhekpa mawkawt kung ah ding joi

            Phaasawt tak ah ding ah na-ngak joi

            Mulung mawkawt mawlawngro

            Nakhekpa lut narang khan

            Mulutro, mulut ro.

2. Nangkha nimirit tak nang lawm dit nih
    Mulutro, mulut ro
    Pumina narachangpui chak rang nih
    Mulutro, mulutro.

3. 'Ma kha nuk teeng mikir ret lai noro
    Mulutro, mulut ro
    Pumikha vak kir boiret rang awm jawt
    Mulutro, mulut ro.

4. He renem tak ah nokoi ra-ngairo
    Mulutro, mulut ro
   Amakha ningai lam ah rujawng ro
    Mulut ro, mulut ro.
