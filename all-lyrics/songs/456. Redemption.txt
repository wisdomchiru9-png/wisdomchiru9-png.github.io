   456. Redemption            S.S. 16.

                    Eph. 1:7.

  Key E

  1. Sindawngroi i-nakhek na risim
     Ningai chawng kha nang le keeirang
     Jisuna keeini nawrawchak joi
     Khan niton nuthung pe sojoi.

            Tak ah sun uro...munu phung hai

            Adik ningai na chawng loiro

            Nakhekpa Jisu kha ring rep ro

            Nangdeeng nepek joi ringna kha.

 2. Thitna 'put ringna teeng nawchawi joi
    Numpok Patheen nai namachang
    Munuphung rang ringna tui molong
    Thisen na neereetheeng masak.

3. Munu ah put a-sawt awm noro
   Lemna sawna raana karkhan
   Numpok Nakhekpa khrista jarra
   Munu ranaak meecheem rang nih.

4. Patheen numpok nakha tun loiro
   Oh Jisu deeng hawng relen ro
   'Ma deeng ah hawngkha nuru loi joi
   'Mana nimi kir lai nawnih.
