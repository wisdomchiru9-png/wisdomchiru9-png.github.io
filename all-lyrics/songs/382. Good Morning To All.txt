382. Good Morning To All

1. Alui awmchan mathan tana, Athar ringkho nirang ro
Mulungthar kha le ningai ro
Kumthar zingkar hoi hi le
Kumthaar nikho zingkar hoi
Nisok engseei hawngsok joi
Kumthar ningai zetna lawm rawi
Nikhothar hi rujawng rawi.

2. Daarkha suro kumthar nijoi
   Ningai kha rehem roso
   Nawhawng rawtawp pui kumlui kha
   Tunhin neresen pui joi.

3. Kumthar hile pumi lawm rawi
   Nang mulung mathar rang ro
   Nang idit kha maan narang khan
   Kumthaar le pumi lawmro.
