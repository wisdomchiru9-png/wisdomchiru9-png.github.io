68. The Prize Is Set Before Us    R.S. 365.
Key G/Flat

1. Nilaw pek rang rasamjoi, 'Ma ra-ngaidam muk rang
   Rasang ah Patheen mik en nuchung a-awm
   'Ma he renem le nokoi munu-ah kaw-awm laikhan
   Jisuna renem tak ah nokoi lai.

           Keeinina rumuk pui rang

           Dawdawn na lam rujawng rang         2

           Jisu le Rengchang ah awm rang

           Dawdawn nah

                                           }

2. 'Ma lamkai na jui ah, keeini raamhoi khan awm rang
    Keeinirang neereeipe, 'Male awm chak rang
    'Male resen boi rang ra-ngam nakha hawngleen rangnih
    'Male resen lai nawning.

3. Chungteeng ni-inn kha var,, azingkha awm lai nawni
    Chungteeng Jisuna keeinihi numnopok
    Chungchon anujui changa keeini mapak jawn jai rang
    'Ma miring haikha thilai naw nih.
