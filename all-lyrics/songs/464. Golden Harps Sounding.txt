464. Golden Harps Sounding S.S. 87.
Acts. 5:31.
Key A/Flat

1. Ravantir Rangkachak tingtang mirijoi
Rengpa lut narang rapa kawt mawlawng joi
Alur Rengpa Khrista numpok Reng Jisu Ro
Raa-mawtawr ah Rengsut munteeng ruhawng joi.
Chon kholok mawtawr ah
Ningai lah thaw rawi
Ningai rang rawi alur
Reng Jisu kajoi.

2. Keeini nakhek rang jong ah nithit pe
Tun 'Pa deeng kung ah Reng lukhum rukhu joi
Sawdoi ret lai nawnih, thiret lai naw nih
Alur Rengpa Jisu ravanteeng hawng joi.

3. Ama awmna munkhan niik pe naihai rang
Ningai rangkhan nokoi, numpok nangni rang
Inn vaar khat nokon pe naipang nang nirang
Bangtik neeiboi ah Jisu ringjawn jai joi.
