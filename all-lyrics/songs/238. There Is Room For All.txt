238. There Is Room For All             S.S.820.

                Lk. 14:22.

Key Flat.

1. Athang ratha vak san ro
   Achai hai vak tawk ro
   Doi rat ah lam kai pero
   Lam re-ep na munkhan
   Numpok tak ah chawiro
   Khrista ikoi nakhan
   Bu rasam joi mezet ro
   Mikholok rang mun awm.

          Munkha awm vanoi rang

           Bu rasam joi mezet ro

          Mikholok rang mun awm.

2. Sawna rana Arshi awm naboi khur ah put khan
   Rangkachak nivar munkhan
   Avar rachak kha awm
   Jisu deeng hawng ruchun ro 'Mana nokoi reret
   Bu rasam joi mezet ro
   Mikholok rang mun awm.

3. Numpok na mulung neei ah 'Ma riming ah sero
   Kumsawt ah ring na chawng hi
    Reeleei ah vak sem ro
    Relen ro Ni awm lai hin, Hawng rilip mala hin
    Bu rasam joi mezet ro
    Mikholok rang mun awm.
