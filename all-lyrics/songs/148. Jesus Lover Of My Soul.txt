148. Jesus Lover of My Soul             S.S. 227.

                  Ps. 9:9.

Key F.

1. Ratha num apo Jisu, nangdeeng kung ah natet ro
   Tui reher na nedep awm, Rithi khopui rang a-awm
   Nethe ro Oh, Nasaanpa, Ringkho sawna hek malim
    Ravan sekhan nawchawi ro, Karatha noloi pero.

2. Rethe narang neei mi-ing, karatha nangdeeng katet
   Kibing ah natet noro, Nurungro naw-awm pui ro
   Kiring narang nangdeeng awm, Pumi nang narachang pui.
   Nasaanro nuhup chak ro, Pumi nang matha thoi khan.

3. Kidit haihi Oh, Khrista, nangniboi ah munaw ning
   Apur haikha nang mothoi, ana haikha nang madam
   Kekeek mikchaw nang moroi, Reetheeng riming chung chon
    nih.
   Adik boi kha keei kani, Adik le numpok nang nih.
4 Numpok kip bir kha nang neei, Keeseetna napai pero.
    Ringle dam put na nangnih, reetheeng ah keei natet ro.
    Ringna tuinar kha nangni, manboi ah nemenek ro
   Keeideeng hawngput roso tui
    Hektik neeiboi ring narang.
