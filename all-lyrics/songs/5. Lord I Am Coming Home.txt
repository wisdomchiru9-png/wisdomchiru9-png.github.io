5. Lord I Am Coming Home                S.S. 471

                 Matt. 11:28.

Key A/Flat.

1. Patheen le rala-a ru awm
   Tun keei kahawng joi
    Munu lam karawna sawt joi,
    Pumi kahawng joi.

            Kahawng joi.....kahawng joi

           Chairet lai nawning,

           Numpok a nangta chang rawng,

            Pumi kahawng joi.

2. Manrawp kumsawt kuru mathang,
    Pumi kahawng joi,
    Sirna mik rithi cho lamma,
    Pumi kahawng joi.

3. Munu a chai hi kasawt joi
    Tun keei kahawng joi.
    Numpok chawng taka sawn rang rawng,
    Innteeng kahawng joi.

4. Karatha kumulung koijoi
    Tun keei kahawng joi.
    Ranak nojong mathar peroh
    Pumi kahawng joi
