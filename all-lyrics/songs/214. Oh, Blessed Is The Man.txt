214. Oh Blessed Is The Man              3rd Alex. 375.

                    Ps. 32.

 Key A.

 1. Maanboi pet ah ra-ngaidam tumpa kha raavantha nih
    Thawlawmboi iruthaw munu haikha hum pesojoi.

 2. Munu aput patheen i-saanpa kha raavantha nih
     Taksa leen rasaat na neeiboi haikha makhan awm uh.

  3. Pumi deeng kiruthaw athaboi kholok rophong rang
     Munu kholok kha tak thar ah nara ngaidam ret ro.

     4. Mulung lek hairang khan 'Mana ninik perang nijoi
      Khapha khan Amakha nang tawk le nang muk rang nijoi.
