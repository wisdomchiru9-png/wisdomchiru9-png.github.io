511. Keep Step With The Master S.S. 1092 IR
Matt. 8:19.
Key D/Flat.

1. Mun rikip ah Pumi le rawtawp chak ro
Lamkha 'zing a-awm, Pumi kha dep chak ro
Narahapui haina navak nawng a-awm
Jisu le nang lawn pha khan chirang awm mak.

Chiboi pet ah maton sawn ro
Ranaak thar loi reret ro
Ningkip Khrista le lawnchak ro
'Mana lam nakai pe chak rang nih.

2. Nangse na rikip ah Pumi le lawnro
Zing na khan 'Mana lam numumuk rang nih
'Ma avaar na nanglam rakhar lai nawnih
Ram mun rikip kha mavaar peng rang nijoi.

3. Pumile rawtawp ro ra-nga lai noro
'Ma ireei limkha relen tak ah jui ro
Thoi tana Ama ranaak khan awm rang ro
Jisu le nang lawn phakhan nang dik rang nih.
