207. Oh Happy Day              S.S. 866.

                 Prov. 16:20.

Key G.

1. Ahoi nikho kamadan joi
    Nakhekpa Patheen deeng kungkhan
    Numulung ningai rang nijoi
    Ningai risim hoikha reeirang.

            Ningai na nikho hoi

            Jisuna munu rusuk joi

            'Amana nuruchuk sojoi

             Ningkip ningai ah ring narang

             Ningai na nikho hoi

             Jisuna munu rusuk joi.

 2. Aleen chon nawmawtawr pejoi
     Pumi kata keei Amata
     Keeina Ama anu kujui
     Keeseetna korophong sujoi.

 3. Kumulung saw ah ru-awn kha
     Saw kumuchum joi Jisu deeng
     Kupumi le resen boi ah
     Atha kholok kumuk sujoi.

 4. Pumi deeng chawng kira kham nih
      Ravan sekhan rujet rang nih
      Kiring lailim chubai bawk ah
      Kithit sekhan Ravan mukrang,
