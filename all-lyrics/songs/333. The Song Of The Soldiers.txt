333. The Song Of The Soldiers S.S. 1174.
II. Tim. 2:3.
Key 'B/Flat.

1. Nangni kheksa naihai thoiro
Khrista le a-awm haikha
Thoirangro ranaak neei phun hai
Zion mina loimala.
Mawtawr na lah kha thaw uro
ang
Tuipui miri kha ah
Inakhek na Khrista thile
Raa mawtawr mihai nek ah
chungchon det kha nani joi (2)
Khrista thisen ranak jarra
Chungchon detkha nanijoi.

2. Mereetheeng araat hai khana
'Raa don na net tak ah ding
Raa mawphawk haina nokoi joi
Thisen jarra mawtawr rang.

3. Thit le chitna kha neeiboi ah
Khrista na ringna nepek
Pumi jonglar na zingkar kha
Ningai alur kha muk rang.

4. 'Mamai kung ah ding rang nanih
Alur Pumi mukrang nanih
Ningai ah 'Ma chubai bawk rang
'Ma tum numuk veek rang nih.
