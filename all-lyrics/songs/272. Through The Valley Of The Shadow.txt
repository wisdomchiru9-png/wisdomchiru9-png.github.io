272. Through The Valley Of The Shadow
S.S. 1038.
Ps. 23:4.
Key G/Flat

1. Phaijaw rilip khan alawn ngir rang kani
Nasaanpa numpok na thoikhan awm ngir rang
Lekse namathan nawning ti-ah neereei
Mana keehi nomoroi rangni.
Phaijaw rilip.... Phaijaw rilip
Khajui ah keei ase ngir rang kani joi
Jordon tui let khana nawmawsaw naw nih
Makhan rachak awm iti kejet.

2. Phaijaw rilip khan keei kawlawn pha roi khan
Sawna rana kholok kha awm lai nawni
Jisu ban ranak khana nawpawm rang nih
Ama deeng sawna neeilai nawning.

3. Phaijaw rilip ah keei kawlawn pha roi khan
Ningai niso hoi kha muk ngir rang kanih.
Jordon vara teeng lah hoi kha thawsom rang
Lawmdit hai ina-ngak na mun khan.

4. Phaijaw rilip khan keei lawn ngir rang kani
Kupumi rusena kha juirang kanih
Phaijaw khan 'Mana lam nakai perang nih
kawmchang ah saw muchum mala lim.
