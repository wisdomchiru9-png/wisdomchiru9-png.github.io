421. The Angel's Songs.S. 28.
LK. 2:14.
Key A/Flat

1. Zanchim laikhan he jong rehem
Ravan na lah hoi khanan jonghem
Chungchon na hoiningai lah kha
Ravantir hai he hoile i-awi nih.
Chungchon na rasang niroso
Reeleei chungah rachak awm roso
Rong rong ah he rehem roso

3. Patheen naihai na thaai anawt
Niton kholok athungrang jong joi
