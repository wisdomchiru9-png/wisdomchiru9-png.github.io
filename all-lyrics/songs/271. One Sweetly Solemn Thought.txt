271. One Sweetly Solemn Thought S.S.967.
Heb 11:16.
Key B/Flat.

1. Ahoi mulung mining
Keeideeng jong awm jawn jai
Von hin kawhawng nai det joi
Athar bak ki-inn khan.
Ki-inn nai joi-(2)
Kuru awm na boipet inn kha
Von kawhawng nai det joi.

2. Kapa inn kha naijoi
Ravan inn hoi munkha
Avar achek Rengsut munkha
Von hin kawhawng nai joi.

3. Ringkho dawngkha naijoi
Pokrik muchum narang
Arik Cross mathan rang naijoi
Reng lukhum man narang.

4. Keeina kupur phakhan
Nojongdep ro Pumi
Khajar khan inn kawhawng naijoi
Tun kawhawng nai det joi.
