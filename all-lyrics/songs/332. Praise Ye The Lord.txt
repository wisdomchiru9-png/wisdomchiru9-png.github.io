 332. Praise ye The Lord            S.S. 215.

                   Ps. 101:2.

 Key B/Flat.

 1. Pumikha mapakro hosana ti-ah
    Ningai lah le mapak ro
    'Marengmun khan ningai mulung le lawnro
    Riming reetheeng meeleen ro
    Aman rawp reetheeng hai khale lawm chak ro
    Numpok nakha tak ah sawn rep ro
    Ama reetheeng var khana na-eh rang nih
    Pha rikip ah 'Ma khutna nomoroi chak rang nih.

           Jam ti-ah lawn rangro nang ponlap kha melek tana

           Rachak reei ah sero, meeichaw, reemeei na-awm

           Namaton chungteeng ah ahoi ningaina inn sekhan se

           utana

           Pumikha mapak ro ne Rengpa khanih.

2. Pumikha lawmro, 'Ma numpokna reei ro
   Chungteeng lah thaw sap ro
   Sero sero ningai lam ah lawn rangro
   Pumi leen nakhan sero
   ningai ah saw muchumna inn hoi khan
   Ruchun som rang nani inn hoikhan
   Nakhekpa riming lawm rawi kha munhoi khan
   Kumsawt ah Nakhekpa rimingkha leenrang nijoi.
