449. There Is Life For A Look S.S. 123,

                Isaiah 14:22.

Key G.

1. Cross ah khaikha nang en-le nang ring rang nih
    Tun tak hin nangrang ringna kha awm
    Seetneei hai' Madeeng enro nasaan rang ni
    Cross ah' thikha khrista kha nih.

            Enro, ringna rang

            Ithaat kha a-en hairang ringna kha awm

            Tuntak hin nangrang ringna kha awm.

2. Oh, mununeei angah sawna 'doi moni?
     Seetna Jisu deeng nangpek malim
     Ijarra reetheengna thisen kha long moni?
    'Ma athina nangphuk thung mak maw?

 3. Mulung sir na mik rithi jarra nimak
     Ratha khekna thisen jarra nih
     Asok thisen khana reetheeng masak joi
     Lungsaw pokrik kholok chawi sojoi.

 4. Patheen irophong kha rakhana noro
     Khaniboi thawrang chonsik awm mak
     Dawng nikhan voikhat jong malar rang nih
     Iruthaw chonkha mawtawr sojoi.

 5. Tuntak Jisu kha ningai le loirang ro
     Hektik neeiboi ringna kha nepek
     Thiret lai nawni iti jet rep ro
     Adik Jisu kha nangrang ringjoi.
