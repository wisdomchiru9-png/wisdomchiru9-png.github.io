292. Gather The Reaper Home S.S. 755.
Rev. 14:15.

1. Rangkachak ram ah lah thaw nang zet maw?
Ningai na lah thar hoi khaust mar
Ningai lam ah sangvui kha khop rang ro
Apha hi sawt lai nawni.
Par pumikha jong relen rang nijoi
Ama meh maitai numuk rang nih
Par pumikha jong relen rang nijoi
Aat hai rawkhawm rang nih.

2. Numpok mirit hai khana rangkachak
Ram ah put nojong en-uh
Chon kha thaw u Cross kha phung uh
Reng lukhum kha tum ujoi.

3. Rangkachak ramkhan ningai lah rehem
Mulung ngar na neeneei joi
Ee-awi na khana ratha menem joi.
Misawna kholok menem joi.

4. Rangkachak ram khan ningai lah rehem
Ningai na phaijaw munkhan
Aring tak ah sawn khana nemezet
Mahi nata ranga nih.
