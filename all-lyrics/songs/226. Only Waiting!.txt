226. Only Waiting!     S.S. 1029.

                  1.Cor. 1:7.
Key G.

1. Rilip pha tekha ngak chak ah
   Nga-ngai ah asin rang maw!
   Ni-eng re tekha ngak chak ah
   Nii-ke dawngkhit kha lut joi
Reeleei ah zanpha mawtawr se
Mulung damveng nikho kha
Ravan arshi malang malim
Rachaak reei avaar kha nih.

2. Rawkhawm rang mite ngakchak ah
Sangvui kha inn na mulut
Kholum phakha hawng mawtawr ah
Phabi rithi hawngrang joi
Rawkhawm rangro relen tak ah
Kumulung kakham pui joi
Ringkho hoinapha hawng hek joi
'Se narang kawraw nawt joi.

3. Vaantir hai tekha ngak chak ah
Kawtpui leen kha mawlawng joi
Tu 'kawt ah rataang mo kani?
Sara saw keereeveei joi
Tun awm kesun miri kejet
'Mani he tu laamet joi
Nokoi nimaw ti-ah ka-ngak
Reei jui rang khan ka-ngak chak.

4. Ningai awm narang kha ka-ngak
Lek se mu-ngam mi-ing lai
Ringna thingkung mawng na mun khan
Raam rubuk en awm nakhan
Nimijir nahet rang ka-ngak
'Mana noloi ret pha khan
Sok ah natet munu kholok
'Saw naat na awm lai nawnih.
