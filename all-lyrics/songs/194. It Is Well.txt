    Sawnakha tuilet ang phakhan
    Kopokrik nang inisit pekha madang
    Athajoi karatha ning heen joi.

2. Athaboi ruhu hina nawmawsaw ah-awm
    Numpok chawng hina khop roso
    Jisuna nasaan rangboi nezet peh
    Ama thisen kha nomolong peh.

3. Kumunu kejet boijarra kiningai
    Kumunu kholok sunbirra
    'Ma Cross khan achijoi, keei phunglai nawning
    Pumikha mapak ro karatha.

4. Kiring narang Khristana nithit pejoi
   Jordon tuipui na nuhup awm
   Kithit kiring awm sawna neei lai naw ning
   Karatha deeng chawng kha nang reei joi.

5. Pumi nang jong retrang khan kana ngak uh
   Kidin utu Ravan ram nih
   Ravantir le Pumi raa mawtawr he kha
   Ratha saw muchum narang ah nih.
