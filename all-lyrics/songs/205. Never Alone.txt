205. Never Alone         R.S. 325.
Key C.

1. Chinoro na awm som, Rangkachak var leh
   Avar Arshi ang ah kalam mavar ro
   janchim reemeei zing se, chawngpek kha var joi
   Keeina nangtheen te-ah namathan lak lai naw ning.

           Namathan nawning (2)

           Nangtheen na namathan nawning      2

           ti-ah chawng narakham pui

2. Keedeeng ah par reeipar Lily awm choijoi
    Reeleei nivaar mawk awm, Ravantu var chak
   Jisu Sharon reeipar tu ratang jawn jai
    Ravan nivar Jisu nanamathan lai nawnih.

3. Kawlawn nakhan thit narang ru-awm ah-awm
    Nakhekpa na keeihi nara naipui chak
    Kholumvahai ang ah kumulung mapar
    Raw-awi tak ah lah thaw, namathan lak lai nawnih.
