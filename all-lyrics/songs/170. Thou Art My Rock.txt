170. Thou Art My Rock            S.S. 525.

                  Ps. 31:2.

Key B/Hat.

1. Lungpum kha nangni, numpok Nakhekpa
   Kerethe narang mukha nangnih
   Lungpum kha nangni ring le dam na mun
   Hoitak ah nang deengkhan awm chak rang.

           Lungpum nangnih, numpok ah Nakhekpa

           Kawlawm dit le lam nakai pepa

           Kara ngam nale Nakhekpa nang ni

           Tak ah sawn na nang deeng rethe rang

2. Lungpum kha nangni, setna nelem pha
   Lungpum kha nangni, chawngsaat pha-ah
   Lungpum kha nangni, kawsaw pha roi ah
   Lungpum kha nangni, imo chining.

3. Lungpum kha nangni, Lemna hawngsok pha
    Rothoboi adik kawlawm nangnih
    karatha hi nangdeeng kerepe joi
    Retheeng tak ah sawn kha nichak rawng.

4. Lungpum kha nangni, Rengmun le phung ah
    Chawngsat neei haile Renghai thang so pha-ah
    Ringna lungpum nangtu nangring rang nih
    Kumsawt lungpum tak ah kawnawsawn.
