392. Lord Dismiss Us S.S. 287.
Eph. 1:3.

1. Pumi raavan nepe uroh
Numpok ningai le rachak
Mulung sung ah jongsip roso
Nang numpok maan som urawng
Ranak thar nojong pe-u ro
Ramthim ah ka-awm lai hin.

2. Chubai bawk ah kanamapak
Nangchawng a-hoi risim kha
Ringkho mo ning-ngai a-hoi kha
Kumulung ah awm chak joi
Ra-ngam napui miring chang rawng
Pumi le rumuk narang.

3. Pumina reeleei mathan nah
Zetna nepek pha roi khan
Nokoi he kejet le hawng rawng
Ravan tir hai kha ang-ah
Hektik neeiboi Ravan ram ah
Khrista le ring jawn jai rang.
