142. Calling The Prodigal R.S. 125.
Key B/Flat.

1. Patheen na achai haikha nokoi hawngsom noro
Rangai ro tunhin' Mana nangkha nokoi
'Madeeng aput latak ah nangchai kha tun hawngro
Tunse he raw-awi ah nokoi.
Nangkha nokoi lai
Achai, asaw hai hawngro
Nangkha nokoilai
Renro reveei doi hawngro.

2. Napana numpok doirat le renem ah nokoi
Ra-ngai ro tunhin Mana nangkha nokoi
Rathana nangkha naw maw kawi lai hawngkir rangro
Tunse he raw-awi ah nokoi.

3. Napa Inn na bumicin rang rasam joi hawngro
Ra-ngairo tunhin' Mana nangkha nokoi
Table chungah bu nehai rang khana nuru ngak
Tunse he raw-awi le nokoi.
