14. Open Wide The Door             S.S. 358.

                 Rev. 3:20.

Key D/Flat.

1. Jisu na nangkha nokoi
   Asaw hai keeideeng hawngro
   Ama tena nakhek ngam
   Mawkawt mawlawng roh.

           Mawkawt mawlawng ro (2)

           Ama tena nasaan ngam

           Mawkawt mawlawng roh.

2. Jisu nasaan rang jongjoi
    Nakhek rangkhan jongthi joi
    Thaan kha 'mana mawtawr joi
    Mawkawt mawlawng ro.

3. Jisu na mawkawt ataw
    Tuntak hin' madeeng lut ro
    Relen na nang lut rang ro
    Ningai sip rang nih.

 4. Jisuna mawkawt ataw
    Ringkhomo rakan narang
    Jisu na kachawi mala
     Mawkawt mawlawng ro
