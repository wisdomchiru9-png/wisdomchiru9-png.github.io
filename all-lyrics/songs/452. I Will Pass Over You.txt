452. I Will Pass Over You             S.S. 144.
Key C/Flat.

1. Nasaanpa khrista Cross ah ruthi
    Munu leeiphuk kha nuru thung pe
    Nang ratha kha ke-ngam thi nawt ro
    Nangkha narakan pui rang nijoi.

            Keeina ke-ngamte

            Thikha kumuk le

            Thisen longkhana

             Keeihi narakan pui rang nijoi.

 2. Munu pok a rik kha nasaan joi
     Chawng rakham na neei ah Nakhek joi
     Tuipui longkhan munu rusuk ro
      Keeina narakan pui rang kani.

  3. Chawng rese na munkhan ding som rang
      Atha chon nithaw tum maan som rang
      Nurusuk ngam thisen khan lut ro
      Keei narakan pui rang kani joi.

  4. Numpok miritkhan ravaat awm mak
      Adik tak ah sawn miring hai rang.
      Thisen khan saw hawng muchum rang ro
      Keeina narakan pui rang kanih.
