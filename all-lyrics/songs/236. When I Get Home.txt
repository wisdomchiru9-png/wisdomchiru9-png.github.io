236. When I Get Home
Key C/Fat.

1. Ki-inn kosok pha khan Rangkachak Lukhum
Kha rukhu ah kopok arik muchum rang
Alur pon le deer kha thaw ah inn kosok le
Nakhekpa risim kha lah a thaww rang.
Ravan inn hoi kosok pha khan
Sawna rana Kholok kha awm lai naw nih
Ki-inn hoi kha kosok pha khan
Khamun khan mulung saw na awm lai naw nih.

2. Inn kosok pha khan azing awm lai naw nih
Avar kha muk rang, Ki-inn kosok phakhan
Ravan a-var khana kalam mavar rang nih
Inn sok malim ma nakai pe rang nih.

3. Inn kosok le nakhekpa maikha muk rang
Inakhek nakha lah ah thaw rang kanih
'Ma-maikung ah ding ah ningai ah mapak rang
'Male ring jawn jai ah awm rang kani.
