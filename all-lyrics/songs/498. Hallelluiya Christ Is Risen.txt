498. Hallelluiya Christ Is Risen            S.S. 153.

                  1.Pet. 1:3.
Key C.

1. Halleluyah.....Halleluyah
   Ravan se he mosok ah
   Mapakna lah kha thaw rang ro
   Ningaina lah thaw rangro
   Manmeeseh hai ringnarang Cross ah 'Mana nithit Pejoi
   Thisen rumosok pa khani
   Athi ah put hawngring joi.

2. Akhat changna khrista ah-ra,
   Athi aput hawngringjoi
   Sip rang ni reetheeng a-raa kha
   'Ma lurna ningai nakhan
   Rangkachak sangvui par haikha
    Ma maikung ah hawngbawm rang
   Thankhurw thuka ah put khan awm
   Nisa na mimin rang nih.

3. Halleluyah....Halleluyah
   Patheen kha lurchak roso
   Halleluya.. Nakhekpa deeng
   Raa mawtawr na kha muk joi
   Halleluyah ratha ah chawng
    Reeinasom awm rang roso
    Halleluyah Pumi thum ruchun
    Ravantha niroso.
