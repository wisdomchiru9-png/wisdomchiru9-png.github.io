71. The Fight Is On 3rd Alex. 229.
Key C/Flat

1. Ra rawthaw joi ra khutchawi rasam ro tina
Sumlawi miri needeeng ah nejet joi
Pumina mawtawr narang namaton na ruse joi
Khrista sumlawi tum rang nijoi.

Raa sok joi Khristen ra miring
Mai re-en chak ah rawthaw pui ro
Ra pon reeseei ah nanglar mosok ro
Adik, dik boi von madang rang
Ra rawthaw puihin saw noro
Rat ro Ma ranak kha mukui ro
Patheen khana maton na awm matu
Mawtawr na lah thawrang nanih.

2. Ra rawthaw joi, thoiro arat adik rami
Jehovah na ra mawtawr ngir rang nih
Rapon inepek haikha nawt tak ah reeseei rang ro
Mawtawr nase khan doi rang rawi.

3. Nupumina mawlawr narang mana kaipe
Nisok nateeng ah chawng rakham nakha
Ama riming ajengkha lawm tana mawtawr rang ro.
Arachak zingkar hawngsok joi.
