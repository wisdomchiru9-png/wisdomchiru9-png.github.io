367. What A Friend We Have In Jesus
S.S. 319.
Ps. 18:24.
Key F.

1. Nawlawmdit Jisu kha neeneei
Sawna munu a-phungrang
Ituk chanthat na nineei mo?
Patheen deengkhan niichak rawi.
I tuk dawrra hoimo nanih?
Niphung munu ariik hi
Keeinina phung lai naw mini
Patheen deengkhan niichak rawi.

2. Lemna khana maw nawmaw kawi
Sawna rana maw neeneei?
Numulung mawdawk lai nawrawi
Rutuk thep lawm mumini maw?
Tunamo nawsaw pui ni?
Nawdawk na Jisu na zet veek
Patheen deeng khan niichak rawi.

3. Munu pokrik nuphung lai maw?
Ariik kha nang doi lai maw?
Nibeeirang Pumi Jisu awm
Patheen deeng khan niichak rawi
Nanglawm haina namathan awm
Nupumi deengkhan niirawi
Ama khut na nurung rang nih
A-hoi kha manrang nanih.
