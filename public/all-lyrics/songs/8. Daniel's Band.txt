1. Adik mining khan ding roh,
    Nang maton sawn roh,

            Daniel anga ngar roh,

            Net tak a ding roh.

            Adik mining khan ngar roh

2. Arat haikha pur sojoi
    Doi ngam boi jarra,
    Patheen na narachangpui
    Daniel hawi kha anga.

3. Mirat mileen hai tampui,
    Kha ram khan'va uh.
    Arat haikha pur sojoi
    Daniel hawi maikunga.

4. Chawng tha kha rak rep rang roh,
    Raa mawlawr narang.
    Seitan na rawthaw pui joi.
    Hawkro Daniel hawi rang.
