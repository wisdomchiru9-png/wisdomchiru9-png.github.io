28. God Is Calling Yet                       S.S. 139.

                   Jer. 22:29

Key A/Flat.

1. Patheen inokoi zet boi ah
    Manmeeseh hoina dit rang maw?

Ringkho relen tak ah sejoi
Karatha aja mae nilai?
Nokoilai ra-ngai ro
Nokoilai he ra-ngai ro
Patheen na nokoilai
Ama he ra-ngai ro
Patheen na nokoi lai
Tun awm nokoilai
'Ma he ra-ngai ro.

2. Nokoilai athoi boi rang maw?
Numpok he kha ra-ngai boi ah
Keeina tunse /Ma ten ret na
Kimasangboi hi sum ning maw?

3. Patheen na natawk ah nokoi
Kumulung kikhop maw nilai?
Amanat unse na-ngak lai
Ra-ngai boi pet rang maw kani?

4. Patheen na nokoi jawt rang ro
Roko sung ah awm lai rang maw?
Tun awm napai mak na-ngak lai
Kumulung hawng mothoi rang ro.

5. Nokoilai ra-nga lai noro
Relen tak ah mulung lek ah
Munu kha keresen pui joi
Kumulung ah Patheen awm joi.
