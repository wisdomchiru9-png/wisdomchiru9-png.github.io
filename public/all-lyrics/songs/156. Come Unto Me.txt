156. Come Unto Me             S.S. 374.0

                 Matt. 9:28.     8211 BM

Key G/Flat.

1. Nakhekpana saw reeveei haikha nokoi
   Pokrik phung ah sawdoi haikha hawngro ti
   Hawngsumu noro sawmuchum narang khan
    Pokrik nang ineei rikip kha hawng chawiro.

           Keeideeng hawngro

           Saw hawng muchum ro

           Nepek urang kanih, kopokajangkha

           Kawchawng ro-ngai uro, nun renem kanih

           Kopok tu ajang ni, sawmuchum na nih.

2. Mulung ning niboi ah achai maw nang nih?
    Mulung thangta khana nangpok rik maw ni?
    Nang mulung saw reeveei le chit nahai kha
    Jisu deeng hawngro, hoina nepek rang nih.

3. Azing reemeei khana inubuk maw nih?
    Seetna meeikhur sung ah sawdoi ah awm hai
    Relen tak ah hawngro renro reeveei hai
    Pumina saw muchum na nepekrang nih.

4. Lemna kha idawr mo nang hawng mawtawr joi
    Nangseet nakhana inawmawsaw moni?
    Reeiro Khristana neereetheeng masak ngam
    Reetheeng rathakha 'Mana nepek rang nih.
