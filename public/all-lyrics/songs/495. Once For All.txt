495. Once For All 3rd Alex. 166.
Eph. 2:8.
Key E/Flat

1. Serna put ningai kha muk narang khan
Jisuna thisen sok ah nakhek joi
Munu pumbot na munu sebe
Jisuna namadam sojoi.
Munuphung hai mahi loi rang ro
Rakhana neei mahi ringrep ro
Cross nangdep le keeini nakhek joi
Khristana keeini nakhek joi.

2. Cross lingkhan nangpok rik nuruphung pe
Nakhekpana riling lukhum rukhu
Munu sawna neeilai nawtini
Nangdeeng ra-ngaidam nepek joi.

3. Tuntu keeini cham neeikha nani joi
Jisuna ringna nokon pe sojoi
Hawngro ti-ah nokoi ra-ngai ro
'Mana keeini nakhek het joi.

4. Patheen naihai ningai ah nokoi lai
'Ma numpok nakhan pur lai naw mini
Thitna mak ah ringna teeng nokoi
Phortha le ringna nepek joi.
