234. Beulah Land          R.S. 389.
Key G.

1. Ningnit na ram hoi kasok joi
   Neei rikip awm kata chang joi
   Makhan var rinim se awm mak
   Avar dawk boi ah var jawn jai.

           Oh, ram ahoi... ahoi tak ram

           Lawn rasang chungah kading ah

           Tui vara teeng khong ka-nga le

           Kawmchang khan kecirang avar peng

           Munhoi rasam sa ru--awm joi.

           Kumsawt ah ki-inn Ravan ram.

2. Nakhekpa na nawratawp pui
   Rakan rep ah nawlawn puichak
   'Ma numpok khut le nomoroi
   Ravan ram sok narang se khan.

3. Manam hoi rithi rang na raam
   Thingkung hai awm ringna jawn jai
   Reeipar hai awm choi tik neeiboi
   Kumsawt ringna tuilong namun.

4. Khothang rithi na nawhwng sem
   Ravan ram he raw-awi khana
   Ponchek reeseei "Vantir haina
   Nakhek na lah hoi kha thaw uh.
