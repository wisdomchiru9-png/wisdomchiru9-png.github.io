129. Go Ye Into The Worlds              S.S. 1090.

                     Matt.28:20.

Key A/Flat.

1. Ram latak ah Khrista' ditboi hai awm uh
   Ratha tampui athang rangkhan awm joi
   Ringna chawngsem rangkhan tumo a-serang?
   Jisu riming ah saw doi ah sero.

           Ranaak kholok nepek veek joi (2)

           'Nangni sero mun rikip ah chawnghi

           vaksem ro naw-awm pui chak rang kanih."

2. Enro, kawt mawlawng ah mirit ah nokoi
   Khrista raami hai thoi tana lut ro
   Khristen hai thoiro ranaak rusuk vir ah
   Munu jam sat tana" Chawngtha semro

3. Patheen na nokoi " Thitna awm lai naw nih"
   Keeini' Ma riming khan thi naw mini
   Nithit narang khan Jisuna jongthi joi
   Khajarkhan ratha ringna chawng semro.

4. Phung rikip deeng Patheen na nawmawsawk joi
     Ma lurna, raa mawtawrna lah thawrang
    Kheksa hai ratha ringnakhan ningai ro
    Halleluyah Nupumi Reng changjoi.
