140. Praise Him Praise Him                 S.S. 208.

                   Ps. 146:2.

Key A/Flat.

1. Lawmro lawmro, Jisu keeini Nakhekpa nih
    Sindawngroi numpok lah le malar ro
    Ama lawmro Ravantir hai na'awm mapak
     Ma riming richit rajak na neei uh
     Ke-ngam ang ah 'Ma naihai kha rung rang nih
     Ama ban ranak khana nakaw chak.

             Lawmro, lawmro, relek' Ma leen na reeiro

             Mapak uro ningaina lah khale.

  2. Lawmro lawmro, Jisu keeini Nakhekpa nih
     Numunu jarra Mana nithit pe
      Ama kumsawt ni-ra-ngampui lungpum khanih
      Ama lawmro nathit pe Jisu nih
      Sawna nadoipe Jisu kha mapak ro
      Dawng awmboi numpok sindawngroi ah raat.

  3. Lawmro lawmro, Jisu keeini Nakhekpa nih
      Ravan na Hossana ti-ah mapak.
      Nakhekpa Jisuna kumsawt ah Reng changjoi
      Theempuleen Rengkha lukhum mukhu ro
      Ra mawtawr na khale khrista kha jongjoi
      Ningai lurna Pumi Jisu tanih.
