290. There Is A Pradise Of Rest
Isaiah. 14:3.
Key E/Flat.

1. Sawmuchum na munhoi khat awm
Sawna tui vara khan
Azing zan iti kha awm lai nawnih
Mik rithi ho rang nijoi.
Khamun khan nuru jawng rang nih.
Avar zingkar hawngsok na munkhan
Khamun khan nuru jawng ro
Tui vara kawmchang teeng khan nuru jawng ro

2. Avar ningai khopui khat awm
Meeleei na reeitheei mak
Khana lut lim na Rengkha muk rang nih
Ama le awm som rang.

3. Khamun na awm Reng lukhum kha
Keei deeng nepek rang nih
Pumi jongrang saw tak ah ru ngak hai
Ama le awm som rang.

4. Makhan thit se tak ah sawn rang
Ravan ram kha raw ah
Ringna kawt pui khan keei ni awm lut rang
Kum sawt ningai nakhan.
