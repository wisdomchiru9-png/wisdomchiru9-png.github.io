294. I Hope To Meet You In Glory R.S: 152.

1. Rumu ret rang ti-ah kimining
Ringkho sawna mawtawr le
Risim alui kha reei rang kani
Alur avar kawmchang kha.
Avar rangkachak vara hoi teeng khan
Napa awm na ram inn hoi mun sung khan
Rumu ngir rang nanih, makhan rumu ngir mun rang.
Mawtawr na Reng lukhum rukhu rang.

2. Rumu ret rang ti-ah kimining
pries!
Ringna thingkung mawng nakhan
Nakhekpa pumi kha mapak rang
Numpok Amadeeng nawchawi.

3. Rumu ret rang ti-ah kimining
Nakhekpa Rengsut mun khan
Kheksa ra miring le rawtawp rang
Nakhek na lah hoi thaw rang.

4. Rumu ret rang ti-ah kimining
Reeleei chon kawmawtawr le
Ningai maja ben som rang nanih
Kumsawt avar kawmchang kha.
