69, Why They Ring The Golden Bell                   H.P.326

1. Ahoi ram chungchon khat ru-awm
   Ahoiram hektik neeiboi ram
   Kha munkhan tak ah sawn hai ruse sojoi
   Tuipui vara narakan le, relek tik neeiboi ramhoi khan
    Rangkhachak daarsut, nangle keei rang ah nih.

           Daar miri nang rujet joi maw?

           Tirtaun hai lah thaw nang jet maw?

           Ningai tak ah Halleluiya he rasang

           Hektik neeiboi ram khopui khan

           Avar le mun hek tik neeiboi

            Rangkachak daar sut nangle keei rang a nih.

2. Keeini Ravanram nosok le, asaw ara awm lai naw nih
    Rangkachak dangka kawmchang narakan le
    Keeini ningaina kha muk rang,
    Ravan ram Pumi Patheen le
    Rangkachak daarsut naangle keei rang a-nih.

3. Manmeeseh ringkho mawtawr le
    Tak ah sawn na athi hai kha
    Nupumina lam jawngna ruthaw rang nih
    Sawna rana awm lai naw nih
    Numpok na ramhoi nosok le
    Rangkachak daarsut nangle keei rang ah ni.
