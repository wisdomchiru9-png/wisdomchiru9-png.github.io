505. Jesus Is Risen              S.S. 146.

                 1.Cor. 15:20.
Key C.

1. Enro, ringna nikho Easter
    Vanoi ningai lurna
    Enro, lungkha rurawlawng joi
    Athi raa mawtawr joi.

            Jisu ringjoi ireei angkhan

            Thaan raa mawtawr ah hawngthoi joi

            Ningai lah thawro Easter nihin

            Araat Nakhek Jisu hawngringjoi.

2. Enro, athi i-jona pon
    Tha tak ah rurethep
     Enro, azing le arok thaan
     Jisu ru awmlaimak.

3. Enro, reeleei ah lengret joi
     'Ma numpok kha lawmro
     Enro, ningaina maiso kha
     Chungteeng ah put jongvar.

4. Easter ningai nakha awm joi
     Easter lah le chawngkha
     Easter khutchawi mukna nikho
     Pumi raa mawtawr na.
