85 What Would I do Without Jesus
3rd Alex. 421.
LK. 9:27.
Key E/Flat.

1. Jisu jawboi ah imo thaw ning?
Sindawng na keeina keeneei reret
Keeina kejet Jisu jaw boi ah tu
Ringkho hi saw na rana rok nih.

Jisu jawboi ah imo thaw ning?
Azing nikho hawngsok pha roi khan
Karatha na sawna rakhana neei phakhan
Jisu jawboi ah tu imo thaw ning?

2. Jisu niboi ah imo thaw ning?
Numpok lawmdit hai ruse pha khan
Karatha azing na numukhu ah
Nisun avar kha munaw ning maw?

3. Jisu jaw boi ah imo thaw ning?
Tekawlawi jongtum nikho tak khan
Ama chawngpek juiboi kinit ala
'Ma ningai chon jetboi maw nining?
