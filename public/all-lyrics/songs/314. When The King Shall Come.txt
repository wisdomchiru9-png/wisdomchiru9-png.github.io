314. When The King Shall Come                    S.S. 174,

                          Ps. 149:2.

                                           M

Key A/Flat.

1. Sawna jan ajing hina reemeei ang ah numu khu joi
    Enroh, Ravan na put Arkhon hawng ah zingkar kha ni joi
    Zion lawn naput ra-ai he keeini nejet rang ni-joi
    Ningai ah ruchun rang nijoi, Rengpa jong phakhan.

            Oh Zion (2)

            Rengpa jongpha khan mawtawr na he chun rang

            Oh Zion (2)

            Mapak urang nijoi Rengpajong nikhan.

 2. Jehovah na irukhek sa naihai simle mar ah awm
     Ningai ah vak kir urang nih
     Sawmuchum na muk narang
     Phungtin jat-tin, ramtin haina Ravan muk urang nijoi.
     Ningai ah ruchun rang nijoi,
     Rengpa jong phakhan.

3. Sun le jan na ru ngak chak haikha,
    Mana mu-ngam nimaw?
    Rapon avar rete lang khale Rengchon thawna munkhan
    Avar reetheeng khanan nemeeivar hi var jawnjai roso
    Ningai ah ruchun rang nijoi, Rengpa jongpha khan.
