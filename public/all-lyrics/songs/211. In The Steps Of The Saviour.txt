211. In The Steps Of The Saviour                R.S. 295
Key D/Flat.

1. Nakhekpa Nupumi a-nukha juirawi
    'Ma kathawt and kha jui rawi
    Niring kho ah Ama awmchan noloi atu
     Nithaw lah kha idawr hoi rang mo.

             Idawr ra atha moni Pumi anu juihi

             Avar khan lawn rawi (2)

             Idawrra atha moni Pumi anu jui hi

             Lamdik khan nawchawi.

  2. Lam Nakai pepa Amale ranai rawi
      nesena lam nawmawlawi pe pha khan
      'Ma ban ranak le keeini nurung rang nih
      Idawr ningai rangmo ningkip lawm urawi.

   3. Doiraat na mulung renem khan kachawi rawi
      Numpok mirit kenu kha juirawi
      'Ma numpok chawngrakham nakha entai ah
      Chungteeng nahawngkha idawr hoirang mo?

   4. Nakhekpa Nupumi anukha juirawi
      Chungteeng Naroipa anu kha juirawi
       Rengpa Ama lurna kha numuk phakhan
      Idawr hoirang mo Amadeeng awm rang.
