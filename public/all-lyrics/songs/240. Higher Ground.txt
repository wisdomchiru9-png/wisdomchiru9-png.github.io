240. Higher Ground           R.S. 797.
Key A/Flat.

1. Chung khopui lam karaw phakhan
   Ningthing kip kawhawng dep dedet
   Tunse Patheen deng kinik lai
   Chung khopui kha kosok narang.

          Pumi nadawm ro khan ding rawng

          Tak ah sawn khana Ravan ram

          Rasang det mun kha kumuk joi

          Pumi chungkhopui khan awm rawng.

2. Karatha "Hin" awm jawt laimak
   Chit na le rakhana munhin
   Hoina munhin awm ujoi awm
   Kininiik u chungkhopui kha

3. Reeleei chungteeng ka-awm jawt joi
   Atha boina nalak na-awm
   Tak ahsawn ningaina kumuk
   Reetheeng haina lah kha thaw uh.

4. Rasang chungchon ramkha kadit
   Ningai avar kha kaman joi
   Ravan ram sok malim niikrang
   Chung khopui khan nawchawi uroh.
