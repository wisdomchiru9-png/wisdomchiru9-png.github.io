57. He Rescued Me    R.S.607.
Key G/Flat.

1. Munuphung kani tun nakhek joi
    'Ma numpok na keei nakhek joi
    Kimik ruchaw tun hawng var joi
   Akang meeichong na put keei nakhek joi.

            Keei Nakhek joi, Mata kanih

            Akang meeichong naput keei nakhek joi

            Hidawr mapak awm, kumsawt pumi nih

            Akang meeichong na put keei nak khek joi.

2. keei ala tak ah kuru chai joi
   'Ma numpok na keei nakhek joi
   Tun rasang Rengpa lam karaw
   Akang meeichong aput keei nakhek joi.

3. Thaboi ah put Patheen ta kanih
   'Ma numpok na keei nakhek joi
    Munu rentuk rui chat so joi
   Akang meeichong ah put keei nakhek joi.
