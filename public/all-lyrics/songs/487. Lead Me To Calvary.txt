487. Lead Me To Calvary                 C.S.L. 203.
Key F.

1. Reng lukhum kha kunumu khu
    Nangjeng na niroso
    Calvary teeng kei nawchawi ro
    Nang sawna kha murawng.

           Gethsemane ah nang sawna

           Keei na kejet nahet rang khan

           Calvary teeng keei nawchawi ro

            Nang numpok na murawng.

 2. Nangzana thaan numu muk ro
     Nangsawna kholok kha
     Ravantir hai avaar khana
     Nang nurung na munkhan.

 3. Marry ang ah nangdeeng khutchawi
     Keeina navak chawirawng
     Calvary teeng keei nawchawiro
     Thaan kha numumuk ro.

 4. Kupumi nang Cross kha keeina
     Ningthingkip khan phungrawng
     Pumi sawna nang idoi kha
     Kiring narang ah nih.
