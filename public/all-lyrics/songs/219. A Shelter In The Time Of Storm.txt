    219. A Shelter In The Time Of Storm            S.S.539

                     Ps. 21:3.

   Key F

   1. Oh, lungpum kerethe na u
      Rithi rang pha saw muchum na
      Nirawtawng pui sawna khan awm
      Sawmuchum na lungpum nang nih.

             Sawna raamkhan nasaan lungpum kha nangni

             Sawna raana mun rikip ah

             Oh, Jisu lungpum nasaan theeipa nangni

             Rithi khopui hawng rang sekhan.

 2. 'Sun ah rilip zan na narung
     Saw muchumna lungpum nangnih
     Chitna rawthawk na awm lai mak
     Rithi khopui rang pha sekhan.

3. Rithi khopuina na nor awm
    Sawmuchumna lungpum nangnih
    Lekse awm rephet naw minih
    Rithi khopui rang pha sekhan.

4. Oh, Lungpum, kerethe na uh
    Saw muchumna lungpum nang nih
   Nasaanpa nedep chak uro
   Rithi khopui hawngrang sekhan.
