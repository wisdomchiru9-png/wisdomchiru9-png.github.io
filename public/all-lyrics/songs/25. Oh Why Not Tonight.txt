25 Oh Why Not Tonight                C.H. 298.
Key E/Flat.

1. Ringna chawng resen pui noro
    Avaar kha thang noboi rang khan
    Munuphung nang mulung mining
    Tunhin lek rang ro.

            Von zan pha sung hin

            Nang mulung lek ro

           Nakhek rang nih

            Tun zan pha sung hin

2. Zing ah iti apha awm mak
    Nang mulung nang lek narang khan
    Von jan mulung nang lek ah tu
    Nakhek rang nijoi.

3. Neei khana rawchaw ngam nawnih
    Mulung lek na ranak neei mak
    Khrista riming tena mawtawr
    Nakhek narang khan.

4. Pumina napai lak nawnih
    Amana num ipok haikha
    Ringrep rang ro nasan rang nih
    Von zan pha sung hin.
