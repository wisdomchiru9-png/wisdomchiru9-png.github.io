222. Revieve They Work Oh, Lord

                 Heb. 3:2

Key G.

1. Pumi nangchon thaw rawng
   Nang ratha awm roso
   Miri kip deeng nangchawng pek kha
   Vanoi ra-ngai roso.

          Nangchon thaw rawng Pumi

          Nangdeeng kaw-awm lai hin

          Jongchum ro numpoktheei Pumi

          Raavan nepe uro.

2. Pumi chonthaw narang
   Nang riming mapak rawng
   Nang numpok kha mirikip deeng
   Meei ang ah chong roso.

3. Pumi nangchon thaw rawng
   Vanoideeng nang chawng kha
   Reetheeng le adik chang roso
   Ringna kha zet roso.

4. Pumi nangchon thawrang
   Penticost phor tha kha
   Nepe uro, ningaina kha
   Keeini rang niroso.
