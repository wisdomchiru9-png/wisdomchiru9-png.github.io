468. He Has Taken My Sins Away                 S.S. 899.

                 Jn. 1:29.

Key B/Flat.

1. Kumulung na Pumi mapak rawng
   'Madeeng ningai rang kani
   'Ma numpok nakha lah ah thaw rang
   Mahi kithaw lah nih.

           Keeseet na kholok nawchawi pejoi

           'Ma riming reetheeng mapak rang kani

            Kumunu kholok nawchawi pejoi

            Halleluyah 'Ma riming h.

 2. Kumunu phung pokrik hai khale
    Sawmuchum rang kawhawng pha
    Kinik nezetpe, khan nawmawsawk
     Kithaw lah khanih.

 3. Karang chat deeng sekhan mapak rang
     Thitna raa mawtawr jarkhan
     hektik neeiboi 'Ma mapak rang,
     Hi kathaw lah kha nih.
