277. Beautiful Home             T.N.B.C. 6.
Key B/Flat.

1. Ravan ram ah keei nirang atha tak inn khat ru-awm
   Ahoi tak inn khat ru-awm joi
    Makha inn ahoi dan kha nener hina reeingam mak
    Khadawr thep ahoi inn kha awm.

            Ravan ramma ahoi tak inn

            Ahoi tak inn kha ru-awm joi

            Arshi kha rakan Veek ah

            Makha inn khan alut rang

             Keeinirang ahoi inn kha awm.

 2. Makha inn avar peng kha ahoi ningai na rok nih
     Ahoi tak inn khat ru-awm joi
     Manmeeseh ram namak le sawna hi awm lai nawni
     Makha inn kha nosok phit le.

 3. Tuipui leen kha rakan na, Khamunkhan ruchun som rang
     Ahoi tak inn khat ru-awm joi
     Arachak reei kawmchang khan, keeinirang ringna mun awm
     Ahoi tak inn khat ru-awm joi.
