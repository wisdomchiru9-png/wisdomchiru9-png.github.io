173. Jisus Shall Reign            S.S. 1084.

                   Ps. 72:8.

Key E.

1. Mun rikip Jisu Rengchang joi,
    Kawkung kawdawng sun rang nijoi
    Reeleei le Ravan sun rang nih
    Thathit thadet awm lai naw nih.

2. Phungtin chawngtin mirikip na
    Numpok lah le awi mathar rawi
    He renem le chawngtha sem rawi
    'Ma riming ah raavan murawi.

 3. 'Madeeng Beekna neei jawn jai rawi
     Lawm tai ah lukhum mukhu rawi
     'Ma riming manam hoi roso
     Zingkar Beek na mun rikip ah.

 4. Reeleei ah phungtin chawngtin na
     Khin neei ah chubai bawk roso
    "Vantir haina lah le jongchum
    Amen ti-ah reeleei muchun.
