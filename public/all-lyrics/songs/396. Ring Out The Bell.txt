396. Ring Out The Bell.

1. Christmas daar kha surang ro ningai nikho nijoi
Reetheeng Misapa rachaak phabi phahin jongsok joi
Sindawngroi, Nasaanpa seh inn rokong ah maja
Ravaantir haina Jisu sok nakha jongheei chip joi

Daar surang ro
Christmas rang
Daar surang ro (3)
Christmas nikho nijoi.

2. Bethlehem seh inn khan thitna put nakhek rang ah
Jisu jongsokjoi ningai lah kha' Vaantirhaina thaw
Zingkar vaarpeng kha le khrista vaar kha rakapjoi
Christmas zingkar var hile ningai daar kha su rang rawi.

3. Khrista meh kha muk le mirikipna maipar joi
Jongsok reetheeng adik numpok Jisu kha nangta nih
Tun Nasaanpa deengkungah nang awm narang na-ngak
Ama numpok ke-ngam hai karkhan hawng jaw cheen rang ro.

4. Jongsok Reng Sapa deengkhan ningai ah mapak ro
Christmas avaar khana mirikip ningai kha jongpek
Khrista bu michin nikhan phung rikip kha koijoi
Jisuna numpok kheksa miring hai lim meeneei mak.
