474. I Am Coming To The Cross                   S.S.477.

                   Jn. 6:37.

Key G.

1. Cross teengkhan keei kahawng joi
    Keei sara mikchaw sawdoi
    Reetheengboi chungchon kani
    Kumsawt ringna kha mukrang.
    Pumi tak ah kawnaw sawn
    Calvary ah Ke-ngam te
    Cross kungkhan kawhawng bujoi
    Nakhek ro tun nasaan ro.

 2. Pha sawt tak kumulung ah
     Raikho na chonkha ruthaw Jisu numpok na neereei
    "Munu nilik perang " ti.

 3. Kineeilim kha kenepek
     Reeleei lawmdit neei le raat
     Karatha le katak hi
     Kumsawt rang nangta nijoi.

 4. Kumulung ningai sip joi
     'Ma deeng keereetheeng sojoi
     Nurupum masak sojoi
      Lur roso Ke-ngam te kha.
