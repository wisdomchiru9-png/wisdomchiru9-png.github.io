257. No Night There    S.S. 1012.

                Rev. 21:25.

Key E/Flat.

1. Nitak naboi ram mun khan
   Ahoi khopui khat awm
    Kumsawt ah thang lai nawni
    Zan awm-awmlai  naw nih.

           Mik rithi ho rang nijoi

            Thitna natna awm naw nih

            Kum le tha iti awm lai mak

            Zan awm-awm lai naw nih.

 2. Kawtpui rangkachak rok nih
     makha khopul hoi khan
     Lam awm rangkachak rok nih
     Zan awm-awm lai nawnih.

  3. Kawtpui kha rawlawng jawn jai
      Makha khopui hoikhan
      Avar ringna tuipui long
      Zan-awm awm lai nawnih.

  4. Nivar awm-awm lai nawnih
      Makha khopui hoikhan
      Ke-ngam te avar khana
      Zan maw awm lai nawnih.
