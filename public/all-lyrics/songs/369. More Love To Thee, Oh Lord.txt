369. More Love To Thee, Oh Lord S.S. 632
John 21:15.
Key A/Flat.

1. Oh Khrista numko nopok na det rang
Nang deengkhan rukhuk sun na kininiik,
Nangdeeng kene ngen chak
Dik tak ah kininiik, nang kha nadit det rawng
Oh Khrista num nopo det rawng.

2. Reeleei hoina hai hi kuru ngam nah
Tuntu nangte keeina kana tawk chak
Nangdeeng kene ngen chak
Khrista num nopo rawng
kupumi num nopo det rawng.

3. Mulung sawna Idawr nawmawsaw awm
Nang chawng hoi khana keei nama ngar joi
Lah hoi kawthaw pha khan
Khrista num nopo rawng
Kupumi num nopo det rawng.

4. Ki-ngam tukse nangkha namapak rawng
Chap ah resen so le, ratha thoirang
Tunse mahi rang hin Pumideeng kininiik,
Oh Khrista num nopo det rawng.
