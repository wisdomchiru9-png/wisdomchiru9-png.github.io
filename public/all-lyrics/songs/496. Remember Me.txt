496. Remember Me             S.S. 630.

                Jer. 15:15

Key F

1. Nakhekpa thikha rusok maw?
   Pumi kha ruthi maw?
   Aman lu reetheeng kha nepek
   1-awm niboi keeirang.

           Numpok nakhekpa nasaan ro

           Tak ah sawn narep rang

           Rengmun na nang rusut phakhan

           Pumi nimining ro.

2. Kiruthaw chonthaboi jarkhan
   Cross ah saw nodoi pe
   'Ma misaan na sindawngroi hi
   Reeingam boi numpok   nih.

3. Nivar na azing mavar ngam
   'Ma lurna te ngam mak
   Athaw ngam khrista nithit pe
    Munu phung keeirang hin.

4. Kumunu kha ruthe rang maw?
   'Ma numpok Cross deengkhan
    Reeirit mulung ningai khana
    Lungjum mik rithi cho.

5. Ana khek na te-ngam naw ning
    Kiton numpok law kha
    Pumi nangdeeng kerepe hi
    Ki-ngam tuk khanijoi.
