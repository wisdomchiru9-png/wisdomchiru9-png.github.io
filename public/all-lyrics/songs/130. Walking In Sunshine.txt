130. Walking In The Sunshine S.S. 1099.
Matt. 4:19.
Key B/Flat.

1. Ningai atha nivaar namun teeng serawi
Zingkar nisok engseei le zan rawdaw khan
Zamti ah maton teeng ningthingkip lawn rawi
Jisu anu jui rawi.
Juirawi, Jisu anu jui rawi
Juirawi, ningthingkip jui rawi
Juirawi Ma lamkai na munkhan
Lam rikip ah Jisu kha juirawi.

2. Vaar nadet le zingna det mun nit a-awm
Ahoi khosa le surlai chimrip nit awm
Ma he ra-ngai tana nawt tak ah jui ro
Jisu anu juichak ro.

3. Rangkachak nivaar le athuk rilip khan
Rithi khopui khana numu khu joi awm
Nikho hawng re-ep awm, 'Ma tak' sawn rep rawi
Jisu anu juichak rawi.
