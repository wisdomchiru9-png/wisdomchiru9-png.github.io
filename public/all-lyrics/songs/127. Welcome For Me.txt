127. Welcome For Me S.S. 469.
Lk. 15:20.
Key B/Flat.

1. Va rubu ah put 'la tak ah chai kha ang ah
Nakhekpa deeng ah put kuruchai
niah nangnumpok na he khana nokoi ret
Nuru jawng rang ni iti kejet.
Nakhekpa na nuru jawng joi
Ningai tak ah nuru jawng joi
Nangnumpok nakhan vasu ang ah
Tun saw muchum na kumuk joi.

2. Rokong na nasaan joi, kamatha kajo joi
Ree theeng numpok misaan na sungkhan
Nang naw-awm pui jarra, kiningaina sipjoi
Kumsawt ningaina keeirang ah ni.

3. Jisu deeng saw kumuchum joi, chilai nawning
Sawna idawr kawrawtawng pui awm
Nita awmna boi Ravan en chak rang kanih
'Mulung ah ningai lah thaw chak rang.
