41. Amazing Grace (S.S. 894)
Key G/Flat

1. Idawr dit awm numpok moni?
Mithaboi keei Nakhek
Ruthang ah awm tun numuk joi
Kimik rujing var joi.

2. Numpok na chit nakha mawtawr
Chitna neeilai mi-ing
Manrawp numpok chonkha lar joi
Tak ah kawsawn nikhan.

3. Thitna theeilim mun rikipkhan
Kawhawng rekhe sojoi
Numpok na nawhawng chawi nasaan
Inn se nawchawi rang nih.

4. Tak sa mulung hi hawngchoi pha
Reeleei ringkho hek le
Makhan keeina rachak ningai
Ringkho tum rang kanih.
