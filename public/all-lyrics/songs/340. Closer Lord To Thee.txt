340. Closer Lord To Thee           S.S. 639.

                Ps. 73:28.

Key G/Flat.

1. Kawnawhawng dep kupumi
   Nang kawnawhawng dep
   nang matha ringna thoi khan
   Keeina awmchak rawng
   Munu kopok rik hina
   Kanuk maton teeng ne-ep
   Mawtawr narang nasan roh
   Kawnawhawng dep joi.

2. Kawnawhawngdep kupumi
   Athang boi lungpum
    Rithi khopui hawngrat awm
    Tui hawng let awm
    Ringna nang kekung thoi khan
    Kerethe chilai nawning
    Thangtik neeiboi kupumi
    Kawnawhawng dep joi.

 3. Kawnawhawng dep Kupumi
    Nang kawnawhawng dep
    Asapa Khrista jarra
    Ningai na kaman
    nang numpok ban ranak khan
    Keeihi nakaw rep rang ro
    Athang ti awm boi lungpum
    Kawnawhawng dep joi.
