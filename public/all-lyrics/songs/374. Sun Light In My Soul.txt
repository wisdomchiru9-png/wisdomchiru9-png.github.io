374. Sun Light In My Soul
John 16:20
Ny E

1. Azing jankhan kuruchai
Keeideeng Jisu jong malim
Ma numpok niivar khana
Azing kha mavar joi.
Von kumulung ah nivaar kha awm
kawlawn na lam nivar na mavaar
Pumina numuk ah munu chawi sojoi
Ama numpok nivaar kha tun kumuk joi

2. Reemeei na Ravan buk ah awm
Tuipui na naheei awm
Manmeeseh zing ah awm
Mulung ah nivaar keeneei.

3. Patheen vaar na kalawn pha khan
Reebeek pui hoi kumuk
Mun reetheeng teeng kase ah
Reeleei zing kunuk teeng kamak.

4. Ramhoikha karatha na
Phaijaw khan keei kalawn nah
'Ma numpok nivaar sung khan
Rangkachak sangvui ka-aat.

5. 'Ma muk rang relen tak ah,
Keeirang jong repe pakha
Enro Ama mai avaar kha
Kumsawt ah vaar rang nih.
