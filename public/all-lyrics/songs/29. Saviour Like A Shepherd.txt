 29. Saviour Like A Shepherd               S.S. 1164.

                    Ps. 23:1.

 Key E/Flat

1. Nakhekpa lam nakai pero
    Nang ten ret na kadit uh
    Nangsak kha le navai uroh
    Arawp det nepe uro
    Pumi Jisu Pumi Jisu
    Nang nuru rawchawk u ah
    Pumi Jisu Pumi Jisu
    Nangta kha kani ujoi.

2. Nanglawm nangta kha ni u rawng
    Ninit pero Pumi lam kha
    Nang ke-ngam hai nasaan uro
    Rehena khan natawk ro
    Kunumpok det Pumi Jisu
    Kininiik pha uroi khan
    Kunumpok det Pumi Jisu
    Nuru ra-ngai pe uro.

3. Keeini rang khan chawng nang rakham
   Munu neei kinit u 'awm
   Numpok khana kere khe joi
   Nang ranak khan keereetheeng joi
   Minumpok theei Pumi Jisu - (2)
   Nang deeng khan vak kir urawng - (2)

4. Nang misan nakha tawk urawng
   Nang idit kha thaw urawng
   Nang numpok kaman theei Pumi
   Numpok kha kaman ujoi
   Pumi Jisu Pumi Jisu
   Num nang nurupok chak uh
   Pumi Jisu Pumi Jisu
   Num nopo thep uret ro.
