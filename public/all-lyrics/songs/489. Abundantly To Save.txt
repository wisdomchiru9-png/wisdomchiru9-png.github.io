489. Abundantly To Save              S.S. 400

                 Isaiah 4:7.

Key A.

1. Tunit awm Cross ah 'thi patheen Sapa kha
    Tak ah sawn na ruloi miring rikip na
    Maanboi ringna kha muk ngir rang nijio
    Ama na Chat boi pet ah varan rang ni.

            Oh, sokpui dit hai pumina nokoilai

            'Ma numpok le misaan na sindawngroi nih

            Ama thisen na munu neei hai nakhek

            Amana chatboi pet ah nasaan rang nih.

 2. Tunit awm patheen chawng aloi miring hai
     Ratha rusuk ngam thisen tak ah sawn kha
     Kumsawt ah inakhek nakha tum rang ni
     Amana nangkha nasaan rangkhan adit.

 3. Munu mathaan na mulung lek mihai kha
     Mulung mawlawng joi pumi lut narang khan
     Rutuk thep ringna khutchawi kha tum rang nih
     Tun nasaan fang Jisu rasam ah awmjoi.
