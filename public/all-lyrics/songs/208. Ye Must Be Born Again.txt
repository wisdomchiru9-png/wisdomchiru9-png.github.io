208. Ye Must Be Born Again                S.S. 366.

                  John. 3:3.

Key E/Flat.

1. Nicodimus na jan na Jisu deeng seh
   Kumsawt ringna le varna lam vak rawdawn
   Pumi Jisuna adik tak kha masang,

           "Nang athar ah sok ro'

           'Nang atharra sok ro' (2)

           Reetheeng heei ah nangdeeng kineereei hini

            "Atharra sok ret ro".

2. Miringhai chawnghi reeirit ah mining ro
   Jisuna renem tak ah ireei chawng hi
   Chawngtha hi reem ah athang ninoroso
    "Nang atharra sok ro"

3. Ningai sawmuchum na munkhan hawng lut hai
    Inakhek na lah hoi kha thaw chak rang ro
    Kumsawt ah ring nakha nang tum jawt atu
    "Nang atharra sok ro"

4. Ravan na lawmdit hai nangmuk jawt atu
    Kawtpui ahoikhan nuru ngak chak rang nih
     Renem chawngpek hai hi mining rep tana
    "Nang atharra sok ro".
