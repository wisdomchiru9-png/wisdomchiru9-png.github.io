199. Look Not Behind Thee                S.S. 438.

                 Gen.19:17.

Key G.

1. Riringro munuphung nuk en noro
    Relen rang ro rehena mun awm joi
   Sum narang ah milem chawng loi noro
    Ijarra rasai mo Jisuna nokoi.

            Riring na chawng awm asawt awm noro

            Manmeesee hoina aput tan rang ro

            Sum narang milem chawng loi lai noro

            Ravan tirna nokoi rasai lai noro.

2. Riringro athang hai nuk en noro
    Ijarra mining dik boi ah saw mo ?
    Nanglam thit na theei azing na buk joi
    Jisuna nokoi ijarra rasai mo?

 3. 'Nuk heei noro nalem rang mi awm joi
     Relen rangro nanglam kha rawlawng joi
     Num napo Jisuna von nokoilai
     Nangpa inn khan hawngro rasai lai noro.
