91. Trust In Jesus           3rd Alex. 161.

                  Prov. 16:20.

Key A/Flat.

1. Jisu deeng te tak ah sawn ro
    'Ma chawng kha po rep tana
    Chawng rakham na saw muchum rang
    Pumina nemejet joi.

            Jisu, Jisu tak ah kawsawn

            Idawr jetna thaw ngam ning

            Pumi Jisu manrawp Patheen

           Numpok, tak ah sawn det rang.

2. Idawr hoi mo 'Ma tak 'sawn hi
    Inakhek na thisen hi
    Tak ah sawn tena nuhup ngam
    'Nakhek na thisen sung ah.

3. Jisu deeng tak ah sawn hi hoi
   Munu aput nakhek joi
   Jisu bai tek ah aloi hi
   Rachak saw muchum nanih.

4. Kiningai 'Ma tak ah sawn hi
   Manrawp Jisu kawlawm nih
   'Mana naw-awm puichak kejet
   Dawngse naw-awm pui rang nih.
