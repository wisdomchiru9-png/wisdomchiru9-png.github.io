440. The Cross Of Jesus.

1. Jisuna thing Cross chungkhan
Munu jarra nithit pe
Cross chunga thisen khana
Munu rikip rusuk joi
Enro, Jisuna keeini
Jarra nojong thit pe joi
Cross ah nithit pe jarra
Munu rikip reetheeng joi.

          Mirikip rang ah 'Ma thisen rulong

          Numunu jarra renro rureeveei

          Munu rikip kha nawchawi pe sojoi.

2. Jisuna Thing Cross chungkhan
   Munu jarra nithit pe
   'Khut le ke ah jotpi le
   Edep ah seei sebe le
   Jonglong thisen aput khan
   Munu rikip reetheeng joi
   Munu  sawna raana kha
   'Ma thisen na mawtawr joi.

3. Jisuna Thing Cross chungkhan
   Munu jarra nithit pe
   Ama thisen rulong kha
   Rawchawk ngam boi thisen nih
   Jisu thisen long khana
   Athang hai a-nakhek joi
   'Ma numpok jarra munu
   Kholok nawchawi pe sojoi

4. Jisuna Thing Cross chungkhan
   Munu jarra nithit pe
   Numunu jarra renro
   Reeveei nurodoi pe joi
   Ama thisenlong khana
   Aja haikha mothoi joi
   Jisu anu jui tai ah
   Ama pakna lah thaw rawi.
S.S.136.
