75. He Will Hide Me           3rd Alex. 172.

                  Ps. 49:2.

Key G.

1. Ringkho rithi khopui khana
    Tuile mo ah hawng rang pha
    Rekhe na mun tawk rang kani
    patheen khut rilip hoi khan.

            'Mana keeihi nethe rang nih

            Sawna rana neeilai naw ning

            'Mana keeihi nethe rang nih

            Patheen khut rilip hoikhan.

2. Sawna rana jongpha Khan awm
    Inn kha nara ngai masak
    Numpok le lunghang awm naboi
    'Ma reetheeng mun nirang nih.

3. Naraha puina nawmaw pawp
    Satan na nawmaw kawi awm
    'Mana nawmawlawi perang nih
    Kumsawt ningai narang khan.

4. Keeina Cross kha kuphung lai khan
    Sawna kha kawrawtawng pui
    Keeirang Jisuna nuphung pe
    Ama naikha kanijoi.
