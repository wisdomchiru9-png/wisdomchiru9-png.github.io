  60. Who Is On The Lord's Side              S.S. 694.

                 Exodus. 22. 26.

  Key F.

  1. Cannan teeng lawp khat ah lah le nase joi
     Athaboi le rawthaw rang ah ramiring
     Nawrawchawk ah tu ranak neei naw minih
     Nakar ah tumoni Patheen le ah-awm ?

            Oh, tumo na-awm nakar ah adik chonthaw?
            } 2

            Pumi deengkung khan tumo ading ngam rang?

 2. Chemjang kha tat rang ro, pho kha melek ro
    Raikho na ravantir kha rasa rang nih
    Azing mulung sung ah lemna rethe theei
    Pumi le ka-awm ti ner na rophong ngam.

3. Tunse achi nakar ah tumo na-awm ?
    Patheen numpokna ajet boi tumo awm?
   Oh, leen rasat nakha 'Madeeng hawngchawi ro
   Pumi le awm narang na-ngak relen ro.

4. Adikboi mulung saw nakha neei noro
   Relen na sawna kha lah le mawtawr rang
   Keeini lam nakaipe Cross kha ra-ngai rawi
   Pumi deeng kachang ti-ah ra-aai rang ro.
