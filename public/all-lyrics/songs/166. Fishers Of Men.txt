 166. Fishers Of Men               3rd Alex. 194.

                    Mk. 1:17.

 Key A/Flat.

 1. Galilee ah ngasur haideeng Jisu ireei kejet
    "Nujui rangro misur miring namachang rang kani"

            Nujui ro (2)

            Misur miring namachang rawng

            Galilee mi nang ikoi kha,

           ang ah nujui rang rawng.

2. Galilee mirang ah nimak Pumina nangle keei
    Misur rang khan nojong koichak, keei nawhawng juiro ti

3. Kumulung kholok le nangkha nujui rang rawng Pumi
   Kurutan ah kala joi, Pumi keei nojong khek ro.

4. Kumulung ah nang numpok nakha nojong mumuk roh
   Galilee ah awmchan angkhan Pumi nojong reeiro.
