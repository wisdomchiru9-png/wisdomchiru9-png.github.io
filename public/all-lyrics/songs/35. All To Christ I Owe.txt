35. All To Christ I Owe (S.S. 855)
1 Peter 2:24
Key E/Flat

1. Jisu irrei kezet
"Ranak neei lak mak che"
Adawk hai niichak ro
Keeihi natawk chak ro.

Kiton rikip kha Jisuna thungjoi
Kumunu phung rikip kha
Vur ang ah mechek joi.

2. Tun Pumi kumuk joi
Nangjeng na ranak kha
Rai rekhek hai dam na
Mulung michim mawjawr.

3. Atha kithaw awm mak
Nang numpok tum narang
Munupon rusuk rawng
Calvary ah thisen le.

4. Thitna jakhang chung ah
Ratha hawngthoi rang nih
Jisu na nuthung pe
Ravan ram lut narang.

5. Rengsut na maikung ah
Keeina kawhawng ding le
Mawtawna zet na kha
Jisu deengkhan tet rang.
