245. Ring The Bell Of Heaven
Lk. 15:10
Key B/Flat.

1. Ravan na ningai darkha suro von hin
Ratha achai khat kha hawng kir joi
Enroh Apana lam ah rawtawng puijoi
Asaw athang achai sapa kha.
Ningai Ravantir haina lah thaw
Ningai Salangdar miri sok joi
Kheksa rami haina tuipui raat ang ah
Ina khek na lah hoi kha thaw uh.

2. Von ningai awjoi Ravan darkha suro
Achai ah athangpa kha hawng kir joi
Munu ah ratha mathangpa akhek joi
Atharra sok joi khek sa nai kha.

3. Dar suro, von bumichin nahin reeiro
Vantir haina ningai mosok joi
Ningai chawng hi ram latak sekhan reeiro.
Manlu, ratha atharra sok joi.
S.S. 973.
