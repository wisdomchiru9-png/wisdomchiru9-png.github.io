11. Love Divine, All Loved Excelling            S.S.242.

                1. John 4:8.
Key 'B/Flat.

1. Reeetheeng numpok ningai Ravan,
   Reeleei chunga jongsok joi.
   Keeinideeng naw awmpui chak roh
   Nang numpok Reng lukhum le.
   Mi numpok theei Jisu nangni
   Dawng awmboi numpok nangni.
   Ringna khale ne-en uroh
    Mulung sunga awm roso.

2. Numpok ratha le phuksan rawng,
    Mulung sawna rikip khan.
    Nang inkok lo kha ni urawng,
    Saw muchumna mu urawng.
    Nakhek rengpa jongro Pumi,
    Nang numpok kha man urawng,
    Relen na nang jongkir pha khan
    Nang Beek inn mathan naw ning.

3. Ringhkho thar kha nangkon sole
    Reetheengdet kha chang urawng,
    Ringna kholok kha mu urawng
    Nangta het kha ni urawng,
    Ningai det a jir rang roso,
    Ravanram kolo malim.
    Nangdeeng lukhum kumuk malim
    Numpok le namapak rang.
