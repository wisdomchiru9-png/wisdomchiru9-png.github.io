1. Kumulung mawkawt ataw,
   Keeina kinik pha khan,          dine
   Pumi Jisu Khrista he,
   Konokor le rehem.

              on munum wire na pod      w

           "Mulung nawmawlawng pero

           Keedeeng ah jong awm ro'

           Pumi na nedep le khan,        manane

           'Male rawtawp chak rang.

                                        o manith

2. Neredep pui ro Pumi,
    Keetheen na kaleng awm,            on Ren luc
    Kimining boi pha se khan

                                       o Aum e

    Nomosok lai noro.                sit odgo

3. Zingna khap pha se khan awm
    Numukui chak rang ro,
    Lam kerehe pha sekhan
    Pumi natawk chak ro.

                                     sa si copns

4. Ringkho sung ah saw na kha
    Kawrawtawng pui pha khan
    Keeihi nasaan ro Pumi,
    Sawna rakan narang.
