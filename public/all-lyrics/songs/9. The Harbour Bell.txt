9. The Harbour Bell     S.S. 395
Key F

1. Ringkho hi tui rawnawng ang ah
    Munu khana nawmawtawr theei.
    Sawna rithipui hawngrang pha
    Tak a sawnboi reemeei na buk.
   Ala tak a nokoi ne jet,
    Rokong chum mun daar he khana,
    Athang hai ringna neereei joi.
    Ratha rakak hai ningai roh.

            Mulung sawhai inteeng hong roh

            Sawna rithi nangdoi a awm,

            Hinteeng hin sawmuchum na awm,

            Ravan daar he hoikha nejet.

2. Nokoina he kha rangai rawi,
    He rasang tak a masang roh.
    Keeini nokoina teeng sir khan,
    He renem ah masang chak rawi.
    Mi tampui ratha thang sojoi,
    Tui longna teeng alan sojoi.
    Keeideeng hongro tia nokoi,
    Ring mukna daar kha miri joi.

3. Lemna rawp joi riring rang roh,
    Pumi chawng rakham khani.
    Mawtawr na lah thaw rang nani,
   Sawdoi ngam miring sengna.
    Patheen ratha kha jong rang ni,
   'Ma numpok chawng mejet rang khan.
   Tuipui rawnawng vara teeng khan,
   Rokong chummun daar na nokoi.
