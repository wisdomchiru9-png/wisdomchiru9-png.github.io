112. Standing On The Promises Of God
S.S. 877,

2. Cor. 1:20.
Key B/Flat.

1. Reng, Khrista chawngrakham nakhan keei kading
Kumsawt ah Ama mapak na niroso
Rasnag Ama jeng nakha lah ah thaw rang
Patheen chawng rakham nakhan kading.
Keeina...kading
Nakhekpa chawng rakham nakhan keeina kading
Keeina...kading
Nakhekpa chawng rakham nakhan kading.

2. Chawng rakham na kading hi pur lai naw ning
Chitna rakhana rithi khopui kar khan
Aring Patheen chawng khana keei nimiring
Patheen chawng rakham nakhan kading.

3. Patheen chawng rakham na kading tun kejet
Rutuk reetheeng narang khan thisen nepek
Krista inepek cham neeinakhan kading
Patheen chawng rakham nakhan kading.

4. Chawng rakham nakhan kading pur lai nawning
Pha rikip ratha inokoi ra-ngai rang
Pumi deeng kineeilim le saw kumuchum
Patheen chawng rakham nakhan kading.
