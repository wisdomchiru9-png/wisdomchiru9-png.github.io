347. I Need Thee Every Hour S.S. 577.
Ps. 86:1.
Key A/Flat

1. Kana ra-ngai jawn jai, numpok Pumi
Nang ang ah he raw-awi, raachak awm mak.
Pumi nangkha pha rikip
Kana ra-ngai jawn jai
Raavantha nepero
Nangdeeng kung ah kahawng.

2. Kana ra-ngai jawn jai, naw-awm pui ro
Lem na khan lut naw ning, Nang nurung le

3. Kana-ra-ngai jawn jai, Ningai sawna.
Nang nedep nawk atu, kathi rang keeng.

4. Kana ra-ngai jawn jai, naracha ro.
Nangchawng rakham na keei, deeng nepe ro.

5. Kana ra-ngai jawn jai, Reetheeng chungchon.
Nangta namachangro, Oh, Kupumi.
