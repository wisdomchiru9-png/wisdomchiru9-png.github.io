280. A Song Of Salvation              S.S. 1182.

                  Jan. 3:16.

Key G/Flat.

1. Nakhekpa numpok    kejet
    Makha sindawng roi numpok inih.
    Ravan naput jong chum kha
    Keeideeng numpok mirit jarra nih.

           Awh, idik keeirang ah nih

           Dik, keeirang ah ni

           Dawng awmboi Pumi numpok jarra

           Nasaan rang Cross ah kecirang nithit pe joi.

2. Thisok ah saw reeveei kha kejet
    Idawr mo Cross ah sawdoi ah thi
    Mun rikip ah nang reei joi maw?
    Keeirang sawna inodoi pe kha?

3. Ravan naput keeideeng neereei
    Jisu nai lim na muk rang nih
    Ravan na awm narang ah mun
    Keeirang nuru rasam pe ni maw?

4. Kinawrawdawn namasang ro
   Nang niboi ah tudeeng mo se ning?
    Nakhekpa le Ravan ram mun
   Nang ratha khana neereei rawng maw?
