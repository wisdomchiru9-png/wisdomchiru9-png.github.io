244. Safe Within The Vail              S.S. 938.

                 Heb. 6:19.

Key G.

1. Theeira awm na ramkha nai joi
   Mo-ah achoiboi sawbo
   Aring tui long na jawn jai
   Ravan kawmchang kumuk joi.

           Rithi khopui chi nawning

           Kumsawt ringna vara khan

           Thirchuk jong mathak ah

           Makhan keei kerekhe joi.

2. Rokong chum kaheei pha ah
   Enro manina najap
    Patheen salangdar ra-ngai ro
   Abangboi miri hoikha.

3. Rokong muchum nakhan serawi
    Dangka ang ah var kawmchang
   Tuipui rilim hawng rat a-awm
    Nivar khana a-eh chak.

4. Lemna kholok nara kan le
    Sawna ringkho hi hek le
    Ringna Lungpum kha mapak rawng
    Inn teeng nawchawi rang nijoi.
S.S.650.
