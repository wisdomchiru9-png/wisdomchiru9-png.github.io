59. Just A Word For Jesus            S.S. 1111.

                 Ps. 71:17.

Key D/Flat.

1. Tun Jisurang chawng khat te
    Adik nanglawmpa nih
    Mana imo nuru thawpe
    Nawhawng me jet rang ro.

           Tun Jisu rang chawng khat te

           Keeini nasaan narang

           Jisu rangkhan chawng khat te

            Lah le niik na neeiro

 2. Tun Jisurang chawng khat te
     Munu napai pejoi

     Ravan Inn sok ngir rang.

 3. Tun Jisurang chawng khat te
     Reel ngamboi Cross jarkhan
     Kunumpok Nakhekpa na
     'Ma ringkho nepek joi.

 4. Tun Jisurang chawng khat te
      Apha mathang noro
      Mulung net boi chonthaw kha
      Sawna rang rok khanih.

  5. Tun Jisurang chawng khat te
      Nang tak ah sawn dawk le
      Dawk nakha hawngchawi tana
