102. Beloveth Now We Are The Sons Of God

                                                S.S. 504,

                  John 3:2.

Key C/Flat

1. Patheen sapa mirit tak
   Jisu numpok na chawng relek
    Asapa jarra numuk
    'Sapa chang nakha nepek.

           Lawmdit keeini Patheen nai nani joi

           Imo sokni ti, keeini tun jet mak meh

           Ama jonglar le nezet rang nih (2)

           Keeini Amale ra-ang rang nanih.

           Ama ang khan numuk rang nih.

2. Phortha nimining kha var joi
    Patheen na mavar pengjoi
    Avaar napeng munhoi khan
    Nakhekpa meh kha muk rang.

3. Numpok na nimijir le
    Ama meh atha kha ang rang
    Chawng rakham na rusur joi
    'Ma ningai nakha tum rang.
