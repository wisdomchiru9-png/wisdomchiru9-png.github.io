  61. Singing Glory           3rd Alex. 49.

                     Col. 3:16.

  Key F.

  1. Jisu inepek kha kumulung ah awm joi
      Ningkip Ama mapak na lah kawthaw jawt
      Karatha nakhek pe-ah kacham ah natet
      Tun ningai na lah kha kawthaw jawt.

              Mana ningkip kesena lam namavar pe

             Kopok rik namajang pe kalam rikip khan

             Ama chawngkha kiningai na khani juirang

             Pha rikip ningai lah kha thaw rang.

 2. Nakhekpa lawm narang kener namajang pe
     Khan ningkip mapak na lah kawthaw jawnjai
     Jisu sawna le Ama lamkha keereei jawt
     Khan ningai lah kha keeina kawthaw.

3. Nakhekpa na kaka chawihi namajang pe
    Khan ningkip mapak na lah kawthaw jawn jai
    Lungpum chungkhan keke sun awm nemenet pe
    Ningai lah thaw kha mohot nawning.

4. Oh, mulung saw ah ratha pokrik aphung hai
   Ningkip mapak na lah hi nangthaw jawt maw?
   Nakhekpa deeng hawng lut ro, 'Male rachang ah
   Ningai lah hi nangthaw jawt rang nih.
