32. If God Be For Us
S.S. 508
Rome 8:31.
Key G/Flat.

Pumi mapak ro, 'Ma numpok na awi ro
Mirawp karra namadam joi
Thisen na nakhek imo chit narang awm?
Tuntu Jisu nata changjoi.

Patheen Amana,
Keeinirangkhan naw awm pui chak ala
Tumo narahapui? Tumoni?
Tumo keeini naraha pui rang?

Pumi rang rat ro, 'Madeengkhan ningai ro
Adik chang rangro ningthingkip
Ratro athaboina narahapuilai
'Mana narachang pui rang nih.

'Ma chawngkhan dingro, chawng rakham adik nih
Khrista deengkhan nirang roso
Reeleeihi thang'awm adoi rat urang ro
Mahi meejeek ah ru awm nih.

Pumideeng awmro, ina kaw na sungkhan
Kumsawt ah ring narang put joi
Ama khut le adik hai a-saar rang nih
Tute na athaw ngam awm mak.
