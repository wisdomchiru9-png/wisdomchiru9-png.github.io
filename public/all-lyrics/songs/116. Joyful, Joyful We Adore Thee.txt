116. Joyful Joyful We Adore Thee
Key G.

1. Ningai, ningai ah kupumi
Chubai kanabawk ujoi
Nangdeeng kungkhan reeipar ang-ah
Mulung pama keeneei uh
Munu, sawna rakhana lam
Azing hi napai pero
Kumsawl ningai na-pe Pumi
Keeinihi namavar ro.

2. Nangchon kholok ningai na sip
Reeleei Ravan na varjoi
Arshi le Ravantir haina
Nang mapak na lah thaw uh
Mole phai ah ram rikip ah
Tuipui chung khan awm var joi
Va maram khan tui miri na
Ningai rangkhan nokoi uh.

3. Kumsawt rang ra-ngaidam nape
Phortha kha nape nang nih
Ningai tak ah saw muchum na
Kiring na nangte nang nih
Kapa, Patheen, Aleen Khrista
Numpok ringna kha nang nih
Ringna nemejet uro.

4. Kumsawt ningai na lah hoikha
   Zingkar Arshi var angkhan
   Keeinina numropo rep ah
   Lah kha thaw jawn jai urawng
   maton sawn rang lah kha thaw ah
   Mawtawr nakha neei ngir rang
   Ningai lah hoi khana keeini
   Mawtawr narang nakhin peh.
