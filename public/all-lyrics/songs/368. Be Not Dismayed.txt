368. Be Not Dismayed              Alex. 2. CSL. 242.

           Phil. 4:6 & 1.Pet. 5:7.

Key 'B/Flat

1. Sawna rana mun rikip khan
   Mulung mawsaw noro
   'Ma matha le nethe rang ni
   Na w raw tawp pui rang niik.

          Mulung mawsaw noro

          Sena hawngna mun rikip ah

          Nangkha Nupumi   na

           Nawrawtawp pui rang nih.

2. Chonthaw ah saw mulung koi hai
   Noroi jawn jai rang nih
    Nang lawn narang ah lam khawk awm
    nawrawtawp pui rang nih.

3. Nang idit lim nepek rang nih
    Tak ah nangsawn atu
    nang ravat na awm lai nawni
    nawr tawtawp pui rang nih.

4. Nanglawm hai na namathan awm
    Na rawtawp pui rang nih
    Mulung rachaak nepek rang nih
    Mulung mawdaw ng noro.

 5. Lem na khadawrkhan rawp a-awm
    Nawrawtawp pui rang nih
    Pumi te kha beeirep rang ro
    Nang mawtawr ngir rang nih.
