414. Christian Awake             C.P. 38.

1. Khristen hai nang ratha see lai noro
   Thoi urang ro, Nasaanpa jong sok na
   Zingkar hoikha lam rujawng u rang rawi
   Chungteeng Ravaantir haina mapak u
   Rapa masa ningai mosok ujoi
   Jongsok Patheen sapa kha a-lawm uh.

2. Ke-ngam roihaina sawmuchum pha\
   Zaan ah 'mani ja hoi ulai tak khan
   Ravaantir avaar na jong mothoi joi
   Khrista 'sokna chawnghoi vak sem rang khan
   Reetheeng ratha chawngkha zet ule khan
   Chawng hoikha asem rang khan se ujoi.

3. Khristen hai thoiro, ja ulai noro
   Bethlehem ah nangni kha munu ah put
   Nakhek rang khan Khrista kha jongsok joi
   Ke-ngam roihai deeng reetheeng ratha na
   Chawngtha hi asem rang khan jongreei joi
   Thoi utana ningai mosok rangro.

4. Thoi urang ro, Khrista rumuk pui rang
   Ke-ngam roihai awm ke-ngam se mak ah
    Ratha seet neeiboi pet ah se ujoi
   Thoi urang ro, Khrista na Reng nijoi
    'Ma Rengmun reeleei ah jong malar joi
    Jisu rengchang joi, ningai urang rawi.
