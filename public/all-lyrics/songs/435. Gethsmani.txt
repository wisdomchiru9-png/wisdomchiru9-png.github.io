435. Gethsmani

1. Jisuna Gethsamani rohon na vaak nik joi
Niik pha ahukha thi-ah sok (Keeinirang)
Jisu thatrang khan navak sur ujoi 'ti kha ruzet
'Ma khaichawi Judas Iscariot ijor nih.
Manmeeseh munuphung hai rang nithit pe
Cross chung ah thisen le
Munuphung hai niring naret rang khan
Enro, keeini thisen khale nawrawchawk joi
Munu aput nakhek joi.
(Parts: Anu juichak uh.)

2. Jisu kha sur u-ah Kaijapha deeng chawi ujoi
Jisu kha 'jar atawk pe u (That narang)
Peterna Jisu asaw kha muk le rurapha joi
Peter voithum rapha le aar hawngkhong joi.

3. Jisukha Pilaat rengdeeng chawngpui saat rang chawi u joi
"Rengsaapa kani ti" thaat ro (Ra-aai uh)
Makhan Pilaat nu-ngak na Pilaat deeng chawngmuruk reei
"Jisu kha" jarboi keeng" sawna penoro.

4. Pilaat na Jisu thaat narang ajaar muk pemak
Rahapui haina jawt umak (Thaat ro ti)
Barnaba mosok ro "Jisu thaat ro" ti-ah ra-aai
Jisu kha Cross ah thaatrang khan chawi ujoi.
