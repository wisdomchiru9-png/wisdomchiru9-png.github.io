132. Jesus Only       S.S. 711.

                Matt. 17:8.

Key A/Flat.

 1. Sawna reemeeina nubuk awm
    Keetheen te-ah ka lawn awm
     Renro keereeveei ah awm
     Ningai nakha thang so awm
      Jisu tekha keeneei atu
      Kineei Jisu avaar hi
      Bethlehem Arshi nek ah awm
       Avaar det Jisu kha nih.

   2. Reeleei khomijin ringkho hin
       Sawna rok keereeveei awm

        Ringkho reeipartha katawk lai

        kha ruhut riling kumuk

        Jisu tekha keeneei atu

        Alu det neei kumuk joi

         Makha phaijaw tuili tak ah

         Par Sharon reeipar khanih.

      3. Kiringkho hi hawnghek sole

          Lawmdit haina namak awm

          Nunseh chawngrok kejet ah awm

          Sawna rilip dawdawn nah

           Jisu tekha keeneei atu

           Dawngse naw-awm pui rang nih

           Kinik na kumuk ngam nawk awm

           Ravantir na nawpawm rep.

        4. Ningai lam ah chunteeng vung ah

            Ina-ngak na mawkawt khan

             Jisu deengkhan keereei atu

    Kawt nawmawlawng pe rang nih
    Lah thaw hai karra ka-awm le
    Ravantir haikha muk rang
    Maanrawp det tu Jisu khani
    Lah le awichak rang kanih.
