104. To The Work          S.S. 751.

                 Matt. 21:28.

Key F.

1. Chonthaw ro, chonthawro keeini Patheen nahai
   Zuirawi keeini deeng Pumi 'neereei lamdik kha
   Ama chawngpek khan keeini ranak thar loi ah
   Ranak mosok tai ah nithaw rang kha thaw rawi.

           Chonthaw rawi (2)

           Nupumi chon thaw rawi

           Pumi jong malalim

           Ani le roroi ah ngak rawi.

2. Chonthawro vawncham adoihai vawnsip narang
   Asaw doihai ringna tuidung khan seroso
   Cross khana keeini ningai nakha nirang nih
   Manboi ringna chawngtha keeini nejet phakhan

3. Chothawro chonthawro vanoi mikholok na
   Adik boi azing khap rengmun kha thang narang
   Jehovah riming hi rasang det niroso
    Manboi ringna chawngtha keeini nejet phakhan

4. Chonthawro, chonthawro, Pumi raat na khale
    Reng lukhum kha keeini nochon thawlaw kha nih
    Tak ah sawn hai awm na mun keeini rang ah ni
    Manboi ringna chawngtha keeini neereei phakhan
