 330. The Banner Of The Cross               S.S. 675.

                  Ps. 9:4.

 Key B/Flat

 1. Ravan Rengpa Ponlap kha jong melek joi
    Rengpa raami hai deeng khan
   Atha ponlap lawmrang vonhin nepek joi
   Kheksa hai lah thaw ang ah.

           Serangro...lawn rang ro

           Khrista rang nangthang veeka-awm

           Rengpa kha lukhum mukhu ro

           Cross ah ponlap kha thoi ah.

2. Tuileen ang ah narahapui ruchun awm
   Nimining kha koi a-awm
   Pumi raamiring khat ah ruchun retro
   Adik khan awm rep uro.

3. Ramma tui ah miringhai awm na munkhan
   Chawnghoi hi mezet rang ro
   Pumi ponlap risimkha reeipe rang ro
   Nupumi ta    chang rang ni.

4. Ningai kho hawngvar le nara naipui joi
   Jongnai   det joi ningthing kip
   Rengpa maikung ah narahapui thangjoi
   Cross na reeleei mawtawr joi.
