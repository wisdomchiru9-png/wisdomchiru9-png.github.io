1. Taitak teeng ni jonglut joi
    Reeleei ravan thim sojoi
    Jansawt nik napha neeiro
    Avar na Ravan lai awm avar sojoi.

            Reetheeng (3) Pumi Patheen

            Ravan Reeleei ah nang sip

            Nang kha rasang ah kanamapak pumi

 2. Ringna pumi kha nang nih
     Manmeeseh ah nang inn khan
     Nangdeeng nuruchun  uro
     Nangdeeng kungkhan awm jawn jai rawng.

 3. Azing rilip releng pha
     Numpok mulung na nawpawm
     Mirit lurna Arshina, Nangmaikha na-eh pe-ah mulung parjoi.

 4. Sun le jan na chung Arshi
     Bangboi ah kunumuk chak
     Vantir pumi kunumuk
     Zingkar rok niroso, ajing awm noroso.
