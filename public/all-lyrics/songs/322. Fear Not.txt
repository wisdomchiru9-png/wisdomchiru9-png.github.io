322. Fear Not        S.S. 811.

                 Gen. 15:1.

Key A.

1. Patheen awm chi noro
   'Ma pho kha nata nih
   Ranak na mawtawr joi
   Pumi hi ranak nih.

           Chinoro patheen chawng

           Nangdeeng neereei joi

           Nang Pumi khrista deeng khan

           Ningai ah lawm rang ro.

2. Patheen awm chinoro,
    Nangsaw na hekha jet
    'Ma chawngrachak khana
    Ratha raavan nepek.

 3. Chitna neei ah noro
    'Mana naw awm puichak
    Narakan pui rang nih
    Ranak nepek rang nih.

 4. Mitawm hai chinoro
    Nasaanpa jong rang nih
    Ningaina bangboi ah
    Inn teeng nawchawi rang nih.
