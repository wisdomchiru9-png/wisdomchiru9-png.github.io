514. SECURITY
(Rangam napui)
Nupumi riming ah nang ningai uro,
Keeina keeneereei thep uret, nang ningai uro.
NUPUMI RIMING AH NANGNINGAI URO:
Nang nina nang idit u pok kholok,
Mulung ratha repe ah, Niik na le roroi ah
Patheen deengkhan reei uro.
IRIKIP AZET VEEK PATHEEN NA;
NANG MULUNG MINING U
KHA NOJONG RENEM MASAAK PERANG NIH.
Khan Khrista Jisu jarra hoina parna nangmuk urang nih.
NIKHO-NIKHO AH CHON SOK HAI KHA;
NAWHAWNG MEENEEI AH;
NAMATON RANG NEZET NGAM NAWK A-AWM.
Khrista riming ah Patheen na inokoi kha le roroi ah,
Chungteeng ah tum kha muk narang keeina ke-ngen
Lawpkhat ah:
KEEI AH PUT, NANGNINA NANG IJET UH, NANG IMUK U
NANG INEEI U KHOLOK KHA DUNG JUI AH THAW URO;
KHANIT ATU ARACHAK PATHEEN NA;
NANG NIKHA PHA LE MUN RIKIP
AH NAWRAWTAWP PUI CHAKU RANG NIH.
