264. In A Little While We're Going Home
S.S. 832.
Dan. 12:23.
Doh is F.

1. Ning namangai masak perang lah kha thawro
Awm bawt le inn teeng hawng rang nanih
Hektik neeiboi nikho khan jan awm lai naw nih
Keeini inn khan awm rang nanijoi.
Pha bawngte sung hin (2)
Tuipui let kha rakan na
Rithi khoplui bangle dawngkhit ah rumu rang
Keeini inn khan awm rang nanijoi.

2. Nukhut na ithaw ngam tuk chon kha keeinina thawrawi
Awm bawt le inn teeng hawng rang nanih
Patheen numpok khana ningkip ranak nepek
Keeini inn khan hawngrang nanih.

3. Kethawt narang boi lamkha thareei ah kon rang
Awm bawt le inn teeng hawng rang nanih
Numropok na mulung par kha resem som ah
Keeini inn khan awm rang nanih.

4. Rweingam boi saw muchum na ningheen na munkhan
Awm bawt le keeini hawng rang nanih
Khopui hoi var khan mik rithi cho naw nih
makha inn khan awm rang nani joi.
