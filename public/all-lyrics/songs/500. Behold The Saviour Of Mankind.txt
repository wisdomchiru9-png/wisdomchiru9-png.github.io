500. Behold The Saviour Of Mankind S.S. 151.
Jn. 1:29.
Key Flai.

1. Enro, miring hai Nakhekpa
Jak awm thing ah ruthi
Ituk ah leen numpok moni
Nangrang thisok ah thi.
Patheen ke-ngam nun renem
Cross ah thi Jisu kha
Nun renem Jisu athi ah put khan
Hawngring ret rang ah nih.

2. 'Ma asaw hekha ra-ngai ro
Ranaak neei kholok pur
Innpui rasaang kholok pur joi
Anet lungkha koijoi

3. Maanrawp tak ah nawrawchak joi
Ratha rang khan ra-aai
Enro, Ama lu mukun joi
Lu mukun na thijoi.

4. Athi kha i-awm rusawn mak
Ningai lurna khana
Patheen ke-ngam rusaw hainaw:
Numnopok jarra nih.
