262. The New Glory Song R.S. 932.
Key E/Flat.

1. Azingtheei mik hi na en-ngam boi ahoi khopui khat awm
Nisun var nek ah vardet re te lang joi
Rangkachak lam, lungraka hawr, aring jin thingkung kha awm
Jisu mapak uh Ravan na Rengchang joi.
Lurna Renglukhum, Chungsok na khut maja
Manrawp tingtang le mawtawr na lah thaw
Hossana ti-u-ah mapak uh
Lurna ramkhan maipar tum chawi ah hawngrang.

2. Sawna meeisa rakan u-ah kheksa hai kumsawt hoi muk
Ramang se-awm jetboi rawchawibak he chun
Reeleei hoi na kha vaitek ah mining a numukma-awm
Adik neeitin Ravan ramkhan thang nawnih.

3. Ravanram khopui le ningai lurna kha tum ngam rawng maw?
Tak ah sawn hairang mun rasam na awm joi
Jisuna munu rusuk rang khan renem tak ah nokoi
Ravan ram ah munkha narasam pejoi.

4. 'Madeeng nava atu akip numpok chawng rakham na awm
Meei ra-ah khana adik kha madan ngam
Ringkho sawna kha chungteeng ah numpok khana mawtawr
ngam
Ra mawtawr hai Ravan ram khan hawng rang roh.
