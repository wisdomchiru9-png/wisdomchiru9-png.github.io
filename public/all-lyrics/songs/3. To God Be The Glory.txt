3. To God Be The Glory              S.S. 23

                   Ps. 29:2

Key A/Flat.

1. Patheen lur roso ajeng chonpui thaw joi
    Manmeeseh numpok jarra 'Sapa nepek
    Munu reetheeng narang 'Ma ringkho nepek
    Mikholok rang ringna mawkawt mawlawng joi.

            Pumi kha mapak ro Reeleei ningai roso

            Pumi kha mapak ro

            Vanoi ningai roso

            Oh, Jisu aput

             'Pa Patheen deeng hawngro

             Ajeng ah leen chon kha

             Amana thaw joi

 2. Oh, thisen le nawrawchawk ah nakhek joi
     Tak ah sawn hai deeng Patheen na chawng rakham
     Mitha boi khana dik tak ah Patheen kha
     Tak ah sawn phakhan ra-ngai dam muk rang nih.

  3. Chonpui iruthaw kha keeini nemejet
     Jisu aput ningai rasang numuk joi
      Reetheeng rasang le aleen det niroso
      Jisu meh numuk le ningai rang nanih.
