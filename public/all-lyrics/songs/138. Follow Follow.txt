   138. Follow Follow            S.S. 529. CSL.63.

                    St. Jn. 12:26.

   Key A/Flat.

  1. Phaijaw khan Nakhekpa le rawtawp ah lawn rang
      Tui long na jawnjai reeipar parna munkhan
      Mun rikip ah Ama sena juirang kanih.
      Reng lukhum kurukhu malalim juirang.

             Juirang...juirang...Jisu juirang kanih

             Khose mun rikip khan awm juirang kani

             Juirang juirang...Jisu juirang kani

             Mun rikip ah Ma anu juirang kani.

2. Phaijaw kha Nakhekpa le rawtawp ah lawn rang
    Rithi khopui rang zing tuilong nakhan
    'Ma khutna numukui chitna neeilai nawning
    Pumi le kawrawtawp putsee munaw-ning.

3. Phaijaw khan arasang det lawnchung khit se khan
    Nakhekpa kedep ah karatha ringjoi.
   '
    Ma senakhan keeihi manakai pe rang nih
   Patheen awm na vanoi ruchun na munkhan.
