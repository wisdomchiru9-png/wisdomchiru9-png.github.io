378. The New Year           S.S. 1049.

                 Ps. 31:3.

Key D/Flat.

1. Kumthar hi phor jongpe rang ro
   Ruchun mirikip deeng
   Nangchawng pon na reeseei urang
   Nangta namachang ro.

2. Nangthisen na ke-ngam rawchawk
   Angar mulung hin chap rawng
   Mikchaw hai awm mavar pero
   Nangkha numu rang roso.

3. Nangchonthaw kuput narang hin
   Ranak thar nepe uro
   Azing rakhana, chitna hi
   Chawi so veek rang roso.

4. Vanoideeng phor nepe uro
   Namapak rangkhan koiro
   Ruchun lim chawngkung chang roso
   Nang numpok naihai hi.
