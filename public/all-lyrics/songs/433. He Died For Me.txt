433. He Died For Me

1. Kumunu phung jarra Nupumina
Saw renro reeveei nodoi peh
Kese narang lam namavaar pe
Aserang kani joi Jisu deeng.
Kithit narang mukhan,
Jisuna nithit pe
Mi-engseet kha awm nodoi pe
Saan ngam laimak Pailaat na Jisu kha.

2. Chawi ujoi thit narang ah mun sekhan
Cross ruput lam ah jem ujoi
'Vit narang seei le anu jui uh
Unu Mary na anu jui lai.

3. Calvary lawn na jisu thit so le
Tuirahai awm menem pe joi
Raai rekhek tawnghai dam na muk joi
seetna neei hailim ningai muk joi.
