152. Only A Step To Jesus

1.Sam. 20:21.
Key Flat.

1. Jisudeeng senarang
Kakhat se chawi mak che lai
Seetna mulung lek tana
Nasaanpa deeng hawngro.
Kachawi khat te, hawngthawt rang ro
Pumina na-ngak
Seetna mulung lek tana
Khan ringna nangmuk rang nih
Manboi ah nang imuk
Ringna kha mathang noro.

2. Jisu kha tak ah sawn ro
Ringna nangmuk rang ni
Rangaidam pek rang khan
Numpok tak ah na-ngak lai.

3. Munu ah put numpok
Muk narang kakhat te nih
Pha hek mala nang mulung ah
Imo nang mining.

4. Ka khat tese thawt ah
Jisudeeng reei mak chelai
nasaanpa deengkhan
Ningai tak ah hawng narang khan.
