339. Arise My Soul              M.B.C. 209.

1. Manmeese hi munu sip joi.
    Makha kar khan karatha hi,
    Kumunu hina naw mawsaw ah,
    Saw reeveei tak ah ka awm.

2. Munu tuipui thuk na tak khan,
    Kalut ah athang kanijoi,
    Kasaw hi ne-en ngam lai mak ah,
    Sawdoi tak ah keei na khek.

3. Nakhek pa saw na reeveei kha
    Mining rang ro Khristen miring,
    Athangrang ratha tampui awm joi
    Thoi tana tun reei pero.

4. Khrista 'thikha tak ah sawn ro
    Nakhekpa chawng mathan noro
    Athi aput nawhawng ring pejoi
    nang le keei niring narang.

5. Khut rawbaw ah chubai bawk ro
   Mulung lut ah 'Madeeng niro
   Khrista Jisu na nojong loi pha
   Ningai ah rawtawng pui rang.

6. Meeikhur kha nang dit nawk ah tu
    Nakhekpa Pumi deeng hawng ro
    Nawt tak ah Khrista jongchum pha khan
    Mulung lek na pha awm mak.
