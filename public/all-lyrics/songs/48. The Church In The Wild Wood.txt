48. The Church In The Wild Wood (D.H. 154)
K.L. 45. MBC. 233.
Key B/Flat.

1. Sindawngroi arachak lawn rasang khan
Numpok awmtak Beek inn khat ru-awm
Makha kuruthoi na Beek Inn kha nih
Ituk dawr ah numpok awm moni ?

Oh, hawng ruchun ro
Pumi Beek Inn khan hawng lut ro
Numpok awm Beek Inn hin hawng lut ro
Mahi nek hin atha chungchon det,
Munsik thadet kha awm lai mak.

2. Khatuk ah en awm Beek Inn vaiteengkhan
Reeipar rikip na a-eh veek joi
Ituk ah numulung apar rang mo
Hi-ang ah atha munsik awm lai mak.

3. Khristana keeinihi nojong koi le
Avar chungchon Inn sok narang khan
Beek Inn naput ah kathawt rang kanih
Ituk dawr ah numpok awm moni ?
