239. What Must It Be To BeThere!                S.S.92

                  Rev. 21:4

Key C.

1. Ahoi ram chawng jar khan neereei
   Makhan avar ahoi kha sip
   Patheen jengna kha lar jawn jai
   Khan na-awm le imo ang ni?

           Khamunkhan... (2)

           Naw-awm pha khan imo ang nih?... (2)

           Khamun khan (2)

           Naw-awm phakhan imo ang nih?

2. Rangkachak lam jar khan neereei
    Makha manlu lung na roko
    Ahoi daan kha reei ngam mak meh
    Khan na-awm le imo ang nijh?

3. Numpok rachak jarkhan neereei
    Reng reeleen na khale reeleen
    Chungteeng ningai lah kha thaw rang
    Khan na-awm le imo ang nih?

4. Munu reetheeng jar khan neerei
    Sawna rana lem na 'put
    Mulung sawna awm na boi ram
    Khan na-awm le imo ang nih?
