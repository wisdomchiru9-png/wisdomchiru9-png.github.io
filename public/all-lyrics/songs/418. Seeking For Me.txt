418. Seeking For Me             S.S. 40.

                    Ezek. 34:11.

   Key G.

   1. Nakhek Jisu Bethlehem ma jongsok
      Seh-kawr saksak na rokong ah maja
      Oh, idawrra num inopok moni?
      Natawk keeihi keeihi (3)
     Oh, idawr relek num pok miningro
     Natawk keeihi keeihi.

 2. Nakhek Jisu Calvary thing Cross ah
     Kitawk kholok kha nawchaw pe sojoi
     Oh, hidawr ah i-ngam hi relek nih
    Jongthi keeirang keeirang (3)
    Oh ituk dawr ra imawtawr moni?
    Keeirang nojongthit-peh.

 3. Nakhekpa Jisu tu nga ngai a-awm
    Ramla tak ah keei kuruchai jarkhan
    Renemtak ah karatha nokoi pe
   Nokoi keeihi keeihi (3)
   Ama mi numpok na he raw awi le
   Nokoi keeihi nokoi.

4. Nakhekpa Jisu jongchum ret rang nih
   Chawng rakham na sawna kum thang so le
   Ravan naput jongchum muk rang kani
  Keeirang jongret rang nih (3)
  Ravan naput jongchum muk rang kanih.
  Keeirang jongret rang nih.
