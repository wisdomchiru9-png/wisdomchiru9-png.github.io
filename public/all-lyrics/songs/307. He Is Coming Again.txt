307. He Is Coming Again               R.S. 842.
Key F.

1. Relek numpok misaan na risim
   Nakhekpa Jisu chungteeng ah put jong
   Numpok ah nakhek 'Ma lurna
   Keeina mahi tum ngir rang kani.

           Numpok Nakhek na relek risim

           Lah hi thawro, thaw ret ro

           Numpok nakhek Ma lurna reeiro

           Jisuna jongkir thep ret rang nih.

2. Numpok ah nakhek adik tak ah
   Jisuna nithit pe ah hawng ring joi
   Numpok ah nakhek sindawng awm hai
   Kumsawt ringna keei deeng nepek joi.

3. Numpok ah nakhek ah keereetheeng joi
    Khristana chungteeng hawng ah ru-awm joi
    Numpok ah nakhek na lah kawthaw
    Ama Rengmun kha keeina muk rang.
