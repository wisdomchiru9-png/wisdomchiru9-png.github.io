  76. Our Christian Band             S.S. 1108.

                     Ps. 39:3.

 Key E/Flat.

 1. Ningai mapak na lah kha le
     'Ma deeng lur tak ah mapak rawi
     'Ma khut le lam nakai pejoi
     Numpok lukhum numukhu joi.

             Mulungkhat pumkhat ah, ningai tak ah ding rawi

             Munu saw reeveei doi, athang vak rawkhawm rawi

             Ma riming kha sem rawi, nochon kha minit rawi

            Jisu relek numpok nale, misaan na reeirang rawi.

2. Lawp khat ah nuruchun lai khan
    Numpok lawmdit rawjawt som ah
    Ningnit na lim chungteeng hawng ah
    'Mana nidit limkha nepek.

3. Naite hai lawmpa Pumi nih
   Kachawina nemejet rang nih
   'Ma khut ranak khale keeini
   Khristen rawlawp na nurung joi.
