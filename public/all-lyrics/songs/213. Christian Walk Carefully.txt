213. Christian Walk Carefully
Eph. 4:1
Key G.

1. Khristen hai riring tak ah lawn uro
Nang sena lamkha thit narang naitek
Milem midawi lim khan lut naboi rang
Munu ah mocho rang nawmawkawi nih
Khristen hai riring ro
Nang lawn na rikip khan
Riring tak ah lawn ro
Thit na naitek

2. Riringro, rithi khopui kar khan awm
Zingkhap phakhan chit nang mopong a-awm
Nawmawsaw haikha thang so rang nijoi
Nakhekpa le saw nang muchum rang nih.
Khristen hai nang lawn pha
Mulung mapar uro
Rithi khopui rang awm
Chi unoro.

3. Khristen hai niichak ro, pur u tini
Nakhekpa kha nang meeneei u-atu
Nanglawn nakhan daan-en na rawp rang nih
Niik na kha raapon na reeseei chak ro.
Khristen hai nanglawn pha
Anii areei raat ro
Riringro pur tini
Niichaak uro.

4. Khristen hai ra-ngam rep ah lawn uro
   Sawmuchumna munkha nangsok rang nih
   Makhan chawngsaat le nangtum rophong joi
   Ningai tak ah hawngro Nupumi deeng.
   Khristen hai nanglawn pha
    Ra-ngam rep ah lawn ro
    Sawmuchum na munkha
    Nangmuk rang nih.
