355. I Must Tell Jesus C.C. 293, y

1. Kithaw chon kholok Jisu deeng reei rang
Munu pok rik hi phung-ngam mi-ing.
Kawsaw nakhan 'Mana nasan rang nih,
'Mana nakaw rep khan num nopok.
in
Jisu deeng reei rang.. (2)
Munu pok rik hi phung ngam mi-ing
Jisu deeng reeirang.. (2)
Jisu tena keeihi nasan ngam.

2. Kawsawna kholok Jisudeeng reeirang
Num napok adik kawlawm kha nih
Keeina keereei le khan nasan rang nih
Sawna kholok awm nasan rang nih.

3. Anakhekpa deeng khan lut rang kanih
Tute na keei nasan rang awm mak
Jisudeeng te keeina reei rang kanih
Kawsawna kha napai perang nih.

4. Manmeese athaboi na nubuk awm
Munu khan kumulung awm a awm
Jisudeeng te khan keeina keereei le
Manmeesehi mawtawr rang kanih.
