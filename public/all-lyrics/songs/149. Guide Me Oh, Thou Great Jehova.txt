149. Guide Me Oh Thou Great Jehovah.
Key A/Flat.                          3rd Alex. 281.

1. Lam nakai pero Jehovah,
   Kelengna reeleei chung hin
   Keeila adawk, Araat nangni
   Nangbaan rat le noroi ro
    Ravan saak le, ravan saak le,
   Kawkhawp maseng navai ro (2)

2. Aring tui nomolong pero
   Damna tuikha nepero
   Reemeei chawpui le najap ro
   Kawlawn na mun rikip ah
   Araat chungchon keei nakhek ro
   Karanak kopho nang ni (2)

3. Jordon tuipui rawnawng chung khan
   Chitna boi rang nepero
   Tui reher na numukui ro
   Canan ram kosok maseng
   Ningai lah le, ningai lah le,
    Bangboi ah namapak rawng (2).
