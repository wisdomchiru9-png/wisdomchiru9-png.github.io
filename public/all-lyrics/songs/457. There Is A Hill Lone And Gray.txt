457. There Is A Hill Lone And Gray                  R.S. 82.
Key E/Flat

1. Athim raamhoi khat awm, jengreei a-la inih
   Tuipui athuk kawmchang vara khan
   En awm Ravan thoi ah, athirang khan chawi joi
   Reeleei le nang rang le khan keeirang.

           Kumulung awm rujing

           Kimik rithi awm cho

           Kimuk raamthim kha kimining le

            En awm Ravan thoikhan

           Athi rangkhan chawi joi

           Reeleei le nangrangle khan keeirang.

2. Enro, reeleei sawna arik phung ah pur joi
    Riling lukhum rukhu ah vak lawn
    Cross phung ah lu mukun mipui karra pang joi
    Athim raamhoi lawn teengkhan kajoi

3. Ra-ngairo sek le ihem mirikha kejet
    Thing ah jotpi le achi ujoi
    Mipui na enlai khan Cross kha mothoi joi
    Ke-ngamte Calvary azing khan.

4. Rangchaat deeng sekhan awm eh-raw le munui uh
    Lawmhaina saw tak ah chap laikhan
    Asaan rang awmboi ah pur awm chawng reeimak
    Athim raam mun lawn chung khan pur joi.

5. Azing awm hawng var joi, lungpum awm hawng koijoi
   Sawna he kha Ravan le rehem
   Makha he kha athi Nerengpa heso nih
   Dawng awmboi sawna khan Nasaanpa.

6. Ni-awm rethe roso, reeleei rawnawk roso
   Nakhek pana athat hai rangkhan
   Enro sawna karkhan, ke-ngamte Patheen jong ah
   Athi aput ah hawngring ret joi.
