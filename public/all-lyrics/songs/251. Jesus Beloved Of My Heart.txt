251. Jesus Beloved Of My Heart               S.S.646

                Canticles 2:16.

Key C.

1. Mulung ah num kipok Jisu
   Nang numpok mirit kha kaman.
   nang khut chawi kha kumuk so joi
   Numpok det ah rotok narawng.

           Mihaina Rangkachak khopui

           En-awm kha mu-urang rosoh

           Ratha sawna awm na boi mun

           Keeirang Ravan ram kha nangni.

2. Avardet reeleei numpok na
   Nemnamak nangta niboi ah
   Chungteeng rasang ving ah ke-en
   Relek nang meh kumuk pha khan.

3. Paradise ah ningai sip joi awm
    Reeipar thakha en awm joi awm
   Ravantir hehoi khana awm
   'Mulung rawthawk masak ngam mak.

4. Nangniboi ah teeitang avar na
   Karatha mavar ngam mak
    Nang letu reeleei hi var joi
    Ravan ningai nakha sip joi.
