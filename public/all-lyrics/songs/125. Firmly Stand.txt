125. Firmly Stan          S.S. 685.

                  Ps. 12:2.

Key E/Flat.

1. Reeleei rawchawk pha Patheen deeng dingro
   Rithi khopui hawngrang pha sekhan awm
   Lungpum tena ringna ranaak nepek
   Seetna navak dep pha khan.

           Lungpum chungkhan dingrawi

           Net tak ah dingrang rawi

           Khrista lungpum net chungkhan

           Narahapui karra sawna nodoi awm

           Rengmun na awm haile ding rawi.

2. Net tak ah dingrawi adik reetheeng khan
   Adik mulung tak ah sawn net na-le
   Reeleei diknaboi karhin lungpum na
   Nawmawtawr masak rang nih.

3. Adik khan dingrawi nasaan chak rang nih
   Sawt ah awm, nawmawtawr masaak rang nih
    Lungpum khana ringkho sawna munkhan
    Sawmuchum' masaak rang nih.
