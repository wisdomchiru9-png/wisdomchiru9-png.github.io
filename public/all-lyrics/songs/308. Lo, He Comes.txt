308. Lo, He Comes             S.S. 161.

                  Rev. 1:7.

Key B/Flat.

1. Enroh reemeei le jongchum joi
    Munuphung hai rang jar khan
    Mireetheeng bawp tampui awm uh
    Ra mawtawrna he mosok
    Halleluiya (3)
    Jisu Rengchang rang jong joi.

2. Mirikip na rumuk rang nih
    Rengpon ijong reeseei kha
    'Jor u-ah thing ah achi-uh
    Sheei le vit ah Cross ah that
    Lungjum ah chap (3)
    Messai ah na muni maw?

 3. Ahoi sumlawi miri phakhan
     Ravan reeleei awm nawnih
     Ama mitir lim thang rang nih
     Nokoi nikhan ra-ngai ro
     Chawngsat nakhan (2) hawng urang ro.
     Chawngsat nakhan hawng uroh.

 4. Sawt tak ah niru ngak chak uh
     Enroh, tun Pumi kha jongjoi
     Ravan na mireetheeng hai le
     Ravan lai ah rumu rang
     halleluiya (3)
      patheen meh numuk rang nih.

  5. Oh, Amen chubai bawk roso
      Rasang kumsawt Rengmun kha
      'Ma rat na le lurna loiro
      Ama Rengmun niroso
      Relentak ah (3)
      Nojong perang ro Pumi.
