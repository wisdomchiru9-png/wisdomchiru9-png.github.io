228. I Love Him R.S. 715.
Key D.

1. Reeleei ah mulung idit awm lai mak
Thisen jarkhan munu aput nasaan
Cross kungkhan kumulung kha rujum awm
Mawnrawp Jisu thina vur ang ah nuru suk.
Amakha num kopok
Mana num noropok
Calvary lawn na ringkho
Nawrawchawk pejoi.

2. Munu khana nuru ralaat masaak
'Lunghang jarra sok ah kuru awm ah
Ning kuru meeseet pe ah kuruchi
Tuntu Jisu thisen kha na nurusuk joi.

3. Kuruchai naput tun' noro so joi
Kimik ruchaw kha namavaar pe joi
Thitna 'put tun' Patheen deeng karingjoi
Reeleei kholok ah Ama rachaak kha reei ro.
