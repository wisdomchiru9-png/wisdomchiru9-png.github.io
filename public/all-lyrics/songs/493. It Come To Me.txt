493. It Come To Me                 S.S. 876.

                   Clo. 1:20.

Key A/Flat.

1. Nikho tha tak keeideeng jongsok
    Pumina sawna nilik pe
    Kerenro ree theei kholok kha
    'Mana Cross ah napai pe joi.

            Cross chungkhan pumikha kumuk

            Keeirang Ma thisen jong long joi

           Reetheeng numpok kha kumuk pha

           Tuntu Ama kata nijoi.

2. Ningai nikho keeideeng jongsok
    Kumunu nawchawi pe sojoi
   *Ma deeng tak ah kawsawn atu
   'Ma riming kha tawk jawnjai rang.

3. Nikho hoi kha keeideeng jongsok
   Kumulung ah Ama awmjoi
   Mulung mawkawt kha mawlawng ah
   Makhan lekse sok lai nawning.n
