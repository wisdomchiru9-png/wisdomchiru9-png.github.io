268. Pardon Peace And Power               S.S.780.
       Ps. 29:11. Acts. 1:81. Jer. 33:8.
Key 'B/Flat.

1. Pumideeng ningal boi rang maw?
   Nang ineei kha tero
   'Machawng tak ah sawn na reeiro
   Tampui nawbawm pejoi.

           Arachak ranak kha awm

           Reetheeng ahoi awm na munkhan

           Khrista le awm lim keeirang'ni

           'Madeeng ningai mapak na lah thaw rang.

2. Numpok jarra munu rikip
   Ra-ngaidam nepek joi
   Jisu thisen khan rachak awm
    Rachak kha kata nih.

3. Numpok na munu ranak khoi
    'Mana nepek veek joi
    Reetheeng ratha kha jong awm joi
    Kumunu reetheengjoi.

4. Ratha mawtawr ngam Puminih
    Ratha kha awm numuk
    Ama Khrista khutchawi khanih
   Tun numulung ah awm.

5. Tun phortha kha nurumuk joi,
    Naite haikha ang ah
    khrista na ningai na nepek
    'Ma chawng rakham na nih.
