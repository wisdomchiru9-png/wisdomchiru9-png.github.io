484. The Cleaning Fountain                  S.S. 125.

                  Zech. 13:1.

Key A/Flat

1. Enroh, ringna tuipui leen kha
    Enroh, chungteeng long khan
    Nakhekpa awm na teeng long
    Makhana munu kha vur ang ah chek rusuk.

            Ringna tuiput kha

            Vonhin jonglong joi

            Varo ringna adit limna

            Munu vak rusuk ro.

2. Jisu thitna Calvary Cross
    Mulungsaw reeveei doi
    Vak pairo nakhek na munkhan
     Makhan nang munu kha: vur ang ah chek rusuk.

 3. Damna ranak nang muk rang nih
     Ringna tuipui put khan
     Maan rawp thisen tak ah sawn ro
     Makhan nang munu kha, vur angah chek rusuk.

 4. Dawngkhit ah chawng kha jongsok pha khan
     Keeini nokoi pha khan
     Thisen ranga keeng nijui rang
     Makhan numunu kha, vur ang ah chek rusuk.

                                        3rd Alex. 407.
