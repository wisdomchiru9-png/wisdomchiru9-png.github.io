250. Fairer Then The Morning              S.S.98.

               Solomon 5:16.

Key Flat

1. Zingkar var nek awm nang var det
   Oh, Nakhek khan Kerengpa
   Nang lur na le nangthat nakha
   Lah hoi thaw rang hin kadit.

          Zingkar var nek awm nang var det

          Nisun var nek ah awm nang var det

           Nang awm na ningai na mun khan

           Azingkha awm lai naw nih.

2. Avar pon le deer reeseei ah
   Retheeng lukhum rukhu rang
   En ro, ravanram ahoi kha
   Kumsawt Pumi kha nangnih.

3. Oh, nang numpok aleen det kha
   Nang numpok na ajengkha
   Oh, numpok awm na Rengmun kha
    Keeirang Rengmun nang kon joi.

4. Rentuk na rui hi chat so le
    Ringkho hi kawmawtawr le
    Keeina lishing sawm kha khe ah
    Lah le namapak chak rawng.
