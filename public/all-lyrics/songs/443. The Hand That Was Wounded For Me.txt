443. The Hand That Was Wounded For Me
3rd. Alex.92.
Jn. 20:27.
Key D.

1. Rahapui na Cross ah ihem khut kha
Numpok jarra manmeeseh na tum veek
Ratha achai haikha tunhin rawkhawm
Ravan ram inn lutna kha nemejet.
Anakhekpa khut kha kumuk
Keeirang khutkha rupawp pejoi
Numpok le chungtceng khek sa le nawchawi joi
Kecirang khut kha rupawp pejoi.

2. Tun awm mik rithi ibuk kha kumuk
Khut khana kum khatuk nawhawng roi ah
Damna le ratha' chaikha nasaanpeh
Ravanram inn lut na kha nemejet.

3. Khut khana kum khatuk nawhawng roi chak
Dangka rangkachak nek ah nuru saan
Munu jak na put' nakhekna law kha
Jisu riming ah ringkho nuru pek.

4. Apawp khut maja khana nisit pha
Kumulung saw nemenem perelen.
Lemna a-awm haikha chilai nawning.
Khutna numukui iti ah mo thang'ning?

5. Numpok mawtawr na, nikhat ading rang
Jisu le rangkachak inn na awm rang
Ama meh 'thakha nikhat mukngri rang
Keeirang ah pawp khut kha mukui chak rang.
