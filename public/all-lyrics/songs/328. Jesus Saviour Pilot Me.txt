328. Jesus Saviour Pilot Me Labs.s. 556.
Jn. 10:3.
Key B/Flat.

1. Nara kan puiro Jisu
Chit awm tuipui hawng let joi
Thitna rithi pui karhin
Rethe narang awm lai mak
Lam nareei pe-ngam nang nih
Nasaanro Pumi Jisu.

2. Nu na nai i-awi ang ah
Tuipui let awm nang mabang
Rithi khopui rang khana
Pumi nangchawng kha ra-ngai
nang ranak araat kha le
Pumi narakan pui ro.

3. Tui kawmchang kawhawng nai le
Chit awm tui miri khana
kawsaw muchum kha nachawk
Pumi kana beei phakhan
Rujet ning maw neereei ro
Chinoro, nasaan rang nih.
