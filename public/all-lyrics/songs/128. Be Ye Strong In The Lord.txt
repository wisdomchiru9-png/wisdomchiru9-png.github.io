  128. Be Ye Strong In The Lord             S.S.686.

                   Eph. 6:10.

 Key G/Flat.

 1. Pumi ranaak sawng tana thawna ngam neeiro
      Ma chawng adik khan net tak ah dingro
     Raa mun khawk na khan awm lam nakai perang nih
     Pumi riming ah mawtawr rang nanih.

            Dingrep ro, adik khan

            Rengpa chawngpek kha le mawtawr rang

            Pumi chit rajak na le, 'Ma jarra mawtawr ngir rang

            'Ma ranaak le net takah dingrang.

2. Pumi ranak sawngtana thaw na ngam neeiro
    Naraha pui ah put khan kir thamak
    Adik raa thaw nakhan nading pui ngir rang nih
    .
    * Ma ranak khale maton sawn rang ro.

3. Pumi ranaak sawng tana, thaw na-ngam neeiro
   'Machawng rakham nakha soilai nawnih
   Adik raa thaw nakhan nangchang teeng numukui
   Tak ah sawnro, maimawk awmlai naw nih.
