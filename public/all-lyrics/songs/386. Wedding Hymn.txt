386. Wedding Hymn S.S. 477.
Key G/Flat.

1. Eden iti rohon khan, nupangkhat pakhat rukon
Adam le Eve ru-awm uh, Patheen na ruchun som joi.

2. Reetheeng ah moi ruloi hi, Patheen chawnpek kha inih.
Numpok ah leng nasom rang, ' Mana raavan pek rang nih.

3. Moiloi nupang le pakha, taksa khat nirang roso
Mulung le cham khat te ah, Inn sungkhur kon uroso.

4. Sawna le hoina mun nah, Ringkho mutum malim seng
Patheen kha mining rep ah, numpok resen noroso.

5. Khrista Inn pumi chang ah, Innsung hawng kon uroso
Chonthaw sinthaw kholok ah, khristana khop chak roso.
