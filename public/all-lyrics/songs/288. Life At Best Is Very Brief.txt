288. Life At Best Is Very Brief             S.S. 437.

                  Ps. 154:4.

Key E/Flat.

1. Ahoi ringkho bawng te ni,
   Thingbo cho le ra-ang nih
   Sangvui kho le ra-ang nih Riring ro
    Nikho awm hawng relen joi,
    Thit narang pha hawng sok joi
    Ringkho kha hek rang nijoi, Riring ro.

            Riring ro.. Riring ro

            Jisuna nangkha nokoilai riring ro

            Munu ah awm nang sawt le,

            Nang mawkawt rawlawng naw nih

            Nang ra-ai se-awm bee lai naw tinih.

2. Reeipar tha awm choi rang ni
    Rongthar hai awm choi rang nih
    Apha sawt awm naw tini...Riring ro
    Rathana nokoi lai hin
    Munu neei chai lai noro
    Thit na lamkha raw tini..Riring ro.

3. Pha relen tak ah sejoi,
   Athi chawngsat pha nai joi
   Jisu deeng hawng lut rang ro.. sumtini
   Nangchonthaw tha lim tero,
   Sawna rikip rakaan rang
   Nang ratha mathang tini...Riring ro.

4. Riningna he ra-ngai ro,
Pumi kha madaan rang ro
Ravan miring ningai rang...kha phakhan
Azing aput avar kha Jisu na midik rangnih
Tuntak 'Marang put rang ro....sum tinih.
