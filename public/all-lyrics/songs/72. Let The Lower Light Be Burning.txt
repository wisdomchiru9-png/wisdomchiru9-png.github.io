72. Let The Lower Lights Be Burning                   S.S.805

                    Matt. 5:1.

Key B/Flat.

1. Pumi numpok avar khana
    Chatboi ah nojong eh joi
    Ama avar khana keeini
    Kawmchang sekhan navar joi.

            Avarkha meei ah chong roso

            Kawmchangteeng var jong tir ro

            Renro reeveei hai saw ujoi

            Vak khek tana saan rang ro.

2. Munu azing na jongbuk joi
   tuipui let kha murung joi
   A-en hai khana ru-ngak uh
   Kawmchang ah jongvar rang khan.

3. Nangvar kha me-eh ro sok pui
   Ratha thang rang mi rawpjoi
   Lungsaw muchum narang konro
   Azingkhan thang naboi rang.
