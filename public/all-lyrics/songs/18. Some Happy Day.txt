18) Some Happy Day      S.S. 934.

                   Rev. 15:3.
Key D/Flat.

1. Lah hoi chungchon keeina kejet
     Lah thaw ret rang ka-ngak rala
     He hoi khana hawng rophong joi
     Patheen lah kha thaw rang kanih.

             Ningai na nikho kha nijoi

             Kawlawm haile lah kha thawrawng

             Lah hoi tak kha thaw rang kanih

             'Ma kekung ah saw muchum rang.

  2. Pha khat ah kachawi rang kani
      Reelei thang rang Ravan awm rang
      Lamkhawk kha kawhawng rakan pha
      Nakhekpa meh muk rang kanih.

  3. Nikhat keeina reeirang kanih
      Lungman rawp kawtpui rawlawng pha
      Relen tak ah lut rang kani
      Kumsawt saw muchum rang kanih.

  4. Kese narang pha hawng sok le
      Inn jawngna lam jet lai naw ning
      'Ni-ah Patheen khut mukui ah
      'Mana inn teeng nawchawi rang nih.
