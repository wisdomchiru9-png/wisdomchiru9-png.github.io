97 The Church Rallying On S.S. 810.
Lev. 25.9.
Key G.

1. Thoiro, thoiro, Pumina tunhin nokoijoi
Thoitana 'Ma chawngkha tak ah sawn ro
Maton sawn na Jubilee kumkha rophong ro
Khrista numpok na chawngkha nang awm jawt rang ro
Rasang ah lah thawro
Thaw rang ro zingkar Sorpa khana jong var joi.

Maton sawn rang rawi
Nasan athaw ngam na lam nakai pe
Kumsawt ningai Rengmun kha ra-ngai ro
Chiboi pet ah Hoss'na ti-ah ra-aai ro
Ra-ngam napui ra-mihai
Jisu tekha jet rang rawi
Hallelluiya titai ah maton sawn rawi.

2. Patheen jetboi ah avar dit hai ra-aai uh
Tuikhang leen vara teeng jong ra-aai joi
Relen na chawngtha kha mun rikip ah sem ro
Nang inn na vawncham tuira 'Mana nejet peh.

3. Oh, Patheen miring hai numpok khut kha pero
Azing ah lawn athang hai son rang khan
Ningai heei ah nang khut khale vak roso ro
Nakhekpa roko khan 'manikha vakchawi ro.

4. Enroh, chawng rakham na nikho kha jong nai joi
Nakhek Reng vanoi na lawm rang nanih
Rachak ningai matha khale najo pha khan
Halleluiya reeleei lurna sut rang nijoi.
