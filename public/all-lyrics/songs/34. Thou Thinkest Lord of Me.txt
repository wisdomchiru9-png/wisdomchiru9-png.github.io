34. Thou Thinkest Lord of Me
S.S. 566.
Ezek. 16:60
Key G/Flat.

Kirawtawng pui lemna karkhan
Riling na kepha nusun awm
Mulung mining khat awm jawn jai
Keeihi nimining ro.

Pumina keeirang nang nimining pe jawn jai
Nang nedep le chit neei naw ning
Pumi nang na redet.

Ringkho ah sawna hawng rawp joi
Karatha sawna rok nijoi
Mulung net boi ah ka awm lai
Keeihi nimining ro.

Azingkha hawngvar rang roso
Naraaha pui karkhan awm
Kijet hin keeitu kahun joi
Keeihi nimi-ning ro.
