  229. Faint, Yet Pursuing            S.S. 681.

                   Judges 8:4.

 Key E/Flat

 1. Kawlawn narang lam kininik ret
     Mawtawr na khit nikho rang sekhan
     Nangsena lamkha nujui ngam rawng
     Avaardet tui kawmchang lam teengkhan.

            Ningkip areeikha rakai nawning

            Thisen na irukhin sa lamkhan

            Nawmawsawk uro Nakhekpa na

            Dawngkhit ringkho se kha 'muk malim

2. Ringkho ah kirawtawng pui haikha
    Kininiik reret keeinirang khan
    Nithit pe sole jongrang nijoi
    'Ma ponlap lah le jongraak rang nih.

3. 'Thit deeng kapur se awm reei ret rang
    Cross ah iruthaat na-uh munkhan
    Ravan reemeei kha zingkhap se awm
    Saw renro reeveei awm lai nawnih.

4. Sawna rekhe se awm reei retrang
   Zingkar Sawrpa kha kumuk malim
   Avaar khana sawna kesun nah
   Jong eh rangni laam hoi mun teengkhan.
