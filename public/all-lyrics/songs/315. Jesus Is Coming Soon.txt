315. Jesus Is Coming Soon            B.L. 359.
Key E/Flat

1. Phakhat ah mulung ah renro reetheei khana
   Mulung sung ah ahoi theeimak meh
   Patheen deeng mulung pe tai ah ning minit rawi
   Mireetheeng hailam khan nangni juiro.

          Zingkar nisun nitak

          Jisu jong narang kha

          Apha jet ngam mak me riring jer roh

          Mireetheeng lim Ravan inn hoi khan ruchun rang

          Athi awm naboi ram teeng khan serang.

2. Dikboi hawngpung rang nih
   Chawngdik hawngtawm rang nih
  Chawngpek hawng koi rang ni ti ah rureei
   Riring jerra awmro Jisu relen tak ah
  Jetboi theeiboi phakhan jongrang nijoi.

3. Nawsawna kholok hi ningai ah jir rang nih
   Tuipui vara khat teeng nurumu leh
   Mathan ngir rang nanih, naw-awm na reeleei hi
   Ahawngrang nanijoi Ravan ram khan.
