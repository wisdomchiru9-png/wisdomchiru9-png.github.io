39. Blessed Jesus (H.P. 139)
Key G/Flat

1. Mulung sawhai kha ning ma-ngai masak
Jisu nasaan Jisu
Natna aneei hai kha awm namadam
Nasaan Jisu nasaan
Keeini nawsaw nara phakhan
Mining ah beeirang awm lak mak
Tute nasaan rang awm lak mak
Nasaanpa Jisu ang ah.

2. Nakhek rang tute awmboi pha khan awm
Jisu nakhek Jisu
Naraha pui kar khan a-na khek joi
Jisu nakhek Jisu
Kesena lamkhan nawlawn pui
Kawsaw kara na nejet peh
'Ma nek ah atheei tumo awm?
Awm mak Jisu ah ang tu.

3. Kupumi kha keeina kumuk pha khan
Jisu nasaan Jisu
Ama riming ban jawn jai rang kani
Jisu nasaan Jisu
Avar jawn jai tui kawmchang khan
Lawm dit haile rumuk pha khan
Keei ningheen veng rang kani joi
Jisu na nakhek jarra.
