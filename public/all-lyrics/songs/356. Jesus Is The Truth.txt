 356. Jesus Is The Truth             B.L. 58.

                   Jn. 14:6.

 Key C/Flat

 1. Pumi nang mawkawt khan keei kawhawng lut ret joi
    Atharra kumunu nurusuk peret roh
    Nangla adik mawkawt ringna lamkha nang nih
    Pumina nang numuk munu neeikha kanih.

           Anasaantheei nang kana beei

           Num nopo ret ro, munu kha keeneei lai

           Nangte kanamapak, Anasaantheei ti-ah

           'Tun awm Pumi keeihi, munu kar hin nakhek ret ro.

2. Pumi Jisu riming aban na ani rang
   Mohot nawning kiring kho dawng hawngsok sekhan
   Pumi ireei adik kha le keei nasaan chak
   Munu neeilai nawning Pumideeng kinik le.

3. Pumi Jisu keeihi numnopo thep ret ro
   Sawna meeisa doi ah chap na hin nasaan ro.
   Ringkho sung ah ningai phortha nape nang nih
   Atharra ningai na phortha kha neperoh.
