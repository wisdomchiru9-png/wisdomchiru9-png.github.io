115. The Master's Call           S.S.756.

                 Matt. 21:28.

Key A/Flat

1. Ra-ngairo Pumina tun nokoi
    Ara rawkhawm rangkhan
    Loimun ah sang min khana na-ngak
    Rawkhawm rang khan mitawm.

           Nupumi riming ah setana

           Mulung renem tak ah

           Manboi ah inakhek na chawng hi

           Vanoi deeng rophong ro.

2. Sero athang haikha Vaksanro
   Azing munu karkhan
   Setana 'manikha Vaksanro
    Rentuk ruisat pero.

3. Sara haikha ning ma-ngaipero
   Numpok bumichin khan
   Ama kumsawt ringna bulawm nih
   Lam nurujawng rang nih.

4. Relen rangro, ni ta rang nijoi
   Avar awm lai nawnih
   Ase phakhana nojong bee le
   Chonkha thaw ngam boi rang.
