445. Lo, The Day Of God Is Breaking s.s.688
Eph. 6:2.
Key 'B/Flat.

1. Patheen avaar khi en uro,
Ala tak ah vaar a-awm
Manmeeseh hai thoi urang ro
Zingkar Arshi vaar lawm rawi.
Jisu jarkhan jawt umak
Ama kha ruthaat ujoi
Thaan naput ah hawng ring joi
Rahapui mawtawr joi
Jisu riming le ranak kha
Ajawt boi hai deeng
'Ma deeng keeini chubai bawk rawi
Reng lukhum mukhu rawi.

2. Nanglam kaipa tak ah sawn ro
Mulung chit awm boi pet ah
Jisuna lam kai rang nijoi
Ama riming ah muk rang.

3. Maton sawn na lawn urang ro
Seeitaan kha chilai noro
Pumina nangle awm chak joi
Reng lukhum rukhu malim.

4. Khong le lawi le mawtawr rang nih
Mole lawn kha chun rang nih
Lah thaw malalim bang noro
Khrista reeleei Reng chang joi.
