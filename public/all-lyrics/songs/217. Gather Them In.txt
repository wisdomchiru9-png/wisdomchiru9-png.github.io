217. Gather Them In S.S. 779.
Lk. 14:23.
Key A/Flat.

1. Ruchun rangro, muntawng awm laihin
Rengpa bumichin na reeijoi
'Manikha inn sip malim rawkhawm ro
Sara vawncham misip rang nih.
Lam khan vaksok ro, vak sok urangro
Munu lamkhan sok rang ro
Numpok mulung le maton sawnro
Achai haikha rawkhawm ro.

2. Ruchun rangro, muntawng awm lai hin
Miring tampuina ikoi kha
I sawn umak ah numulung kham joi
Ikoi jet ret lai naw minih.

3. Ruchun rangro, muntawng awm lai hin
Chungteen ah Patheen chawngpek nih
Rawkhawm rangro 'Ma numpok rotho khan
Ama numpok khut maja khan.
