370. Consecration          S.S. 616.
Key G/Flat.

1. Kiringkho noloi peroh
   Pumi nangdeeng kenepek
   Keeina kiring lai lim hin
   Nangte namapak chak rawng (2)

2. Kukhut hi noloi peroh
   Nang numpok chon thaw narang
   Ke ke hi noloi peroh
   Nangrang atha niroso.. (2)

3. Kehe hi noloi peroh
    Nang numpok lah thaw narang
    Kener hi noloi peroh
    Nangchawng hoi hi reei narang.. (2)

4. Kadangka noloi peroh
    Lekte se awm hawi boi ah
    Kitheeina noloi pero
    Nang idit nawmaw thawroh.. (2)

5. Kimining noloi peroh
    Nangta namachang peroh
    Kumulung hi nangta keeng
    Nang awm na mun chang rosoh.. (2)

 6. Kunumpok noloi peroh
    nang kekung ah kenepek
     Katak hi noloi peroh
     Nangta namachang peroh... (2)
