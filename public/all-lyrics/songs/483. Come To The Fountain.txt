483. Come To The Fountain

                  Ps. 36:9.

Key F

1. Nang munu le nangpok rik kha
   Aput tuikhan hawngchawi ro
    Athuk tuikhan hawng nawm rang ro
   Ningnit na nang muk rang nih.

           Relen rang ro....rasai noro

           Nang ratha ahawngrang musum noro

           Nasan rang Jisuna na-ngak

           Numpok ah nawmaw kawi lai.

2. Nang munu khale hawng rang ro
   Jisu khrista na na-ngak
   Nang munu khadawr akhe kha
   Vur ang ah nangchek rang nih.

3. Nakhek pana keeini deeng reei
   Asirra tak ah sawn hai
   Amakha tak ah sawn hai lim
    Kumsawt ringna tum rang nih.

4.   Ma deeng hawng tana dam rangro
    Inokoi he ro-ngai ro
    Seet aput nang hawng kir le
    Ravantir ningai rang nih.
