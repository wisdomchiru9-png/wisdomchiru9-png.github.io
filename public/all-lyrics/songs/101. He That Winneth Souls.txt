101. He That Winneth Souls              R.S. 317.
Key A/Flat.

1. Pumi Rengpa chonthaw kha raavantha nih.
   Ravan na maanrawp lung numuk rang nijoi
   Ratha athang hawngkhek miring haikhana
   Pumi raavan muk rang nih.

           Ratha mawtawr miring kha

           Ravan chung ah Innhoi khan

            Reng lukhum alur maan rawp kha man rang ni

            Ratha mawtawr miring kha.

2. Inn hoi tak khan numpok ringkho var kha le
    Ningthingkip Jisu le ra-ang ah ring rang
    Athang rang ah ratha lam hawngkai hai kha
    Pumi raavan muk rang nih.

3. Anile roroi ah Pumi lam raw rawi
    Muntin na ratha thang haikha vak tawk ro
    Ke-ngam roipa deeng ningai ah ruchun rang
    Pumi raavan muk rang nih.

4. Ringna chawngtha kha muntin na vak sem ro
    Numpok na chontha mulung ah sawng rep ro
    Seetneei hai Pumi deeng ah ahawng hai kha
    Pumi raavan muk rang nih.
