98. What Shall I Do           TNBCH. 82.
Key G.

1. Pumi keei ichon mo thaw ning?
   Karatha ring narang
   Keeina reeleei hin karing ah
   Keei kumulung saw joi.

           SP. Diik tak ah nang ring jawt maw?

           TB. Nang atharra sok ret ro

           SP. Nang munu phung kholok kha

           TB. Jisu deeng nang rophong le

           Alt. Rethe na awm naw nih

           SP. Nupumi Jisu maikung ah

           Alt. Munuphung hawng rang ro

           SP. Mitha namachang rang nijoi

           All. Pumi kha ring rep ro.

2. Munu lamhi khatuk khan rawp
    Khose teeng mo se ning?
    Pumi ratha ringna lamkha
    Keeideeng nemezet ro.

3. Keei ang munuphung miring hi
    Tumo awm lai nita?
   Calvary ah thisen khana
    Munu na taan pero.

4. Ravan ram lut narang lamkha
    Lam thawr pui khat te nih
    Makha lamkhan nang lut narang
    Mulung mathar rang ro.

5. Niringkho hi pha bawng te nih
     Tute na awm reei ngam mak
     Niringlai numropo som hi
     Patheen chawng pek khanih.
