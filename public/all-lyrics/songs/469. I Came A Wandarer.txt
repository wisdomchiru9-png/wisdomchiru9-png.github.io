469. I Came A Wandarer                 S.S. 850.

                  Ps. 34:6.

Key A/Flat.

1. Achai ah ru-awm kahawng joi
    Zan ang ah zing lam hin
    Raavantha Pumi kene-en
    Avaarkha kumuk joi.

            Nakhekpa Jisu Ke-ngamte

            Kiton rawp hai dak naw!

            Nang numpok mirit najarkhan

            Numpok kha nang nepek.

2. Chitna rakhana le kahawng
    Ra-ngam na neei mi-ing
     Nangthisen kha kawhawng en pha
     Ra-ngaidam he kejet.

 3. Ratha ranaak kha sip roso
     Sara mulung keeideeng
     Nangchawng keemeeivaar chang roso
     'Ranaak ninit pero.

 4. Nang he so kha nuruchu ro
     Seetna raam 'zing noi 'hin
     Kara-ngam nale kiringna
     Kumsawt kala nang nih.
