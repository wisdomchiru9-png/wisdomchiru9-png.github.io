275. Repentance              G.T.25.
Key E/Flalt.

1. Sawna manmeeseh sung hin
    Alawn kha keei kani
    Nit' awm kharam ahoi munkhan
    Tak ah sawn hai lut rang nih.

            Makha inn ahoi sung khan

            Tak ah sawn hai lut pha khan

            Pumi keei awm nete cheen ro

            Tak ah sawn hai le rawtawp ah.

2. Mateeng ah ruhawng miring
    Role chim kholok le
   Vara teeng khan rumuk pui rang
   Tak ah sawn hai lut phakhan

3. Pha khat ah nasanpa kha
    Rumuk pui rang kani
   'Mana keei nuru ngak rang nih
   Tak ah sawn hai lut pha khan.
