 309. When The Bridegroom Comes                S.S. 185.

                 Matt. 25:6.

 Key E

1. Na thaimeei ruvar ni maw
    Moipa jong pha khan?
   Avar kha reetheeng ngam ni maw
   Moipa jong pha khan?
   Makha moiloi ni jan khan
   Nathai meei ruvar nimaw?
   Moipa jong pha khan?

           Ruriring ro....ru riring ro,

           Riring chak ro moipa jong pha khan.

2. Rumuk na he jet mini maw
   Moipa jong pha khan?
   Ningai na he jet mini maw
   Moipa jongpha khan?
   Zanchimthim moiloi jan khan
   Nathaimeei ru var nimaw?
   Moipa jong pha khan?

3. Pha rurekhe masak noro, Moipa jong maseng
   Neresen masak rang nijoi, Pumi jong pha khan
   Makha moiloi ni jan khan
   Nathai meei ru var nimaw, Moipa jong nikhan?

4. Mulung sawna pha awm nitheei, Moipa jong phakhan
   Thaitui phuk rang nani ah tu, Moipa jong phakhan
   Makha Moiloi ni jan khan, Nathaimeei ruvar ni maw?
   Moipa jong phakhan.

5. Ningaidet rumuk na nirang,
   Moipa jong pha khan
   Halleluiya lam rujawng rang
   Moipa jong pha khan
   Makha jan ningai na jan
   Nathai meei avar khale
   Moipa rujawng rang.
