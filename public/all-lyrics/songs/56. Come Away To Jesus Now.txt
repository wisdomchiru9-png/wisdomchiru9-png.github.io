56. Come Away To Jesus Now                R.S. 191.
Key E/Flat.

1. Nang kethawt jawt boi ah nang ding cheen
   Saw muchum na hoi sok deeng tak ah
   'Mana nokoi, nuru jawng rang nih
    Tun Jisu deengkhan hawngro.

           Hawng uro...Jisu deeng (3)

            Tun Jisu deengkhan hawng ro.

2. Ratha ranak jarra nangding ngam
    Ahoi ningaina ramkha en-na
    Nge karra tersaan thaw thitna nih
    Tun Jisudeeng khan hawngro.

3. Nanglawmdit vara teeng se so joi
    Mik ah mukboi khut na najaap joi
    Mani numpok hekhana nokoi
    Tun Jisu deengkhan hawngro.
