492. Nor Silver Nor Gold               3rd Alex. 403.

                1.Peter. 1:18.
Key 'D.

1. Dangka Rangkachak nimak keei ana khek hi
   Karatha reeleei neeinak na khek ngam mak
   Cross ah sok thisen te khana nomothoi joi
   Athi Nakhekpa na tun keei nakhek joi.

            Keei nakhek joi Dangka le nimak

            Rangkachak le awm nimak

            Nawrawchawk joi Jisu thisen na

            Man rawp numpok reei ngam boi.

2. Dangka Rangkachak nimak keei ana khek hi
    Kimining rehe kha dawr arik haikha
    Cross ah sok thisen te khana nomothoi joi
    Nakhekpa athi khana napai pejoi.

3. Dangka Rangkachak nimak keei ana khek hi
    Reetheeng chawngpek khana nara nai masak
    Cross ah sok thisen te khana nomothoi joi
    Athi nakhekpa na chitna napai peh.

4. Dangka Rangkachak nimak keei ana khek hi
    Ravan ram raw narang nawraw chaw ngam mak
    Cross ah sok thisen te khana keei nomothoi
    Athi nakhek pana reetheeng ah nakhek.
