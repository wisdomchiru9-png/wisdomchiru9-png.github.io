223. Loyalty To Chirst            S.S. 1102.

                   Jn. 2:5.

Key F.

1. Alatak raamhoi mun khan ponlap melek ah
   Mirikip nokoi Khrista kha beeirep rang rawi
   Raa mawlawr na lah kha mo le lawn na thawro
   Mulung net tak ah Khrista kha beeirep urang rawi

           Raa nawtawr narang... mawtawr na-ngir rang

           Lamkaipa kha ra-aai joi

           'Ma anukha jui rawi

           Ramhoi nutum rang nih

           Mulung net tak ah Khrista kha nabeei rep atu.

2. Reeleei kaw rikip ah, a-per he ra-ngai ro
    Mulung net tak ah Khrista kha beeirep rang rawi
    Thoiro rasaam rangro, Seeitan mawtawr narang
    Jisu Khrista kha mulung net tak ah beeirep rawi.

3. Rongthar hai ranaak kha, von Jisu kekung ah
    Keeinina phun urang rawi mulung net tak ah
    Keeinina chontha hi mun rikip ah sem rawi
    Khrista kha tak ah sawm rep tai ah jui urang rawi.
