2. Achai ravak ang ah Nii hawng ta joi
   Azing na nuhup awm
   Lungpum nang nih
   Karamang se khan awm

3. Ravan ram se rangkhan lam kha murawng,
   Numpok mirit kha nang nojong tir peh
   Vantir hai na najap.

4. Mulung mining vaar le, namapak rawng
   Sawna kar khan awm Bethel mothoi rawng
   Kumulung saw pha awm,

5. Ningaina matha le kaleng ah awm
   Nii tha Arshi chung ah kavung ah awm
   Lah kha thaw rang kanih.
