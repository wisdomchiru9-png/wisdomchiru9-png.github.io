350. Pray Pray           3rd Alex. 139.

                   Col. 4:7.

Key G/Flat.

1. Pumi deeng te khan nichak roh
    Sawna pha rikip ah
    Nang iniik nejet perang ni
    Amana nara nai pui chak.

           Nangrang amatat kholok

           Chubai thaw ah 'Madeeng niro,

           Pumi na nejet peveek

           Ama deeng kungkhan peveek rang ro.

2. Chit tak ah thim beeng ah niro
    Ama cham niroso
    Atha namchang perang nih
    Ama raasam na niroso.

3. Chubai thaw tana niik pero
    Lungboi roi hai rang khan
   Athaw ngam Pumi mai kung khan
   Nang ire hai kha vak chawi ro.

4. Chubai thawro tak ah sawn na
    Ningai na lah thaw ro
    Reetheeng ah nangthit narang khan
    Nang mulung 'Madeeng peveek ro.
