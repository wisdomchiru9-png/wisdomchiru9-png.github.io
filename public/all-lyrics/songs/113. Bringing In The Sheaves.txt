113. Bringing In The Sheave            S.S. 757.

                 Ps. 126:6.

Key C.

1. Zingkar le nisun na nitak kho rinim ah
   Numpok muchi ni-ivawr rikip haikha
   Rawkhawm rangkhan ngak ro, ara atha haikha
   Ningai ah hawngchawi ro atet munkhan.

           Hawngchawi urang rawi, atetna munkhan     2

           Ningai ah hawngchawi ro atetna munkhan

2. Nisaat kar awm kawvawr, khorinim awm kawvawr
   Reemeei le phabi vur kha chiboi pet ahsirodo
   Dawdawn na rawkhawm kha, nawhawng mawtawr sojoi
   Ningai ah hawngchawi rawi atetna munkhan.

3. Achap nek ah sero, Pumi rang vak vawr ro
   Nem naboi ah naratha mawsaw nek ah
   Asaw nemetheng le, lam nuru jawng rang nih
    Ningai ah hawngchawi rawi atetna munkhan.
