446. At Calvary M.B.C.               54.K.L.20.

                   Col.2:14.

Key C.

1. Pha sawt tak keeina kuruchai ah
   Jisu athi iru sawn mi-ing
   Keeirang ruthi kha rujet mi-inng
   Calvary ah.

           Numpok misaan na ravaat boi mun

            Ra-ngaidam inepek na munkha

            Karatha sawna kha napai pe,

            Calvary ah.

2. 'Ma chawngkhan munu kamathan joi
    Idawr ra singdawng awm mo nijoi
    Kumulung kelek malim kahawng
    Calvary ah.

3. Tuntu Jisu deeng kepek veek joi
    Tun Ama kerengpa kha nijoi
    Tuntu karatha kha ningai joi
    Calvary ah.

 4. Oh, numpokna ringkhomo nepek
    Oh, misaan nakha numumuk joi
    Oh, Jisuna nojong thit pe joi
    Calvary ah.
