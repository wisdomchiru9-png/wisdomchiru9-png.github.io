458. I Gave My Life For Thee                S.S. 621.

                 Jn. 10:15.

Key E/Flat.

1. Kiringkho le thisen, nangrang khan kenepek
   Nang ring sawt narang khan, kono jongthit pejoi
   Kenepek kiringkho, nang imo nang nepek.

2. Saw renro reeveei ah, kumsawt tak kurudoi
   Hek tik neeiboi ningai hoinakha muk narang
   Kumsawt nangrang kodoi, imo nangdoi keeirang?

3. Kapa inn avaar le, kereng sut na munkhan
    Keeina kamathaan na, sawna kojong reeveei
   Keeirang hin nangna la, imo mathan ngam rawng?

4. Jeng sawna kidoi kha meeleei na reei ngam mak
    Nangkha khek narang khan, sawna engseet kodoi
    Ajeng sawna kodoi, imo doirawng keeirang?

5. Kapa inn aput ah, nangdeeng konojong chawi
    Ringkhomo rakan na, ra-ngaidam le numpok
    Khutchawi konojang chawi, nang imo nanghawng chawi?

 6. Nang ringkho kha pero, 'Ma rangkhan niroso
    Manmeeseh rahana sawna kholok haikha
    'Madeeng hawngchawi tana, Nakhekpa kha juiro.
