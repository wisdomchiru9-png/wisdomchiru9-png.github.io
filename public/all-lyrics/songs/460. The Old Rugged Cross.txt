460. The Old Rugged Cross    Ch.S.H. 155

                  S.L. 112.

Key B/Flat.

1. Raam latak lawn chung ah
   Alui Cross khat ru-awm
   Zak nale Sawna reeveei na
   Makha Cross aluikha
   Keeirang maan lu tak nih
   Seet neei hailim makhan ruthat uh.
    Makha Cross maanlu det khan dep rawng
    Kithit malim khan lut rang kani
    Cross alui kha mukui rang kani
    Nikhat Reng lukhum maan rang kanih.

2. Makha Cross alui kha
    Vanoi na rudit mak
    Keeirangtu kidit det kha nih
    Patheen ke-ngam te na
   'Ma lurna kha mak ah
   Calvary thing Cross chungkhan ruthi.

3. Oh, Cross alui chung khan
   Thisen mirit jonglong
   Rekhek tak ah thi kha kumuk joi
   Makha thing Cross chung khan
   Jisu sawdoi ah thi
   Kumunu reetheeng masak narang.

4. Makha Cross alui kha
   Dik tak ah jui rang rawng
   Sawna le zak na mun rikip ah
   Raamlatak innpui khan     b
   Nikhat nokoi rang nih
   Kumsawt ringna kha muk rang Kanih.
