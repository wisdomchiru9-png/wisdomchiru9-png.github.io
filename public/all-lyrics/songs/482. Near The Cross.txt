   482. Near The Cross                S.S. 134.

                     Eph. 3:23.

   Key F.

   1. Cross kungkhan keei natet ro
      Ringna tui longna mun
      Manboi pet damna tuikha
      Calvary ah jonglong.

             Cross kung khan (2)

             Ningai bang noroso

             Asaw karatha hina

            Saw muchum malalim.

 2. Ratha sawhai Cross kungkhan
    Numpok na numuk joi
    Zingkar Arshi vaar khana
    Keeihi nojong vaar ro.

3. Cross kungkhan ke-ngam Sapa
   'Madeeng khan nawchawi ro
   Keeihi nasaan chak tana
   Nang rilip khan awm rawng.

4. Cross kungkhan kana-ngak chak
   Tak'sawn ra-ngam na le
  Nang ravan raam munhoi khan
  Kawhawng sok malalim.
