206. More About Jesus            S.S.571.

                 Hosea 6:3.

Key A/Flat.

1. Jisu nunchan kha jet det rawng
   'Ma numpok nakha mumuk rawng
   Inasaan nakha kumuk joi
   Keeirang numpok ah nithit peh.

           Jisu jarkha leek ah

           Keeina kejet jawt det

           Inasaan nakha kumuk joi

           Keeirang numpok ah nithit peh.

2. Jisu nunchan kha ruchu rang
   'Ma reetheeng nakha jetdet rawng
   patheen ratha na keeirang ah
   Ithawchon nemezet roso.

3. 'Ma chawng ahput Jisu jarkha
    Pumi le rejet jawn jai rang
   Ama chawng rikip ra-ngai rang
   Tak ah sawn narang khan reeirang.

4. Rengsut na munkhan Jisuna
    Ningai lurna 'Ma ta rok nih
    'Ma Rengmun kha leen det rang nih
    Rachak Rengpa jongchum phakhan.
