224. The Church's One Foundation                  S.S.228

                  Eph. 5.23.

Key E/Flat.

1. Khrista Beekrup changna,
    Beekrup khat kha ruphun
    'Mana tuile chawngna,
    Irukon Beekrup khanih.
    Ravan aput 'Mana,
    Reetheeng Beekrup kha jongtawk
    Beekrup kha rurawchawk joi.

2. Reeleei ah phung rikip
Madaan na khat te minit
Tak ahsawn, 'sok na le
Pumi khat te, Beekrup nih
'Reetheeng saak kha saak som
Reetheeng mining khat te-ah
Ra-ngam napui khat le
'Mana Beekrup kha achep.

3. Mihaai na Beekrup kha
Eng' thawpha mulung sitpe
Beekrup khoijawt haina
Ranaak ah chawng niboi sem uh
'Ni-ah reetheeng raatha na
Beekrup sawnakha en chak
Mulung sawna chap nakha
Ningai lah le mawlawr rang.

4. Beekrup chonthaw lai tak
Rawchawk narang 'raa thaw uh
Maton rang mining ah
Raachak le mawtawr narang
Mining ningai lur nah
Beekrup tenret nakha nih
Beekrup na mawtawr le
Saw muchum nakha muk rang.

5. 'Thum Patheen 'Khat tena
Reeleei hin nuruchun joi
Ningai ah Ama le
Mawtawr hai saw muchum rang
Reetheeng ah ningai hai
Pumi numpok na numuk
Nun renem hai khana
Ama le awm som rang nih.
