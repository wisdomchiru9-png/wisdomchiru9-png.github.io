325. Fall Into Line            S.S. 705.

                 1. Tim. 6:12.
Key Flat

1. Mole lawn rasang kha khe ah
    Ringna chawngtha khana nokoi
    Ra-ngairo nokoi na hekha
    Keeini rang ah chawng tha nih.

           Rawthaw puirang, dingsom rang rawi. (2)

           hawng ruchun rangro sumlawi inokoi khan

           khristen raami ruchun ro.

2. Raapon kha hawng ree seeitana
   Ningai tak ah rawlawp rang ro
   Adik ah raa mawtawr narang
    Ravantir na nurung chak.

3. Dangka sumlawi kha tum tana
    Raa hekha rasang masak ro
    Jisu Khrista rang rok inih
    Nithit awr mawtawr ngir rang.
