163. Praise Our Creator             S.S.219.

                  Ps. 16:1.

Key C.

1. Nakon nakhek pa Pumi kha mapak rawi
   Thitna le thaan aput 'Mana nakhek
   'Ma leen na lah ah thaw tana lawm ro
   Arat nasan, nakhekpa Pumi nih.

           Mapak som rawi

           Rasang chungchon Ama lurna kha mapak rawi

           Mapak u rawi

           Pumi rimingkha raavan tha nih.

2. Nakonpa narungpa kha mapak urawi
    Rethe narang lungpum kha Ama nih
    Naroipa Amana lam nakai pechak
    Phaijaw ah tui rachak long na munkhan.

3. Nakonpa atha jongput na mapak rawi
    Chungteeng Ama lurna kha mapak uh
    'Ma chawng rakham nakha tak ah sawn rep ro
    Numpok khana numukui chak rang nih.
S.S. 209.
