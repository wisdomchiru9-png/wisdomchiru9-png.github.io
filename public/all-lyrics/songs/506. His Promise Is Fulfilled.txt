   506. His Promise Is Fulfilled.

1. Arimethi ah Joseph khana Jisu rok kha
   Pilaat rengpa deeng keeina khum rawng ti-ah vaak nik
   Pilaat na jawt pe-ah ruver sa thaan na khum
   Dawm ngam boi lungle ru mukhu ujoi.

           Sp.       Mitheeihai na

           parts:    Jisu chawngkha

           Sp.       Mining u-ah

           Parts:    Chi u jarra

           Sp.       Thaan kha rung uh

           Parts:    Jisuna 'reei',

           Sp.       Nithumchang na

           Parts:    Hawngring rang tih,

           Sp,       Patheen Sapa

           Parts:    Kha hawngring joi

           Sp.       Mapak rang rawi

           Parts: Ningai rang rawi

           All:      Hawgring het joi, Jisu Von hawngring joi

           Parts: Halleluyah.

2. Nithum changna Jisuna athi mawtawr ah
   Mateeng ah iru reei dungjui ah hawng ring joi
   Thaan rung hai khana hawngring kha rujet u mak
   Hawngring joi Halleluya hawng ring joi.

3. Jisu hawngring het joi iti chawng zet ule
   Jisu raha pui haikhana rujawt umak
   Thaan runghaikha seer le ner ruhup pe ujoi
   Jisu tu keeini rang hawngring het joi.

4. Jisu thaan a-en se Marry le lawm hai deeng
   Ravantir na ru-reei, Jisu ringjoi ti-ah
   Galilee teeng khan ruse joi kir urang ro
   Ningai chawngchawi tana vaak reei rang ro.
