108. Keep Close To Jesus                R.s. 376.
Key C/Flat.

1. Sawmuchum na rang Ravan ram nangraw pha
    Jisu tekha depchak rang ro
    Lamhoi na jet ah 'Mana lam nakai pe
    Jisu tekha dep chak rang ro.

            Jisu depchak ro(2)

            Jisu tekha depchak rang ro

            Adik ah putkhan lekse kirlai noro

            Jisu tekha depchak rangro.

2. Sawna rana khan mulung mawdawng noro
    Jisu tekha dep chak rangro
    'Madeeng hoina le ningai zet narang khan
    Jisu tekha depchak rangro.

3. Athaboi chonthaw aput rekhe narang
    Jisu tekha dep chak rang ro
    Tak ah sawn phokha mawtawr se sawngchak rang ro
    Jisu tekha dep chak rang ro.

4. Keeini Ravan Innhoi dawdawn na sok rang
    Jisu tekha depchak rangro
    Khamun hoikhan keeini resen laai naw minih
    Jisu tekha dep chak rang ro.
