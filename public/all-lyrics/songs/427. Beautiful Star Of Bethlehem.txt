427. Beautiful Star Of Bethlehem

1. Oh, en awm Bethlehem Arshi
Ala tak ah var peng ah awm
Mateeng ruse hairang avar nang pek
Mitheei haikha lam nangkai pe
Jisu sok na ram mun sekhan
Oh, en awm Bethlehem Arshi var ro.
Oh, en awm Bethlehem Arshi
Nojong var uro kosok malalim
Oh, kalam namavar pe uro
Nisut sawt kawlawn narang khan
Oh, en awm Bethlehem Arshi, var ro.

2. Oh, ra-ngam napui Arshi vaar
Se hawng mihai lam nangkai pe
Ching rakhan na kho hawngvar malalim
Avaar khana nikho hoitak ah
Lamkha narakhan pui rang nih
On, en awm Bethlehem Arshi var ro.

3. En-awm Arshi saw muchum na
Nakhek narang raavan nape
Relek ningai Reng lukhum kha kaman
Mitheei haikha lam nang kaipe
'Ma avaar kha hawngvar sisit joi
Oh, en-awm Bethlehem Arshi, var ro.
