346. Only A Sinner.

1. Keeina kimuk hi man boi pet ah ni
Tak ah kawsawn jarra phor kha nepek
Keeleen rasat narang awm lak mak
Munu phung nakhek numpok khana.
Munu phung miring ranga kha
Numpok khana keei nakhek joi
Kirisim hi 'Ma ningai na niroso
Munu phung keei numpok na nakhek.

2. Kawmawng jarra munu ruchi mi-ing
Patheen khale nuru ralat masak
Jisu na numuk ah kiningai
Munuphung nakhek numpok khana.

3. Mik rithi rulong awm nem na mi-ing
nakhek nawk atu kuruthi ngir joi
Munu khana Patheen mai kung ah
Kuruchi tuntu keei nakhek joi.
