409. No Born King Like Jesus                TNBC 528.

                (Virgin Birth)

1. Manmeeseh sung ah reng hituk hi sip ah awm
   Reng khrista a-angtu khat se-awm, awm lak mak
   Nasaanpa sokjoi Bethlehem seh inn sung ah
   Serawi Ravaan rengpa kha chubai vakbawk rawi.

           Ningai ah mapak rawi, Ravaan Reng chungchon kha

           ama sok niko hin ninga-ngai ah mapak rawi

           Ningai na lah kha manmeeseh hin sip roso

           Ravaantir hai na-awm ningai ah lawm Toso.

2. Ravaantir haina awm ningai ah lah thaw uh
    Ke-ngam roihai na awm lamlai munkhan zet uh
   Nakhekpa sok namun kha' manideeng reei joi
   Makhan Ravaan rengpa kha chubai vak bawk uh.

3. Nisok nateeng ah mitheei thum hai ava kha
    Ravaam Arshi vaar khana lam vak kai pe uh
    Unu Mary ipawm kha vaak muk ule khan
    Maanrawp khutchawi khale chubai vak bawk ujoi.

4. Reng Herod na jet le mulung rusaw pe joi
    Ereetheeng naite jarkhan mihai deeng rawdawn
    Bethlehem naite kholok kha vak thaat so joi
    Naite Jisu khatu patheen na rusaan joi.

5. Oh, kupumi tun na put hin kumulung ah
    Pumi nang le awmchak rawng nojong awm puiro
    Kukhut akhe hi Mary khut ti-ah loiro
    Kupumi kumsawt ah nangkha namapk rawng.
