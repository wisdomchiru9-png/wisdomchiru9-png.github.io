154. The handwriting On The Wall                 S.S.432

                 Dan. 5:5.

Key F

1. Belsazer le raampo hai lishing bumichin nakhan
    Rangkachak hai ah zu nek u-ah ningai ah ru-awm u.
    Janchim ah Rengpa innhoi sung ah ru-awm ulai khan
    Mulung rakak na khatu baang ah ijong meezeek kha nih.

           Baang ah kha Patheen khut kha nih (2)

           I mezecek kha kip rang nimaw?

            Tak sawn na neei mini maw?

            Khut na baang ah meezeek laiseng khan.

 2. Enro lungroko ah-awm Daniel reng maikung ah ding
     Reng leen rasat na le seetna haikha ip boipet ah reei
     Meezeek kha jongte pe-ah mipui mulung rakak joi
     Rengpa tun naput ah nangreng chang nahi dawng kha nijoi.

3. Enro tak ah sawn le rat na chon adik thaw narang
    Daniel deeng ah ratha na ijongpekchungchon ranak kha
    Judea ram ah' Ma inn lung roko sung ah awm lai khan
    Baang ah patheen khut imeezeek khana mana ruzet joi.

4. Hi-anghin nithaw kholok khut le rumeezeek khat awm
    Seetneei hai nangseet na kha hawngchawi tana hawng bu
    rang ro
   Mirikip deeng mahi jongsok narang nikho nai joi
   Seetneeihai nangseet nakha baang ah meezeek veek rang
   nijoi.
S.S. 481.
