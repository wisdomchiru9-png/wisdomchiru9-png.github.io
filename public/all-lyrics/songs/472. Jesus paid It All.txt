472. Jesus paid It All.

1. Kiton leeiphuk lim Jisuna numpok ah nuthung pe
Kirehe na kholok awm, nawchawi pe sojoi
Jisukha that narang ah, Cross teengkhan ruchawi ujoi
Keeseetna kholok 'Mana nawchawi pe sojoi.
Kumunu reetheeng sojoi,
Calvary lawn thing Cross chung a khan) 2.
Thisen mirit kha long
Reetheeng narang khan.

2. I dawrra hoimoni, kumunu nilik pe sojoi
Azing khap kumunu kha namavaar pe joi
Kumunu rawp hai jarra, 'Ma thisen le nakhek joi
Kumunu phung kholok kha Jisuna phungjoi.

3. Kumunu jarra jisu kha dep ah seei le ruvit
Engseet tak ah Rome raami haina rujem uh
Riling lukhum kha thisok malalim rumukhu uh
Jisu kha manina mai ah rumeecheei uh.

4. Manmeeseh miringhai athaboi kholok kha zet na
Jisu deeng nek thaboi athur tui rupek uh
Jisuna kha-ang 'thaboi chon le raaikho ranaak kha
He rasang tak ah ra-aai ah ru mawtawr joi.
S.S. 337
