201. I Beleive!       3rd Alex. 59.

                Rome. 10:10

Key C

1. Munu ah put nasaan ningal kha kumuk joi
   Kumulung ah Nakhekpa kiring joi
    Pok rik kamathan ah put rachak keeneei joi
   Patheen Sapa kha kiring joi.

           Kiring joi (2)

           Kumulung ah Nakhekpa Kiring Joi

           Kiring joi (2)

           Patheen Sapa kha kiring joi.

2. Karavaat na nepek ah numpok le nakhek
   Kumulung ah Nakhekpa kiring joi
   Kimik namavar pe ah avar numumuk
    Patheen Sapa kha kiring joi.

3. "Ma misaan nale maiso var kha kumuk joi
   Kumulung ah Nakhekpa kiringjoi
   Numpok le Nakhekpa na najap kiningai
   Patheen Sapa kha kiringjoi.

4. Ma mulung khan hoitak ah saw muchum na awm
   Kumulung ah Nakhekpa kiring joi
   'Ma numpok nakha lah le mapak rang kanih
    Patheen Sapa kha kiring joi.
