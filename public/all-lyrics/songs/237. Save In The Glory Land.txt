237. Save In The Glory Land                R.S. 422.
Key A/Flat.

1. Mireetheeng hai ruse na lam hoi khan
    Rengpana lamnakai pejoi
   Ni-inn ravan ram teeng khan nase uh
   Nisun Arshi na eh chak

           Ravan inn hoi teeng nase uh

           Ningai na Lukhum kha rukhu rang

           Ningai rang khamun khan nosok le

           Ningai na ram hoi khan.

2. Kheksa hai lim ruchun na lam hoi khan
    Nang ni deeng Zion zingkar jong kir joi.
    Rengpa chawng pek khan inn teeng nase uh
   Ni meeivar awm chong jawn jai joi.

3. Tak ah sawn net tak ah kese pha khan
    Numpok le rawlawp na sungkhan
    Rengpa numuk le idawwr hoi rang mo?
    'Male ruchun som rang nanih.

4. Rachak Jordon tui kawmchang kha keeneei
    Nichong na-ngir rang nit ah awm
    Rengpa le vara kai ngir rang kani
    Mapak na lah thaw jawn jai rang.
