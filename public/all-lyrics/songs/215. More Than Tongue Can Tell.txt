215. More Than Tongue Can Tell                 S.S.648.

                 St. Jn. 15:13.

Key G.

1. Jisuna num noropok jar ah
    Sawna thingkhan renro reeveei
    Karatha ina khek pekha
    Meeleeina reei ngam mak.

           Ama minupok nahaikha

           Meeleeina reei narang awm mak

            Jisuna num inopok na

           Meeleei na reei ngam mak

2. Saw renro reeveei idoikha
    'Mana riling lukhum rukhu
    Kumsawt ah kiring narang khan
    Meeleeina reei ngam mak.

3. Pumideeng kineei rachaak kha
    Patheen Rengsut nakhan mukrang
   Ama maanrawp thisen ranaak
   Meeleei na reei ngam mak.

4. 'Mana nedep le kiningai
   Ngamchet ah saw kumuchum joi
    Kitum phawm kha malang seng joi
   Meeleei na reei ngam mak.
