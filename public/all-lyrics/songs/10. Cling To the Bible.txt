 10. Cling To the Bible           S.S. 263.

                 Psalm. 119:105.

 Key B/Flat.

 1. Irikip nang neei nawk awm Bible beei roh,
     'Man rawp reetheeng tak hi mathan noroh.
     Chawngtha hina ratha aja mothoi ngam,
    Athi, ringnarang chawng rakham nanih.

            Bible beeirep roh, Bible ditrep ro,

            Bible raak rep ro, Avar lam khani.

2. Bible beei ro manlu det neeibawm nani,
    Kum sawt ringna vakchawi na san ngam ni.
    Mahi manhi dan lakngam tute awm mak
    Nang ratlai tawk ro Ama phor tha kha.

3. Lamlaia chai hai rang awm meeivar khani.
    Rongthar apur hai lamkai sik awmmak.
   Munu ringkho ah awmhai ringna ret nih,
   'Tar hai rang-awm athadet sunsro nih.
