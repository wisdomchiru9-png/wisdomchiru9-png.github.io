283. Never Say Good Bye              S.S. 974.

                2.Cor. 5:11.
Key G/Flat.

1. Oh innhoi ah ruchun hai lim
   Resen awm lai naw nih
   Sokpui mirit ah reebee lim
   Resen na awm lai nawnih.

           Resen lai naw nih (2)

           Ravan chungteeng ah inn hoi khan

           Resen awm lai nawnih.

2. Sawna reeveei hi namak le
   Resen awm lai naw nih
   Reeleei ah chap ah resen hi
   Makhan awm lai naw nih.

3. Khek sahai ningai na munkhan
   Resen awm lai naw nih.
   Kumsawt ringna lah thaw som ah
   Resen awm lai naw nih.

4. A hoi le en awm kawmchang khan
    Resen awm lai nawnih
    Khrista le kumsawt awm som rang
    Resen awm lai nawnih.
