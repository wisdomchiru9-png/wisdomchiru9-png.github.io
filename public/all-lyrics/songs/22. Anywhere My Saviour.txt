22. Anywhere My Saviour    S.S. 596.
Jn. 10:3.
Key B/Flat.

1. Kese na jawt khan Nupumina noroi
Nangkhut le nomoroiro numpok na sung khan
Sawna riling karkhan awm nujui rang kanih
Nakhek pana keeihi nang noroi atu.

Mun rikip ah keei nakhekpa
Nujui rang kani (2)
Nakhekpa na keeihi lam nang nakai pe-ah tu.

2. Nakhekpa le lawnhi ningai na rok kha nih
Nurungro namarat ro kining nit narang
Kesena lam zing a-awm chitna neei nawning
Nakhek pana keeihi nang noroi atu.

3. Nakhekpa nang deeng mun rikip ah kininiik
Lam khawk nakhan noroi ro rehe lai nawning
Seetna khan cho naboi rang nasan jawn jai ro
Pumi noroiro lamdik rawna theei rang.
