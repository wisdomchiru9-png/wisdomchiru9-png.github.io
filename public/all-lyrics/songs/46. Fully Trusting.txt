46. Fully Trusting (S.S. 868)
Ps. 119:42
Key F.

1. Kira khana Jisu kepek
Chawng rakham na kha kejet
Lek se-awm kir ret lai nawning
'Ma chawng tak ah kawsawn rep joi.

Keeina ... tak ah kawsawn rep joi
} 2
'Machawng tak ah kawsawn joi

2. Kumunu Jisu deeng kawbawm
'Ma thi khana nurusuk
'Mana nee ree theeng masak joi.
'Ma Inn na nawchawi rang nih.

3. Chitna kholok Jisu kepek
'Madeeng saw kumuchum joi
Kiringkho lamkha zing ah awm
'Ma avar na na-eh chak.

4. Ningai kholok Jisu kepek
Kidit kholok 'Madeeng awm
Manmeeseh kholok Pumi nih
Kidit lim kha 'Madeeng awm.

5. Kineei kholok Jisu kepek
Karatha katak kholok
Kineeile kimining kholok
Abang boi ranga nijoi.
