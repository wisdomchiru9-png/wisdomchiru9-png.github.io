221. Whosoever Will                S.S. 389.

                   Rev. 22:17

Key D.

1. He hi ajet mirikip na reei uro
     Atha chawng hi vanoi deeng vak sem pero
     Nang irawtawng pui mirikip deeng reei ro
     Adit mirikip hawngro.

              Chawngsem rang adit miring rikip na

              Phai le lawn rikip ah vak reei peroso

              Nokoilai Jisuna numpok he kha le

               Adit mirikip hawngro.

   2. Adit mirikip na ra-nga lai noro
       Tun mawkawt mawlawng lai nang lut natheei rang
       Jisu hi adik le ringna lam khanih
       Adit mirikip hawngro.

    3. Adit rikip na chawng rakhamna muk joi

        Adit mirikipna adoi raat rang nih

        Adit mirikipna ringna muk rang nih

        Adit mirikip hawng ro

                                             S.S. 309.
