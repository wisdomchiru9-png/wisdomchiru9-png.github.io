327. Soldiers Of The King               S.S. 684.
Key G.

1. Rengpa raami keeini
    Thisen le kheksa hai
    Tuntu Khrista raa thaw rang khan
    nang awm naw maw jawjoi
    Thitna theei ah na-awm
    Ningai na lah nawthaw
     Rengpa raami lamkai pana mulung nama ngar pe.

            Rengpa raa miring hai

             Mapak na lah thawrang

             Ama beeirep ah chon thawrawi

             Ajeng ah lur Reng nih.

  2. Rengpa raami keeini
      "Ma riming nawsawng chak
      Voikhat Cross ah ruchi cheen kha
      Khrista riming khani
      Kinem na musung rang
      Sawna tur deer ngam rang
      Raami khat ah Khrista riming
      Richit rajak pek rang.

  3. Rengpa raami keeini
      Ama le awm som rang
      'Male rawtawp ah nawsaw awm
      Napai cheen se khan awm
      Rasang tak ah lar rang
      Apha kha hawng naijoi
      Rengpa raamihai Reng lukhum
      Rukhu narang nikho.
