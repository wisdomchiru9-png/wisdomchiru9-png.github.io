235. When We All Get To Heaven 3rd Alex. 13
Isai. 2:11.
Key C.

1. Jisu numpok na lah thaw ro
Ama numpok mirit kha
Awm narang mum hoi var kha
Keeini rang rukon joi.
Ravan raam nosok phakhan
Ituk ah hoi ningai nikho rang mo?
Jisu numuk le khan
Mawtawr na lah hoi kha thaw rang.

2. Ravan ram lamkhan nalawn le
Reemeei releng rang nijoi
Kachawi na dawng nikho khan
Azing awm lai naw nih.

3. Adik tak ah sawn kha nirawi
Mirikip 'Ma chon thaw rawi
'Ma ningai luma khana
Sawlaw nepek rang nih.

4. Rojoro sawlaw pek rang khan
'Khut le numukui rang nih
Rangkachak lam mawkawt kha
Nawmaw lawng perang nih.
R.S. 466.
