114. Work For The Night Is Coming
John. 9:4.
Key F.

1. Chonthawro janpha jongjoi
Thawro zingkar sehin
Rawdaw cho phakhan thawro
Reeipar par laihin
Chonthawro nivar laiseng
Thawro nilut sekhan
Chonthawro zanpha jongjoi
Mawtawr se rang khan.

2. Chonthawro zanpha jongjoi
Thawro nisun varkhan
Var lailimkhan chon thawro
Bang narang naijoi
Ringna ranak kha pero
Lek te tak awm thawro
Chonthawro zanpha jongjoi
Chonthaw naboi pha.

3. Chonthawro zanpha jongjoi
Nilut malalim se
Avar awm lailim sehin
Chonthaw napha nih
Thawro avar dawngkhit se
Mohot boi ah thawro
Azing zanpha kha jong joi
Chonthaw na theeiboi.
