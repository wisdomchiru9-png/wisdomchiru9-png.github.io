470. None But Christ Alone s.S. 853.
Rom. 5:2.
Key F.

1. Khrista deeng karatha kamaan
'Ma deeng rang ah kamaan
Rachak ningai na kholok kha
Tunse jet mi-ing lai.
Khrista kha niboi ah tu
Ningheen narang awm lak mak
Numpok le ningai ringna kha
Jusu deeng te kamaan.

2. Ningai saw muchum na katawk
Khose awm mumi-ing
Nasaanpa nawtawng pha khan tu
'Ma khut le numu kui.

3. Tui zan nakha kawphawt ah awm
Riting ngam mak Pumi
Mani nek rang tuikha kawdawn
Numpok mirit kumuk.

4. Reeleei hoi narang kajum awm
Nangrang tu chap mi-ing
Numpok mirit kumuk phakhan
Nang numpok kutum joi.
