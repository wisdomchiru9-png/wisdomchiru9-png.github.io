Key E/Flat

1. Pumi nangdeengkhan kawhawngchap
   Nang niboi ah kathi rang keeng
   Kumsawt ringna kha nepero
   Numpok ah noloi ro.

          Seetna neei munuphung

           Keeihi nuruloi ro

           Keeirang khristana nithit pe

           Keeihi nuruloi ro.

2. Seet neei keei nasaan rang awm mak
   Keeirangkhan nang thisen rulong
   Atharra nokon ret tana
   Keeihi nuruloiro

3. Kenem narang kon ngam mi-ing
   Kohoi narang keeina kachawk
   Khanit a-awm nang nasan ret
   Keeihi nuruloiro

4. Nang kekung khan keei natethero
   Nangta kha namachangro
    Nangchon hi kawmawtawr sole
    Keeihi noroloiro.
