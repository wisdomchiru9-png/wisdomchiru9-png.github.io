164. Bless The Lord My Soul
Ps. 34:1.
Key B/Flat.

1. Alur Reng mapak ro
Amate Patheen, relek chon nawthaw pe jarra mapak ro
Ringkho lam ah 'Machawng rakham na kha nih
Nisun na reemeei, zan na meeichaw khanih.
Pumikha mapak ro, nang salang daar le
Vanoi na 'Ma mai en tana mapak ro
Ma ram aleen sungkhan kumsawt hek boiah
Ama chonthaw limna (3) Pumi mapak ro.

2. Nakhek joi mapak ro, Ratha nakhekpa
'Ma thisen na nakhek jarra mapak ro
Ama numpok khutchawi ten ina re-nah
Niniik namasang jarra mapak rang ro.

3. Numpokna chawngsat kha, keeinideeng nepek
Chungteeng ah le naranai masak det joi
Tak ah sawn jarra mining jing lai naw nih.
Numpok awmna munkhan, 'Madeeng ruchun rang.
