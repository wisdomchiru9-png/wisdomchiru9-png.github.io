405. Bethlehem            S.S. 28.

                  Matt. 2:1.

Key G.

1. Oh, asin khopui Bethlehem
    Itik mo numu ning
    Denchim boi ah aja haiseng
    Zaanchim Arshi kha lut joi
    Azing lamkhan vaarpeng la.
    Kumsawt avaar khana
    Kum khatuk itawk ukha
    Vonjaan nangdeeng muk ujoi.

2. Khrista Mary deeng sok joi
    Chungteeng nuruchun joi
    Ajaa haikha 'vaantir na rung
    Numpok relek kha le rung
    Oh, zingkar Arshi sawrpa
    Asok chawngkha reeiro
    Rengpa kha lah le mapak ro
    Reeleei rachaak roso.

3. Idawr hoi ah rachaak moni
    Relek khutchawi nepek
    Numulung sung ah awm jawn jai
    Ravaan na phor thakha
    Manmeeseh munu raam hin jong rang rujet mak meh
    Ratha numpok numuk phakhan
    Khrista namaan rang nih.

4. Ereetheeng Bethlehem ah naai
   Kiniik nepe uro
   Kumunu niliik pe uro,
   Atharra sok urawng
   'Vaantir hai ireei chawnghoi
   Keeini na kejet uh
   Keeini nojong awm pui chak ro
   Pumi Immanual.
