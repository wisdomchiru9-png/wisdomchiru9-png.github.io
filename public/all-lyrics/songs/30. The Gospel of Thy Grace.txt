30. The Gospel of Thy Grace              S.S. 490.

                   Jn. 3:16.

Key G/Flat.

1. Numpok chawngtha khana, karatha ma-ngar joi
    Patheen numpok jarra Asapa nepek joi

            Tu nit a-awm Amakha tak ah sawn lim na

            Kum sawt ring na muk rang (2)

2. Moses imakhai moro ru-en lim ring uh
    Thing Cross ah khai Jisu en lim ringna muk rang.

    3. Munuhi thit na nih, munu neei kha kani
    Kumsawt ah kuruthang, nit'awm hitin neereei.

4. Miring hai sawna kha raha pui jong nimak
    'Ma riming ah ring na, reeleei ah muk rang nih.

5. Kupumi nasaan ro, lungdam na nepero
    Nangchawngtha kha keeina tak ah sawn rang rawng.
