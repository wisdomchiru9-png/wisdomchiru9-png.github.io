436. Up Calvary Mountain.

1. Calvary lawn na Nupumina
   En-ngam thoiboi ah sawna rudoi
   Munu phung hairang Cross ah ruthi
   Athang keeihi' Mana nuru khek.

           Num napo pumi, maanrawp nasanpa

           Calvary thing Cross chungah ruthat uh

           Munuphung renro reeveei keeirang

           Sawdoi tak ah nuruthit pe joi.

2. Calvary Cross kha en-ngam mi-ing
   Thisen jonglong seei sebe ah put
   Munuphung keeirang sawna rudoi
   'Ma numpok nakha relek tak nih.

3. Thisen khana keeihi nawraw chawk
   Azing manmeeseh hi mavaar joi
   Pumi numpokna kha kamaan na              mo
    Ma numpok jarra "Ringna Kumuk"
