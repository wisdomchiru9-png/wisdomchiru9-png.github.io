 12. All This For Me           S.S. 613.

                  John 19:17

 Key G/Flat

 1. Anakhekpa nangvar khana
     Kiringkho nang namavar peh
     Idawr sindawng awm nang numpok?
    Keeirang ah ni akholok ah.

            Oh, Kupumi anakhekpa

            Nangdeeng ah repe kha nirawng

            Tun naput ah mawtawr sekhan

            Nang rang ah ni a - kholok ah.

2. Rilip khale nang nurung chak
    I-sawn boi ah na pai ah awm
    Gathsamani ah nang sawna
    Keeirang ah ni ah kholok ah.

3. Nanglu ah thisen reetheeng na
    Tuntu keeihi nang nasan joi
    Singdawngroi Calvary Cross kha
    Keei rang ah ni a - kholok ah.

4. Numpok le rachak rawchawng na
    Nang neeibawm na chungteeng ah inn
   Numpok ah nang inakhek nah
   Keeirang ah ni a - kholok ah.
