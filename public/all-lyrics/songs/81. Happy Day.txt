81. Happy Day           H.P. 105.

1. Ningai khana keeihi nawrawtawp pui chak
   Kopok arik kha namajang masak peh
   Kawlawn na lamkha namavar masak peh
   Jisu na noroi jarra.

           Jisuna keeihi nakhek ah

           Kining nama ngai masak peh

           Karatha awm ningai masak

           Namapak masak, Pumi riming ah

           Keei kacham ah ka-awm

           Keeina kupumi chon thaw pe rang kanih

           Amale resen naw ning

           Kupumi chawngpek khan keeina kiningai.

2. Jisu na ningai hi keeideeng nepek joi
   Karatha awm saw nu muchum masak peh
    Kupumi chawng rachak tak ah kawsawn na
   Amana nasan rang nih.

3. Miteei tang hai kha awm ningai masak ro
    Karatha awm saw numuchum masak peh
    Amani asaw ara awm naboi rang
    Amani le rawtawp ro.
