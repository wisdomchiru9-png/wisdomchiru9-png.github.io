15. Come To The Saviour S.S. 1165.
Ps. 16:2
Key B/Flat.

1. Hawngro Nakhekpa deeng sum noro
Ama chawngna lamkha numumuk
Von hin nakar ah nading puisom
Renem ah reei "hawngro"
Ningai Ningai heei ah rumurang
Seet na mulung hi reetheeng so le
Nakhekpa khale ruchun som rang
Kumsawt inn ahoi khan.

2. Ra-ngai ro sawna doi naite hai
Mulung ningai rasang masak ro
Nupumi kha nata machang rawi
Hawngro sumlai noro.

3. Vonhin Pumi keeini le awmchak
Ama chanwgpek hoikha ra-ngai ro
He renem le nokoi ra-ngai ro
Hawngro ti-ah nokoi.
