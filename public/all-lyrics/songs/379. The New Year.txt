379. The New Year.
Key C/Flat.

1. Kumului se ah nile tha kum rotho joi
Von zing kumthar nikho niret joi
Nangja na-ah put hawngro ningai lam ah
Ningai ah mapak rawi niso-engseei ah.
Hawngro, hawngro, Npumi mapak rang
Vonhin kumthar nikho niret joi
Kum mutum se dam veng ah Nasaanpa nih
Kumsawt ah Ma riming aleen niroso.

2. Ningai chawng kha hawngchawi tana hawng uro
Patheen jongjoi raavan asem rang
Patheen na nangtha ti-ah noloi narang
Mulung lek tana hawng rang ro pumi deeng.

3. Kumlui mathan tana kumthar mapak ro
Vanoi na numulung lek tai ah
Nuk ah Patheen raavantha numuk narang
Ningai ah mapak urawi Halleluiya.

4. Ringkho dawngse ah nawlawm dit pa khani
Keeinina nithaw na rikip ah
Natna, saatna put namadam theeipa nih
Ningai ah lawm urawi "kumthar nikho hi "
