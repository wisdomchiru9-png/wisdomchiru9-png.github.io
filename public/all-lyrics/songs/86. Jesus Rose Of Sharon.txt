86 Jesus Rose of Sharon C.S.L. 80.
Key E/Flat.

1. Sharon reeipar Jisu mulung sung ah par
Adik ah reetheeng det atha chungchon nih
Patheen numpok na singna theem na kha nih
Munu rikip ah keeideeng hin nemezet chak.
Jisu Sharon reeipar.
Rumum chim ah kumulung sung ah par joi

2. Sharon reeipar Jisu en jawt awm hai naw
Atha reeipar rikip nek khan awm tha det
Ningthing kip keeihi num nang nopok rep joi
Ringkho nawmawtawr malim nawrawtawp pui chak ro.

3. Sharon reeipar Jisu choiboi pet ah par
Nang jeng na ranak mun rikip sip roso
Asaw meeisa doi ratha kholok chung ah
Nang te 'ni mulung sung ah ningai na ape.
