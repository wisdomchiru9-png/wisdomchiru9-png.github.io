106. My Saviour Leads             S.S. 544.

                  Ps. 129:24.

Key G.

1. Nakhekpa na noroi lailim
    Karavat na lim nepek veek
    'Ma numpok mirit natet na
    Ama anu juichak rang.

           Nakhekpa sena juichak rang (3)

            Ama anu juichak rang.

2. Kawlawn na lamhi matat awm
    Kachawi narang kasaw ah awm
    'Ma numpok hena nawmaw awi
    Ama anu juichak rang.

3. Sawna hina namanat awm
    Ringkho rithi na nanor ah-awm
    'Ma numpok khutna nakaw chak
   Ama anu juichak rang.

4. Reeleei ningaina hek sole
   Ama inn khan keei nokoi le
    Numpok na tui vara teengkhan
   Ama anu juichak rang.
