   390. Oh, Wait Meekly Tune               S.S. 710.
   Key C.

   1. Uleen nai narachang hek joi
      Resen narang pha hawng sok joi
     Manmeeseh sawna ram sung hin
     Resen ret rang nani joi.

            Mining chak ro khrista numpok na (2)

            Kanamathan uleen nai

            Narang chang hek joi.

 2. Sokpui hai mining chak uroh
    Manmeeseh ringkho hawng hek teeng
    Nurumu boi ret rang awm jawt
    Ravan ram nosok malalim.

3. Manmeeseh neeihi ii-nimak
   Nikhat ah hoi narang rok nih
   Hi-ang hin niring kho hi-awm
   Ningkip ahek theei rok nih

4. Sokpuihai nitheei naboi awm
  Khat le khat ra-ngaidam urawi
  Nupumi numpok na jarra
  Ningai ah resen urang rawi.
