2. Keeirang kapa awm, 'Mana ra-ngam nakhat
Nepek, e-he hoi le raavan adik kha
Relen na ravan na nurumuk pui rang nih.,
Oh, nangle keeile rawtawp som mini maw?

3. Reng reeleen na rui achek kha keeneei joi
Kachawi nakhan minit tak ah na-ngak lai
Makha avaar i-na eh kha keeneei pha khan
Rodit-tum neei miring ah numu ning maw?

4. Rachak keeneei, makha tui ang ah rachak
Manmeese kawlawm haina jet mak lai
Put nale apepa tu nakhekpanih,
Ti-ah mo jet ning nang deeng nepek joi tihi?

5. Jisuna nuru muk pui phakhan
Keei le nang nasaan pakha khat te inih.
Amana keeini teeng nawchawi rang nih
Niik na namasang rang ni, namasang joi.
