 497. I Have Found A Rare Friend                       D.P.30.

                 Lk. 23:42 & 43.

 Key G.

             Keeirang tu i-te awm neei lak mi-ing

              Nit awm lawmdit tak Jisu keeneei joi

             Ravaat iti neeilai mi-ing

             Kining heen veng joi, Oh, kining heen veng joi

             Von kawrawtawng puijoi, muk ngamboi lawmpa kha

1. Adawk hai ranaak ni, a-sin hai leen nakha nih
    Sara hai hoina kha nih
    Mulung zing awm lai mak, mulung ah numpok vaar joi
    Numpok avaar lawmpa keeneei joi.

2. Ana hai damna nih, athi hai ringna kha nih
    Munu neei Nakhekpa nih
    Achap hai bang sojoi, asaw hai sawmuchum na
    Kumsawt ringna lawmpa keeneei joi.

3. Mulung lek hai ring na, Jisu kha ruresen pui
    Iscariot ang naboi rang
    Tumo khrista kha le, ringna rang lawmpa thaw rang?
    Hektik neeiboi adik lawmpa nih.

4. Nakhekpa Jisu le, murusa pasa di na
    Cross ling ah khai som pha khan
    Jisu 'zet pakhana, lawmpa dik tak kha muk joi.
    Azetboi pakha le resen joi.
