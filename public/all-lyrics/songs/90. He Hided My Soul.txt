90. He Hideth My Soul S.S. 540.
Isaiah 32:2.
Key D.

1. Sindawng roi Nakhekpa Jisu Kupumi
Sindawngroi tak ah keei nakhek
'Mana karatha ne-thepe lungpum khan
Khan ningai na tui hawr kumuk.

Mana karatha ne-thepe lungpum khan
Makhan tui ravat awm naw ni
Ma numpok sung khan numumuk joi
Ma khut na keei numukui joi. (2)

2. Sindawngroi Nakhekpa Jisu Kupumi
Kopokrik nawchawi pe sojoi
'Mana keeihi narak joi sok lai nawning
Ama ranak kha nepek joi.

3. Te ngam boi man rawp lukhum numukhu joi
Ama dik na that na kha sip
Patheen deeng ningai lurna lah kha kawthaw
Nakhekpa kha kata nijoi.

4. 'Ma avar ponchek khan kawhawngthoi pha khan
Reemeei karra Ama muk rang
Ama inakhek na relek numpok kha
Rasang chungchon na mapak rang.
