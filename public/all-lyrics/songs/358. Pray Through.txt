(358. Pray Through            3rd. Slex. 39.

                1. Thes. 5:17.
Key E/Flat

1. Patheen deeng ah ni bang noro
   Bang noro nang he zet rang nih
   Adik Patheen na chawng rakham
   Bang noro namasang rang nih.

2. Nang idit lim kha niichak ro
   Nichak ro Pumina zet veek
   Ama deeng ah nihi leen det
   Niro, 'Mana nepek rang nih.

3. Munu kar khan awm niichak ro
   Bang noro nang mawtawr rang nih
   Khristana adik chawng rakham
   Niichak ro numukui rang ni.

4. Sawna nangdoi awm niichak ro
   Bang noro nang hawng dam rang nih
   Nang sawna Pumina muk veek
   Niichak ro nimidik rang nih.

5. Ringrep tana niichak rang ro
   Bang noro keeini dik ngir rang
   Tak ah sawn na mawtawr veek rang
   Patheen na niniik namasaang.
