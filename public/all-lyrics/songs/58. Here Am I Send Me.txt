58. Here Am I Send Me             S.S. 813.

                  Isaish. 6:8.

Key F.

1. Jisu he rasang ra-ngai ro
   Von tumo chonthaw serang
   Loi ah sang min na na-ngak joi
   Tu namo vak rawkhawm ni
   Pumina nilaw pek rang khan
   He rasang ah nokoi joi
   Tumo ningai ah masang rang
   Keeihi nitir ro ti-ah
   Hin ka-awm keei nitir ro.

2. Tuipui rakan ngam nawk a-awm
   Richi chawiboi raam mun khan
    Makha miring haikha dep ah
   Nangna nangsan ngam rangnih.
   Lishing nangpek ngam nawk a-awm
   Nang ineeidak kha pero
   Jisurang nang ithaw limkha
   'Marang manrawp tak nijoi (2)

3. Vantir ang ah nangchawng nawk awm
   Poul ang ah nang chawng nawk awm
   Jisu numpok kha nang reel ngam
   Vanoi rang 'Ma thitna chawng
   Adawk nang mothoi ngam nawk awm
   Chawngsatpa ranak jarra
   Naipang khaikha nang moroi ngam
   Nasaanpa deengkung sekhan (2)

4. Mi rungkha nangchang  ngam nawk awm
   Zion lawnkhan ding tana
    Ravan ram lamkha khin pero
    Ringkho repe rachak chawng
    Niik nale numpok ranak ah
    Patheen idit nangthaw ngam
    Aron tak ah sawn kha ang ah
    Theempuleen khut lawm puiro (2)

5. Uleenchang miring haideeng khan
    Chawng nangsem ngam nawk se awm
    Ke-ngam roi pa Khristana reei
    Ke-ngam haikha vairo ti
    Ranak neeiboi naipang haikha
   Chit tak ah lam nang kaipe
   Kha law kha nang muk rang nijoi
    Ram hoimun nangsok phakhan (2)

6. Nemna boi chawng ra-ngai noro
   Kithaw ngam awm mak ti-ah
   Miring ratha ru-ngui pha khan
   Pumina nokoi rang nih
   'Ma chonkha ningai ah thaw ro
   Khale nang ning ma-ngai ro
    Masangro 'Mana nokoi pha
    Hin ka-awm keei nitir ro (2)
