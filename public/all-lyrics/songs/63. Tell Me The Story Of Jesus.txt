63. Tell Me The Story Of Jesus R.S. 456.
Key E/Flat.

1. Jisu rimingkha neereei ro, kumulung ah awm ro so.

Manrawp risim kha neereei ro,
Rujetboi ahoi det kha, 'Ma sokna Vantir hai ithaw
Lam jawng lahkha neereei ro,
Patheen rasang ningai khana, Reeleei rachak roso.
Jisu risim kha neereei ro
Kumulung ah awm roso
Kiru jetboi pet ahoidet
Manrawp risim neereei ro.

2. 'Mana thimbeeng ah vawncham doi.
Iruthaw haikha reeiro,
Numunu jarra 'ma rulem,' nit awm' Mana rumawtawr
'Ma iruthaw chonkha reeiro, sawna irudoi haikha
Inn le loiboi sara khat ah, Engseet ah ruthaw uh.

3. Cross ah hemna mun neereei ro, Ajeng sawna natnakha
'Ma khumna thankha neereeiro,
Hawngring naret kha reeiro, kimuk nek ah thadetrisim
Keeina makha numpok rang,
Herenem le keeina chap rang, numpok khana nakhek joi.
