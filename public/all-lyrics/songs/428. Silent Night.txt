428. Silent Night        M.H. 495.
Key E/Flalt.

1. Arachak, zanchim ah, Ravan teeng jong var joi
   Chubai ibawk uh naikha, Ama chung ah jongvar joi
   Lungboiroi en-awm ah, Rachak reei ah maja.

2. Ereetheeng, arachak, zanchim ah, avaar khan
   Ravantir hai na 'awm lah thaw,
    Halleluiya Rengpa deeng, Nasanpa Nupumi
    Vonjan hin jongsok joi.

3. Arachak, ereetheeng, zanchim ah, avaarkhan
    Nisok nateeng mitheem hai,
    Reng deeng chubai vak bawk uh,
    Nasaanpa sok joi, ti chawng hi zet ule.

 4. Arachak, ereetheeng, Arshi kha jongvaar joi
    Ningaina lah thaw som uh,
    Mapak urawi Rengpa kha, Khrista sok nikho hin
     Rachak ah mapak rawi.
