103. What A Wonderful Saviour                 S.S. 119.

                  Isaiah 9:6.

Key D.

1. Khristana munu nakhek pe
   Sindawngroi Nakhekpa nih
   'Man nepek pe-ah nakhek joi
   Sindawngroi Nakhekpa nih.

Sindawngroi Nakhekpa kha
Jisu Khrista khani
Sindawngroi Nakhek pakha
Pumi Khrista nih.

2. 'Ma thina nurusuk jarra
Pumi keeina kamapak
Karatha hi Patheen kha le
Nawhawng ree ngeei masak joi

3. Kumunu nurusuk pe joi
Sindawngroi Nakhekpa nih
Amana Rengchang ah awm joi
Sin dawngroi Nakhekpa nih.

4. Mawtawr narang ningai na-pe
Sindawngroi Nakhekpa nih
Ningkip tak ah nawmawsawn joi
Sindawngroi Nakhek panih.

5. Mawtawr narang ningai nape
Sindawngroi Nakhekpa nih
Lemna kholok kawmawtawr joi
Sindawngroi Nakhekpa nih.

6. 'Madeeng mulung kepek so joi
Sindawngroi Nakhekpa nih
Reeleei ningai dit lai nawning
Sindawngroi Nakhekpa nih.
