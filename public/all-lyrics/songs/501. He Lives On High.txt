501. He Lives On High               M.H. 42.

                 Acts. 1:11.

Key B/Flat.

1. Ahoi tak raam Ravan kha maathan nah
    Reeleei chunghin Jisu kha jongjoi
    'Mana riling lukhum jong rukhu ah
    Munu phung saara kajarra jongthi.

            Hawngthoi ah Ravanteeng kajoi

            Athi raakha mawtawr ah hawngring hetjoi

            Hawngthoi ah Ravanteeng ka joi

            Keei naloi jongret rang nih.

2. Thaan sung ah put khan Jisu hawngthoi joi
    Patheen deengkung sok rangkhan hawng joi
    Jisu na voikhat jongkir ret rang nih
      Ma chawng ajui haiseng kha chawi rang nih.

 3. Mulungsaw hai Jisu deengkhan hawngro
     Maanboi ah ringna nepek rang nih
     Jisu tak ah sawn miring rikip kha
     Kumsawt saw muchunna muk rang nijoi.

 4. Jisudeeng hawngro pokrik phung rikip
     Keeideeng hawngro ti-ah nokoi joi
     Nangpok rik kha hawng muchum rang tana
     Kumsawt saw muchumna nepek rang ti.

                                                  S.S. 158.
