462. Draw Me Nearer            S.S. 607.

                Heb. 10:22.

Key A/Flat.

1. Pumi nangta kani, nang he kejet
   Nang numpok na nemezet
   Tak ah sawn khut khan keei kathoi jawt joi
   Nangdeeng khan natet chak ro.

           Numpok Pumi deeng khan natet ro

           Nang ruthit na Cross kungkhan

           Numpok Pumi deengkhan natet ro

           Nang thisen long na munkhan.

2. Nangchon thaw rang neereetheeng masak ro
   Reetheeng numpok ranaak ah
   Karatha na ne-en chak rang roso
   Kacham neeina thang roso.

3. Pha bawngte nit' awm, reetheeng ningai ah
    Rengmun khan kaw-awm mala
   Rukhuk sun na nangdeeng ki niik phakhan
   Nanglawm dit ah kajir joi.

4. Kizet ngam boi numpok athuk kha awm
    Tuipui karakan malim
    Kiphak ngamboi, rasaang ningai kha awm
    Nangle saw'muchum malim.
