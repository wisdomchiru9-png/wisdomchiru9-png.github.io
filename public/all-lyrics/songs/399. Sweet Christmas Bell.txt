 399. Sweet Christmas Bell            C.P.37.

 1. Christmas pha daar mirikha na
    Ningai chawmgkha malar
    Daar miri hoi na numulung
    Numuchun masaak peh.

           Sp: Christmas daar miri hoi

           Parts: Daarkha

           Sp : Christmas daar ahoi kha

           Parts: Miri

           Sp    Ravaan lamteeng numulung ning nama ngai

                   peh.

           Ten Ravaan lam teengkhan         Christmas daar

                                           miri hoi

           Bass: Lam teengkhan              Khana numulung

                 Ravaan lam teeng numulung ningna ma ngai

                  peh.

           Ten    Ravaan lamteengkha Christmas daar miri hoi

                   khana

           Bass: Lamteengkhan numulung ning nama ngai pe

2. Christmas chawng hoikha rehem joi
    Vanoi ningai rang rawi
    Bethlehem seh inn ah Jisu
   Jongsok ah nojong khek.

3. Christmas daar miri rehemah
   Jisu riming rophong
   Lah hina numulung mapar
   Ningai daar mini joi.
