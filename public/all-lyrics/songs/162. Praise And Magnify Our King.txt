 162. Praise And Magnify Our King                 S.S. 229.

                   Ps. 34.3.

 Key C.

 1. Ram rikip apo aleen Pumina
    Thoiro, thoiro, lah kha thawro
    Ama kekung ah chubai bawk tai ah
    Rengkha rasang ah lawm rawi.

           Chungteeng ah kheksa hai

           Numpok lah le awi ro,

           Phortha kha lawm ro

            Ma ithaw na-ngamkha rasang ah lawm rawi

           Ama jengna reeiro

           Rengkha rasang ah lawm rawi.

2. Aleen Pumi i-reeisa athaw ngam
   Thoiro, thoiro, lah kha thaw ro
   Ranak neei haikha 'Mana mawtawr joi
   Rengkha rasang ah lawm ro.

3. Aleen Pumi reetheeng ningai le jong
   Thoiro, thoiro, lah kha thaw ro
   Reeleei ah phung rikip ningai rang ro
   Rengkha rasang ah lawm rawi.

4. Aleen Pumi reetheeng 'Ma riming nih,
   Thoiro, thoiro, lah kha thaw ro
   'Machon relen 'Vantir haina rophong
   Rengkha rasang ah lawm rawi.
