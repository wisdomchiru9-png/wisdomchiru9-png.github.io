477. I Do Believe S.S. 493.

Key G/Flat.

1. Kapa nangdeeng khut kawhawng sawn
Nasaan rang awm lak mak
Keeihi nangna mathan nala
Khose teeng mo se ning?

Jisuna nithit pe

3. Oh, Jisu tak ah sawn ning maw?

Karavaat na-awm sip sojoi
Tun pha hoi sung tak hin

4. Adik Pumi deeng kene-en
Asaw kimik hina
