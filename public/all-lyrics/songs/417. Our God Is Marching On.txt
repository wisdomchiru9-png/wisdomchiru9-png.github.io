417. Our God Is Marching On                  S.S. 697.

                  Ps. 24:3.

Key C.

1. Lur tak ah Pumi ajongkha kimik na kumuk het joi
    Mulung hang ah Drakha bawr haikha Ma kele chong najoi
    Kho melek ang ah var chemjam kha Pumina rudek joi
    Nupumi kha jongjoi.

            Lurchak roso halleluiya (3)

            Nupumi kha jongjoi.

 2. Mi raja raja mirawp karra Pumikha kumuk joi
     Manina nitak rawdaw karra pek mun khat kon ujoi
     Nupumi chawng adik tak kha var khan jong malar joi
     Nupumi kha jongjoi.

 3. Mikir boidet ranga Pumi pumtak na sumlawi tum joi
     Pumi chawngsat na munkhan mirikip mulung melek joi
     Oh, karatha nang romasang na-ngam rang relen rangro
     Nupumi kha jongjoi.

 4. Tui vara teengkhan tuili reeipar karra Jisu sok joi
      Pumi lurna var kha ang ah nangle keei meh relek rang
      Jisu jongthi kha ang ah thi tai ah miringhai san rawi
      Nupumi kha jongjoi.
