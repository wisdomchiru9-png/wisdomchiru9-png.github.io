216. God Boundless Might
     (Negro Spiritual) Hymn No. 165.
Key C.

1. Ranaak neeichung chon avaar Reng Pumi
   Nang Rengmun kha keeinina
   Chubai nabawk rang kani
   Nang riming ah mapak na lah thaw rang
   Relek nang ranaak kholok(2)

          Chi heei ah nang riming kha lawm ah

           Aring jawn jai athaw ngam Patheen

          Nang misaan na numpok relek kha

           Rophong rang khan mapak rang kanih

           sun zan na, reeleei ah, tui ah, chungle thoi ah

           Nangkha namapak chak rang kani

           Ranaak neei chungchon avaar neei Reng

           Pumi nangni, Amen (7)

2. Chatboi pet ah namapak chak urawng
   Phung rikip na ruchun na
   Nang kekung khan chubai  bawk
   Jehovah Reng ti-ah namapak uh
    Nangkha ratha reetheeng haina lah le namapak uh.

3. Ravan chung ah awm, ranaak neei chungchon
    Irikip aa-kon Pumi, nangkha kanamapak uh
    Mirikip ning minitpa Reng nang nih
    Patheen Sapa adik tak reetheeng
    chungchon kha nang nih.
