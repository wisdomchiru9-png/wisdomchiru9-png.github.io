55. Come Great Deliver Come
Ps. 11:17.
Key G/Flat.
S.S. 462.

1. Nang numpok ah kehe nurujet pero
Jongro Nakhekpa
Karatha asaw hina nangkha na-awi
Jongro Nakhekpa.

Inn na put ah raamla lawn rasang ah,
Saw renro reeveei ah kachai
Nang awm na munhoi khan keeini nawchawi ro
Jongro Nakhekpa.

2. Zanna tung narang awm, mun runeei mi-ing
Jongro Nakhekpa
Ring nale var muk narang kawnawhawng en
Jongro Nakhekpa.

3. Lam rebe ah kawlawn nakeke saw joi
Jongro Nakhekpa
Nang numpok maipar kha kimik-na ke-en.
Jongro Nakhekpa.

4. Mulung mining koi ah namalai noro
Jongro Nakhekpa
Kininiik na he hi nuru jet pero
Jongro Nakhekpa.
