246. On Jordon's Stormy Bank
Isaiah 33:12.
Key G.

1. Jordon Kawmchang ah kading ah
La tak ah ke-en le
Ki-itum rang ah cannan ram
Ahoi keeina kumuk.
Sawmuchum rang nani ahoi ram khan
Tui vara kha kara kan so le
Ningthingkip Moses lah hoi khale mapak ah
Ningai ah Jisu le awm rang.

2. Khadawr ajai ram hoi sung khan
Azing iti awm mak
Patheen le Sapa Reng chang na
Munkha var jawn jai joi.

3. Khamun hoikha kosok le khan
Raavantha kha tum rang
Kapa meh kha kumuk pha khan
'Madeeng saw muchum rang.

4. Karatha ningaina sip rang
Asaw awm lai naw nih
Jordon tuipui kha leen na awm
Chiboi ah rakan rang.
