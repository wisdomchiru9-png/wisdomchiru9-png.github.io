126. The Grand Old Storry Of Salvation
R.S. 130.
Key B/Flat.

1. Chungteeng innhoi lam naraw lai hin, keeini deeng neereei
Relek inakhekna risim lui
Ningai tak ah nehe hoile, numpok na lah nawthaw
Relek inakhek na risim lui.
Daar suro, rophong ro
Vanoi phungtin deeng mezet ro
Vaksaan ro muntin na
Relek inakhek na risim lui.

2. Apur haikha' Ma khut le mothoi, thisen le rusuk
Relek inakhek na risim lui
'Ma numpok na'var khana, azingkha jong mavar joi
Relek inakhek na risim lui.

3. Ramunkhan' Ma risimkha lah a, thaw-ah mawtawr rang
Relek inakhek na risim lui
Azing rilip hi seso malim, lah thawrang nanih
Relek inakhek na risim lui.

4. Lah kha thaw u a-awm Ravantir haina sindawng a-en.
Relek inakhek na risim lui
Amana ' inakhek na pon alur, khutchawi manrawp neei
Relek inakhek na risim lui.
