430. Christ Die For Sinners

1. Kumunu reetheeng narang khan
Nupumina saw renro reeveei nodoi pe
Keei kawsaw ruhut Pumina rudoi
Calvary Cross khan sawtak ah khai joi.
Kithit naarang khan Jisuna thijoi
Kumunu phung kholok kha chawi so joi
Raai rekhek tawng hai Calvary thina
Damna muk so ah ningai na maan joi.

2. Gethsamani kha 'Padeeng ranaak nik
Munuphung hairang ahu thi-ah sok
Reeleei seetna kholok kha phung lam ah
Mi-engseet awm doi Calvary Cross khan.

3. Asaan rang'boi Jisuna theen te-ah
Theempuleen pa maikung ah vaak dingjoi
Khristana sawna idoi kha muk le
Nupanghai na Jisu kha chap pui uh.

4. Khrista thit nikhan ni le the awm 'zing
Reeleei rawnawk ah thaan awm rawlawng joi
Vanoi rang ah thi Nupumi Khrista
Hawngring ret rang ni, "Nithum chang nakhan"
