 429. Who Is He           S.S. 66.

                  Heb. 2:9.

 1. Tumoni seh inn sung ah
    Ke-ngam roi chubai bawk kha?

           Pumini relek risim keeng

           Ningaina Reng Pumi khani

           Chit tak ah chubai bawk urawi

           Reng lukhum kha mukhu rawi.

 2. Tumoni saw doi tak ah
    Rampa ah vawncham rudoi?

3. Tumoni renem le
    Raavan nape ngam pakha?

4. Ana asaw hai kholok
    Tudeeng khan mo ruchawi uh?

5. Lazarus jana than na
    Achap pakha tumoni?

6. Tumoni mawtawr lah le
    Mipui hai lam rujawng kha?

7. Tumoni janchim rip ah
   Gethsamani ah ani kha?

8. Tumoni thing Cross ler ah
   Saw reeveei ah jongthi kha?

9. Thaan naput ah hawngthoi ah
   Nakhekpa kha tumo ni?

10. Ravan reeleei kholok ah
   Rengmun lokha tumo ni?
