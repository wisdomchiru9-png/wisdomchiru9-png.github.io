296. What A Gathering

                Gen. 41:10.

Key C. Mode rate.

1. Rangkachak ang ah var zingkarra Misapa jong phakhan
    'Ma lurna ningai maiso numuk rang nih
    Phung rikip Ama miring haikha Inn na koi rang nijoi
     Kheksa hai ruchun kha idawr hoi rang mo?

             Kheksa hai ruchun rang nanih

             Numpok na ramhoi mun khan

             Keeini ruchun na awm rang nih

              Kheksa hai ruchun rang nijoi,

              Chung khopui ningai na ahoi inn sung khan.

   2. Jisu riming ah ruthi ravan naihai hawngthoi rangnih
       Tuipui le athimbeeng thankhur ah put khan

        Reetheeng taksa kha le pumi kha rumuk pui rang nijoi.

    3. Makha rumuk nakha idawr hoi rang mo?

        Khopui sung ah inn hoi en-awm avar hawrtui along kha,

        Keeini na nimik tak na numuk pha khan

        Niresen pui lawmdit hai le ningai ah ruchun rangni

         Makha ruchun nakha idawr hoi rang mo?

    4. Oh, Nerengpa jongchum na ngir rang phakha jong naijoi.

         Chawng rakham na nikho hoi numuk pha khan

         Mik makhap pha ah miring kholok ratha ah jir rang nih.

         Khan'deeng kung ah awmchak rang nani joi.
