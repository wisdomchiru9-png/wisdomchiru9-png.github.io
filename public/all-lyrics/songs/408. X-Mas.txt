408. X-Mas.

1. Zanchim ah ke-ngam roihai
Bethlehem raam ah reek hai kha
Ravaan na he na jong hem
Ra-ngai rangkhan thoi uh.

           Ningaina rithi hawngrang ahi

           Ravaantir haina jongreei

           Vanoina mapak rang nanih

           Khrista sokna chawng reei ah

          Ningai lah thaw som rawi.

2. Ravaan nai vaak en uro
   Bethlehem seh inn sung ah khan
   Sara nu Mary khana
   Raanring kheng ah maja.

           Chung arshi na hawngvaar engseei

           Naitepa jana munkhan

          Nisok teeng mihai na hawngjui

          Neei alu kha hawngchawi ah

          Reng chunbai hawng bawk uh.

3. Mahi ni Ravaan na chawng
   Phung rikip hai saan narang khan
   Khut le ke-ah vit u-ah
   Edep ah sun rang nih.

          Ringna thikha jonglong rang nih

          Calvary thing Cross chung ah

          Makhan   ring na numuk rang ni

          Ravaan tir ireei ang ah

          Phung rikip dam narang.
