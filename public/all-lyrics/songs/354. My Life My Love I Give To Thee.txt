354. My Life My Love I Give To Thee
C.W.H. 298. A.A.C.H. 378.
Key F.

1. Kiring kho le kunumpok hi
Kupumi deeng kepek sojoi
Keeiranga nathit pepa nih
Keei nakhek pa Patheen.
Kupumi Chon thaw rang kani
Hituk hin na a-hoi ringkho
Keeirang nang nuruthit pejoi
Keei nakhekpa Pumi.

2. Keeirang hin nuruthit pe ah
'Thina nawrawchawk karing joi.
Von na put tak ah kawsawn joi.
Keei nakhekpa Patheen.

3. Karatha hi nakhek narang
Calvary ah nithit pe joi
Karatha hi kepek so joi
keei nakhekpa Patheen.
