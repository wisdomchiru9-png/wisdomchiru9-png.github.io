47. Rejoice And Be Glad (S.S. 224.)
Ps. 158.
Key G/Flat.

1. Ningairo..... hoiro
Nasaanpa kha jongjoi
Enro Ama Cross, zana khan Ama than.

Ithaat pa u risim kha reei tana mapak ro
Hawng ring ret ningai risim kha reeitana lawmro.

2. Ningairo, hoiro, tuntu ni hawg varjoi
Reemeei releng sojoi, rilip awm lai mak.

3. Ningairo hoiro, thisen kha jonglong joi
Amana maan kha te-ah nawrawchawk joi.

4. Ningairo hoiro, ra-ngaidam nepek joi
Adikboi midik rang Cross ah ruthi joi.

5. Ningairo hoiro ithat u ke-ngam khan
Athi kha mawtawr ah hawngring thep ret joi

6. Ningairo hoiro, Nerengpa chungteeng awm
Ravan na keeini rang roi nurureei peh.

7. Ningairo hoiro, jongkir ret rang ah nih
Ithaat u ke-ngam 'Ma jengna le jong.
