Install from the website (recommended)

This keeps the app updated automatically and works offline after the first load.

  1. Open this site in Chrome or Edge on Android.
  2. Tap the menu (three dots) and choose "Install app" or "Add to Home screen".
  3. Open the new app icon on your home screen.

Allow installs from unknown sources

  1. Open Settings and go to Security or Apps.
  2. Open Install unknown apps and enable your browser.
  3. Open the APK from Downloads and confirm install.

Offline use

Open the app once while online so it can save songs. After that, it will work offline.
