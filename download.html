Allow installs when Android asks

  1. Open Settings and go to Security or Apps.
  2. Open Install unknown apps and allow your browser or files app.
  3. Open the APK from Downloads and confirm install.

Install on iPhone or iPad

iPhone and iPad do not install APK files. Use Safari and add the songbook to your Home Screen instead.

  1. Open this site in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button.
  3. Choose Add to Home Screen.
  4. Open the new icon from your Home Screen like a normal app.

Install from the website

This keeps the app updated automatically and works offline after the first load.

  1. On Android, open this site in Chrome or Edge.
  2. Tap the menu and choose "Install app" or "Add to Home screen".
  3. On iPhone or iPad, use Safari and choose "Add to Home Screen" from Share.
  4. Open the new app icon on your home screen.
